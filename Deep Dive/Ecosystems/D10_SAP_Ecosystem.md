## Ecosystem Overview

SAP is a global leader in enterprise resource planning (ERP) and cloud-based business applications. The SAP ecosystem spans:

- **Core ERP**: SAP S/4HANA (on-premise, private cloud, public cloud)
- **Cloud Platforms**: SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP)
- **Human Capital Management**: SAP SuccessFactors
- **Spend Management**: SAP Ariba, SAP Fieldglass, SAP Concur
- **Commerce & CX**: SAP Commerce Cloud (formerly Hybris), SAP Customer Data Platform
- **Databases**: SAP HANA
- **Development**: ABAP, SAP Cloud Application Programming Model (CAP), SAP BTP low-code/no-code

The certification ecosystem has evolved in 2026 toward **performance-based, scenario-driven assessments** replacing traditional multiple-choice formats on most exams.

## 2026 Strategic Developments

### SAP RISE with SAP

**Definition**: A comprehensive cloud transformation service combining:
- SAP S/4HANA Cloud (private or public)
- SAP BTP for extensions and integrations
- SAP Analytics Cloud for analytics
- SAP Datasphere for master data governance
- Implementation, change management, training (via partners)

**Business Model**:
- Subscription-based (typically 5–7 year contracts)
- Transparent, tiered pricing
- Operational expenditure (OpEx) model vs. traditional CapEx

**Timeline**: Full transformations typically 6–18 months depending on landscape complexity and data migration volume.

**Career Impact**: Drives demand for **Cloud Solution Architects**, **Data Migration Specialists**, **SAP Basis Cloud Administrators**, and **Change Management Leads**.

---

### Joule: Agentic AI Assistant (2026 Rollout)

**What is Joule?** (Q1 2026 release)

Joule evolved from a copilot (2023–2025) to an **agentic AI platform** capable of autonomously executing end-to-end business processes across the SAP ecosystem.

**2026 Capabilities**:
- Over 40 specialized AI agents deployed
- 2,400+ Joule Skills across 35 SAP solutions
- Open agent-to-agent protocol for interoperability
- Natural language interface for process automation
- Multi-step execution without human intervention at each step

**Target Processes** (2026 roadmap):
- Finance: AP invoice processing, GL reconciliation, financial close automation
- HR: Hiring workflows, onboarding, payroll approvals
- Supply Chain: Procurement, vendor scorecards, demand planning
- IT: System administration, incident resolution, patch deployment

**Platform Requirements**:
- Requires RISE with SAP or GROW with SAP contract
- Excludes on-premise (ECC 6.0) installations
- Drives urgency for cloud migrations (ECC 6.0 support ends **December 31, 2027**)

**Career Implications**:
- New role: **Joule Implementation Specialist** (design agent workflows, skill configuration)
- Shift: Functional consultants → Process automation architects
- Demand: AI governance, prompt engineering, agent monitoring

### New Certification Model (2026)

**Practical, Scenario-Based Exams**:
- Rollout started **November 2025** (selected exams)
- All practical exams transitioned by **end-March 2026**
- Performance-based evaluation using real SAP environments
- Open-book format for most exams
- System and scenario-based testing

**Exams Transitioned First** (Nov 2025 onward):
- SAP BTP Administrator
- SAP Build Developer
- Solution Architect – SAP BTP
- SAP Business Data Cloud
- SAP Generative AI Developer

**Advantages**:
- Better alignment with actual job skills
- Less reliance on memorization
- Immediate practical applicability
- Higher relevance for employers

### ECC 6.0 End-of-Life (December 31, 2027)

SAP ends support for **mySAP ECC 6.0** on **December 31, 2027**. Over 10,000 customers face a migration decision:

**Options**:
1. **RISE with SAP** (S/4HANA Cloud Private/Public)
2. **Self-managed S/4HANA Cloud (Private Edition)**
3. **S/4HANA On-Premise** (extended support available, higher cost)

**Consulting Opportunity**: Mass migration workload drives hiring for:
- Data migration specialists
- Legacy system SMEs (ABAP, workflows, customizations)
- Cloud architects
- Change management leaders

## SAP Authorized Trainer Program

**Overview**: Pathway to become an official SAP trainer and instructor

**Eligibility Requirements**:
- Valid, current SAP certification in your target product/solution
- 3–4 years demonstrated experience with SAP solutions (portfolio/CV evidence)
- Training certification from recognized provider:
  - **Global Association for Quality Management (GAQM)** – Certified Professional Trainer (CPT)
  - **People 1st** – Training credentials
  - **Association for Talent Development (ATD)** – Train-the-trainer certification
- Annual renewal (12-month cycle)

**Benefits**:
- Access to SAP training materials and content
- Recognition in SAP Trainer Directory
- Eligible for SAP training partnerships
- Typical billing rate: $150–$300/hour (varies by geography, company)

**Process**:
1. Obtain SAP certification + trainer credential
2. Apply on [learning.sap.com/authorized-trainer](https://learning.sap.com/authorized-trainer)
3. SAP review & approval (2–4 weeks)
4. Annual renewal with continuing education
