# SR11 — Microsoft Dynamics 365 Functional Consultant → Solution Architect

**Role Summary:** Enterprise ERP/CRM systems engineer; progresses from domain-specialist (Sales, Finance, Supply Chain, Service) to full-stack solution designer bridging business requirement, technical architecture, Power Platform ecosystem, and Dataverse/Azure integration.

**Geography:** Global; mature partner ecosystem in South Africa (Synergy Software, Britehouse, BCX); strong adoption in banking/financial services (Discovery, Standard Bank).

## Two Career Paths: CRM vs. ERP

### Path A: D365 Customer Engagement (CRM)

**Scope:** Sales, Service, Marketing, Field Service, Customer Insights.

**Module Sequence:**
1. **MB-210 — Dynamics 365 Sales** (active)
   - Sales entities, opportunity management, quotes, order management.
   - Entry point for most CRM practitioners.
   - ~6 weeks study; renewal cycle 6 months.

2. **MB-230 — Dynamics 365 Customer Service** (active)
   - Case management, knowledge articles, service scheduling, omnichannel routing.
   - Often paired with Sales for full customer lifecycle.

3. **MB-220 — Dynamics 365 Marketing / Customer Insights – Journeys** (active; note: rebrand in progress)
   - Email marketing, lead scoring, customer journeys, event management.
   - Formerly "Marketing"; now integrates Microsoft Cloud for Customer Data Platform.
   - Pre-requisite: Sales fundamentals recommended.

4. **MB-240 — Dynamics 365 Field Service** (active)
   - Work orders, mobile scheduling, resource optimization, IoT integration.
   - Niche; typically specialist path.

5. **MB-280 — Customer Experience Analyst** (**RETIRING July 31, 2026**)
   - NPS, sentiment analysis, customer insights.
   - **REPLACEMENT: AB-210 Sales AI Consultant** (launching 2026, AI-native).

6. **PL-200 Functional Consultant — Power Platform** (**RETIRING Aug 31, 2026**)
   - Model-driven and canvas apps, automation, integration.
   - Bridges D365 and Power Apps/Automate.
   - Rapidly replaced by AI-focused credentials.

### Path B: D365 Finance & Operations (ERP)

**Scope:** Finance, Supply Chain Management, Manufacturing, Commerce, Business Central.

**Module Sequence:**

1. **MB-310 — Dynamics 365 Finance** (active)
   - GL, AR/AP, cash management, fixed assets, budgeting.
   - Core of ERP path.

2. **MB-330 — Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management** (**RETIRING June 30, 2026**)
   - Inventory, procurement, warehouse management, planning.
   - High-demand; affected by retirement.

3. **MB-335 — D365 Supply Chain Management Functional Expert** (**RETIRING June 30, 2026**)
   - Advanced SCM design; planning module depth.

4. **MB-320 — D365 Manufacturing** (**RETIRING June 30, 2026**)
   - Bill of materials, production scheduling, lean manufacturing.
   - Retiring; significant skill loss in partner ecosystem.

5. **MB-340 — Dynamics 365 Commerce** (active)
   - E-commerce, retail POS, omnichannel, merchandising.
   - Growth area; Cloud POS adoption accelerating.

6. **MB-800 — Dynamics 365 Business Central Functional Consultant** (active)
   - Smaller-market ERP; finance + supply chain + project accounting for SMB.
   - Lower barrier-to-entry than F&O; strong SMB partner growth.

7. **MB-820 — Business Central Developer** (verify 2026 status)
   - AL language (JavaScript-like syntax for BC extensions).
   - Active but less visible than F&O developer track.

## Technical Track: Developers & Solution Architects

### Development Path

1. **MB-500 — Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations Apps Developer** (active)
   - X++ language (legacy C-like syntax; still primary for F&O customization).
   - Plugin architecture, data entities, OData/Web API.
   - Prerequisite: MB-310 (Finance) or F&O functional knowledge.

2. **MB-700 — Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations Apps Solution Architect Expert** (active)
   - Cloud solution architecture (Azure, Dataverse, Power Platform integration).
   - Governance, ALM, security, compliance (SOX, GDPR).
   - Prerequisite: MB-500 or equivalent hands-on experience.
   - **High-value credential: $170–240K baseline; premium for hybrid cloud + AI integration.**

### Power Platform Parallel (Critical for Modern D365)

1. **PL-900 — Power Platform Fundamentals** (active)
   - Entry point; covers Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI, Copilot Studio.
   - ~8 hours study; non-technical overview.

2. **PL-100 — Power Apps App Maker** (active)
   - Canvas and model-driven app creation; Dataverse basics; Power Fx.
   - Bridge to functional consultant track.

3. **PL-200 — Power Platform Functional Consultant** (**RETIRING Aug 31, 2026**)
   - Deep model-driven design; business process flows; portals; integration patterns.
   - **Successor credentials unknown; likely AI-integrated by Sept 2026.**

4. **PL-400 — Power Platform Developer** (active)
   - Power Fx, JavaScript, Web API, plugins, custom connectors.
   - Bridges Power Apps and Dataverse custom development.

5. **PL-500 — Robotic Process Automation Developer** (**RETIRING June 30, 2026**)
   - Power Automate RPA (attended / unattended bots).
   - Migrating to AI-first automation; likely replaced by AI Process Miner credential.

6. **PL-600 — Power Platform Solution Architect** (**RETIRING June 30, 2026**)
   - Cloud architecture, Power BI governance, security, integration at scale.
   - **Critical gap: successor architecture credential unclear; monitor Microsoft Learn June 2026.**

## 2026 Transition: AI-Focused Credentials (AB- Series)

Microsoft is launching **AB-series credentials** focused on **AI, Copilot, and agent-based process automation**. As of April 2026:

- **AB-210 — Sales AI Consultant** (launched; replaces MB-280 Customer Experience Analyst)
- **AB-260 — Operations AI Consultant** (likely launching 2026 for Supply Chain / Ops)
- **PL-AI-1xx series** — Power Platform Copilot and Agents (anticipated)
- **MB-7xx AI specialists** — D365 Copilot design (anticipated; may replace PL-600)

**Impact on Career Path:**
- Functional consultants should **pursue AI-adjacent credentials early** (e.g., PL-900, PL-100, PL-400) to bridge into AI track.
- **Legacy credentials retiring June–Aug 2026** without clear successor for 6–12 months; best to complete them before cutoff if team depth required.
- Solution Architects must add **AI prompt engineering, Copilot user experience, agent orchestration** to 2026–2027 roadmap.

## Skills & Technical Depth

### Functional (All Tracks)

- D365 model-driven app design and configuration
- Dataverse entity design, relationships, business rules
- Power Fx (formula language; replaces JavaScript)
- Power Automate workflow automation (cloud + desktop)
- Requirements gathering and process mapping
- Role-based security and access control
- Data validation, error handling, audit trails

### CRM Specialist

- Sales pipeline and opportunity management
- Service SLA, routing rules, and omnichannel
- Marketing lead scoring and customer journeys
- Field Service resource optimization and IoT
- Voice of Customer (Insights) and sentiment analysis

### ERP Specialist

- Chart of Accounts and GL posting logic
- AR/AP, cash management, consolidated reporting
- Inventory and warehouse management (WMS)
- Procurement and vendor management
- Bill of materials, production scheduling
- Multi-entity, multi-currency, multi-legal-entity consolidation

### Technical (Development Track)

- **C# / .NET:** Plugin development (platform, event-driven, synchronous/asynchronous)
- **X++:** Finance & Operations custom code (legacy but persistent)
- **AL (Business Central):** Extension language for BC; JavaScript-like syntax
- **Power Fx:** Functional language for canvas/model-driven apps
- **Web APIs & OData:** Dataverse query, CRUD, batch operations
- **Custom Connectors:** Power Automate integration to non-native systems
- **Azure Integration:** Logic Apps, Azure Service Bus, Event Grid, Synapse Analytics
- **Data Migration:** Duplicate detection, staging, validation, rollback
- **ALM (Application Lifecycle Management):** Branching, merging, testing, promotion across dev/test/prod

### Solution Architect (Levels 5–6)

- Enterprise architecture frameworks (TOGAF, Azure Well-Architected)
- Cloud governance and compliance (SOX, GDPR, HIPAA, industry-specific)
- Multi-cloud scenarios (Azure, AWS hybrid, edge)
- Organizational change management and training design
- Total cost of ownership (TCO) and ROI modeling
- Microsoft Cloud for Industry (Healthcare, Sustainability, Manufacturing)
- Copilot and AI integration strategy (2026 onward)
- Customer success and managed services planning

## Industry & 2026 Trends

### Copilot & Generative AI Integration

By April 2026:
- **Dynamics 365 Copilots** embed AI across Sales, Service, Finance, Supply Chain.
  - Sales: opportunity scoring, email draft, next-best-action.
  - Service: case resolution suggestion, sentiment detection.
  - Finance: GL anomaly detection, budget forecasting.

- **Agentforce equivalent:** D365 Copilots with autonomous task execution (TBD naming).

**Career Impact:** Solution Architects must understand **prompt design, responsible AI governance, data privacy for AI models, and human-in-the-loop process design.**

**Career Path:** Industry architects specialize in 1–2 verticals; $220K+ salary potential in Financial Services / Health.

**Critical:** MB-320, MB-330, MB-335 all retiring **June 30, 2026**.
- Replacement strategy unclear; likely migrates to **"Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Copilot"** or **AB-260 Operations AI Consultant.**
- Organizations with active SCM projects should **complete MB-330 + MB-335 before June 30, 2026** if team certification matters.

### Business Central Growth

- **SMB ERP pivot:** Many organizations downscale F&O to Business Central (lower cost, faster ROI).
- MB-800 (BC Functional Consultant) and MB-820 (BC Developer) growing in demand.
- **AL language** becoming more valued; C# skills less critical for BC developers.

### Power Platform Maturity

- **PL-200 and PL-600 retiring Aug/June 2026** without clear successors until late 2026.

## Recommended Progression: Functional Consultant → Solution Architect

### Year 1–2: Specialist Depth (Levels 1–2)

1. Choose **one** module pathway (CRM: Sales/Service; ERP: Finance/SCM).
2. **Certify:** MB-210 (Sales) or MB-310 (Finance) by month 6.
3. **Skills:** Dataverse fundamentals; Power Automate basics; requirements gathering.
4. **Role:** Junior or Mid-level Consultant; deliver 2–3 implementations (project-based).

### Year 2–4: Cross-Module & Power Platform (Level 3)

1. Add **second module certification:** MB-230 (Service) if CRM; MB-340 (Commerce) or another ERP domain.
2. **Power Platform bridge:** Pursue PL-200 (Functional Consultant) *before Aug 31, 2026 retirement.*
3. **Skills:** Multi-module solution design; process re-engineering; stakeholder communication.
4. **Role:** Senior Consultant; lead 1–2 customer engagement streams.

### Year 4–6: Technical Track Decision (Level 4)

**Path A: Development / Technical Lead**
- Pursue **PL-400 (Power Platform Developer)** if primarily Power Apps/Automate.
- Pursue **MB-500 (F&O Developer)** if ERP deep-dive; learn X++.
- Build plugin, custom connector, and API integration expertise.
- **Role:** Technical Lead or Principal Developer; architect integrations, custom extensions, data migration.

**Path B: Solution Architecture (Recommended for Generalists)**
- Pursue **MB-700 (F&O Solution Architect)** or **PL-600 (Power Platform Solution Architect)** *before June 30, 2026 retirement.*
- Combine functional + technical knowledge; leadership skills (mentoring, governance, ALM).
- **Skills:** Azure, compliance, enterprise architecture, change management.
- **Role:** Solution Architect; pre-sales, customer strategy, team technical lead.

### Year 6+: Practice Leadership & Vertical Specialization (Level 6)

1. **Vertical expertise:** Health, Sustainability, Financial Services, Manufacturing.
2. **Add:** Microsoft Cloud for Industry certifications or partner specializations.
3. **Skills:** Industry regulation (HIPAA, GDPR, ISO, SOX), customer success, M&A due diligence, innovation roadmap.
4. **Copilot & AI:** Integrate Copilot design, prompt engineering, responsible AI governance.
5. **Role:** Practice Lead, Senior Solution Architect, or Industry Architect; business development, strategic customers, partner enablement.

## Final Progression: Solutions Architect to Practice Leader

**Level 5 → Level 6 Transition (Years 6–10+)**

To move from Solution Architect to Practice Lead or Industry Architect:

1. **Vertical Industry Depth:** Master 1–2 industries (Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing, Sustainability).
   - Certifications: Industry-specific "Advanced Specialization" or Microsoft Cloud for Industry modules.
   - Knowledge: Regulatory compliance (HIPAA, GDPR, SOX), vertical process standards.

2. **Business Acumen:** Shift from technical delivery to customer strategy.
   - Skills: M&A due diligence, TCO/ROI modeling, org change management, board-level communication.
   - Credentials: Optional; MBA or executive education strengthens positioning (2–3 year commitment).

3. **Innovation Leadership:** Own emerging tech integration (Copilot, AI, Agentforce).
   - Partner with Microsoft research and power CAT community.
   - Author thought leadership; speak at industry conferences.

4. **People Leadership:** Build and mentor a team of architects and developers.
   - Invest in team certifications; sponsor bootcamps.
   - Establish internal practices (governance, ALM, security standards).

5. **Compensation & Trajectory:** $200–300K+ (USD); equity in partnership model or executive role in consulting firm.

## Red Flags for Career Risk

- **Skipping functional depth:** Jumping straight to developer track without module certification creates knowledge gaps; avoid. Build breadth first (2–3 modules), then depth.
- **Overspecializing too early:** Locking into SCM (retiring June 2026) or MB-280 (retiring July 2026) without backup plan. Diversify module knowledge.
- **Ignoring Power Platform:** Power Apps/Automate integration is critical for modern D365. Functional consultants must learn PL-100 + PL-200 or risk obsolescence as Power Platform becomes standard.
- **Neglecting AI/Copilot upskilling:** By 2027, Copilot design and responsible AI governance are table-stakes for architects. Delay = career stall.
- **Not renewing certifications:** All D365/PL certs expire every 6 months. Renewal exams required; lapsed credentials affect job market credibility.
