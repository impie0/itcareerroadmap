# Microsoft Vendor Overview

Microsoft operates the largest IT certification portfolio in the industry, with **54+ active certifications** across cloud infrastructure (Azure), productivity (Microsoft 365), security and identity (Security/Compliance), artificial intelligence, Power Platform low-code/no-code, and Dynamics 365 business applications. This overview consolidates the current state as of May 1, 2026, including **critical retirement dates** in 2026, free learning pathways, renewal mechanics, and salary impact.

---

## About Microsoft Learn

[Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/) is the official, **completely free** learning platform for all Microsoft certifications. Every certification pathway includes:
- Self-paced learning modules (free, no account required beyond a Microsoft account)
- Official exam preparation guides and study materials
- Hands-on labs and interactive content
- Free renewal assessments for Associate, Specialty, and Expert certifications

**No third-party vendor required.** All training content is directly authored and maintained by Microsoft.

---

## Certification Tier Structure

Microsoft transitioned from legacy MCSA/MCSE/MTA paths (2012–2020) to a modern **role-based certification model** launched between 2018 and 2020:

| Tier | Duration | Renewal | Salary Impact | Examples |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fundamentals** | ~6–12 hrs study | No expiry | +$2k–$5k entry | AZ-900, SC-900, PL-900 |
| **Associate** | ~40–60 hrs study | Annual free assessment | +$5k–$15k mid-career | AZ-104, SC-200, PL-200 |
| **Specialty** | ~40–60 hrs study | Annual free assessment | +$8k–$20k niche | AZ-140 AVD, DP-700 |
| **Expert** | ~60–100 hrs study | Annual free assessment | +$15k–$30k senior | AZ-305, SC-100, PL-600 |

### Renewal Mechanism (All Role-Based Certs)

**Free, unproctored online assessment** available in the 6-month window before expiry. Assessment covers only recent updates and takes ~45 minutes. Unlimited retakes; if you don't pass, wait 24 hours and retry. **Cost: $0.** Once you pass, certification extends 1 year from original expiration date.

## 2026 Retirement Calendar & Replacements

Microsoft is **retiring 7+ certifications in 2026**, primarily to pivot toward AI-native roles. All retiring certifications remain forever on your transcript and are permanently verifiable; you simply cannot take the exam or renew after the retirement date.

| **AI-900** | June 30, 2026 | AI-901 (Fundamentals) | April 21, 2026 (beta) | Shift: individual Azure AI services → Microsoft Foundry + agentic AI |

## Salary & Career Impact Summary

**Fundamentals:** Entry point; no salary bump alone, but required for career entry.

**Associate certs (first):** +$5k–$15k annual salary increase; roles like Azure Admin, Cloud Developer, DBA: $85k–$140k base.

**Associate certs (multiple):** Cumulative credibility; associate holders typically $110k–$160k.

**Expert certs:** +$15k–$30k per cert; Solutions Architects, Security Architects, DevOps Engineers: $130k–$210k.

**Specialty certs:** +$8k–$25k depending on niche (AVD, IoT, SAP); narrow but lucrative.

**Renewal cadence:** Annual $0 cost via free assessments; certs lapse if not renewed (but transcripts show they were earned).
