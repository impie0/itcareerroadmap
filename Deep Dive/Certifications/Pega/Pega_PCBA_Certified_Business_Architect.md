## About

The PCBA certification is designed for individuals who want to demonstrate their ability to design and implement Pega solutions to meet business requirements. This credential validates expertise in translating business requirements into functional application components and working with stakeholders to design processes.

## Domain Context

The PCBA sits at the intermediate level of the Pega certification pathway. It focuses on business architecture and design aspects of the Pega platform, making it ideal for business analysts, business architects, and solution designers who need to bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical development teams.
