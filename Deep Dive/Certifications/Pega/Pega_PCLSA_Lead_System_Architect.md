## About

The PCLSA is the top-tier Pega certification, requiring demonstrated expertise in both theoretical architecture knowledge and practical application development. This certification is designed for senior architects and technical leads who architect complex, enterprise-scale Pega solutions and provide technical leadership on implementation projects.

## Domain Context

PCLSA represents the pinnacle of Pega technical certification. It combines written architecture examination with hands-on development assessment, validating the ability to design and implement large-scale, complex Pega Platform solutions. This certification is ideal for architects, technical leads, and principal engineers.
