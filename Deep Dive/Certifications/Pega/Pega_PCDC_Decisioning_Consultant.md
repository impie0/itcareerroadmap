## About

The PCDC certification demonstrates expertise in Pega's advanced decisioning capabilities, including decision rules, customer data platforms, and AI/ML integration. This certification is designed for professionals focused on decision science, analytics, and intelligent business automation, particularly in financial services and customer-centric organizations.

## Domain Context

PCDC addresses the specialized domain of decisioning and customer intelligence within Pega applications. It complements traditional system architecture certifications by focusing on the decision science and analytics aspects of the platform. This certification is increasingly valuable as organizations embed AI/ML into their Pega solutions.
