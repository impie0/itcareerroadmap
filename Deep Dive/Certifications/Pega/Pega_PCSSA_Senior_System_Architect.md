## About

The PCSSA certification validates advanced technical expertise in Pega Platform architecture and development. This certification demonstrates the ability to design and implement complex Pega applications at scale, with deep knowledge of system architecture, integration patterns, and optimization techniques. It is a key stepping stone toward the PCLSA (Lead System Architect) certification.

## Domain Context

PCSSA represents advanced technical capability in Pega development and architecture. It bridges the gap between mid-level developers (PCBA level) and enterprise architects (PCLSA level). This certification is ideal for senior developers, architects, and technical leads who design complex Pega solutions and mentor junior team members.
