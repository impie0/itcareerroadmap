## About

The CSSYB certification is the foundational credential in the ASQ Six Sigma certification pathway. Yellow Belts support process improvement initiatives as team members and task leaders, focusing on basic problem-solving and understanding of Six Sigma fundamentals. This certification requires no prior experience or education prerequisites, making it an ideal starting point for professionals new to quality improvement, continuous improvement, and Lean methodologies.

## Domain Context

The CSSYB serves as the entry point to the Six Sigma credential pathway (Yellow Belt → Green Belt → Black Belt → Master Black Belt). Yellow Belts typically work within their functional areas supporting improvement projects, identifying improvement opportunities, and contributing to team-based initiatives. This certification is valuable across all industries and roles.
