## About

The CQIA certification is an entry-level credential designed for individuals new to quality improvement and process optimization. Unlike the Six Sigma pathway (Yellow Belt, Green Belt, Black Belt), the CQIA provides a foundational alternative for professionals beginning their quality management careers. It validates understanding of fundamental quality principles, problem-solving methodologies, and process improvement concepts applicable across industries.

## Domain Context

The CQIA serves as an accessible entry point into quality management for students, early-career professionals, and career changers. It complements the Six Sigma pathway and can precede Yellow Belt or Green Belt certifications. Many organizations use CQIA as a stepping stone for identifying and developing future improvement leaders.
