## About

The CSSGB certification validates expertise in the application of Six Sigma tools, data-driven problem-solving, and process improvement. Green Belts are operationalization specialists who lead or support improvement projects under Black Belt guidance, typically managing projects within their functional areas. This credential is ideal for professionals with at least three years of work experience seeking to enhance their quality and improvement expertise.

## Domain Context

The CSSGB sits at the intermediate level of the Six Sigma certification pathway. Green Belts serve as execution leaders for improvement initiatives, working on projects that deliver measurable business results through process optimization. This role bridges front-line operations and strategic improvement initiatives (Black Belt) level.
