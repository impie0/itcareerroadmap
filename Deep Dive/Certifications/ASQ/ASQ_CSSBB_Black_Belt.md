## About

The CSSBB certification is the premier credential for continuous improvement and operational excellence professionals. Black Belts are strategic change leaders who design, implement, and manage major process improvement initiatives using Six Sigma and Lean methodologies. This certification demonstrates expertise in advanced statistical analysis, project management, and organizational change. Candidates must have completed at least one Six Sigma project and submitted a signed project affidavit.

## Domain Context

The CSSBB represents mastery-level expertise in Six Sigma methodology and organizational improvement. Black Belts serve as transformation leaders within organizations, owning cross-functional projects that deliver significant financial and operational impact. This certification is highly valued in manufacturing, financial services, healthcare, and technology sectors.

## Topics Covered

- Define phase (advanced): Enterprise alignment, strategic objectives, resource allocation
- Measure phase (advanced): Advanced MSA, process capability indices, SIPOC analysis
- Analyze phase (advanced): Hypothesis testing, design of experiments (DOE), multivariate analysis
- Improve phase (advanced): Design for Six Sigma (DFSS), optimization techniques, Lean integration
- Control phase (advanced): Control plans, SPC (Statistical Process Control), mistake-proofing
- Advanced statistical methods: Regression, ANOVA, multivariate analysis
- Design of Experiments (DOE): Factorial designs, response surface methodology
- Lean integration and waste elimination
- Change management and organizational development
- Financial justification and ROI calculation
- Project portfolio management
- Supplier management and quality assurance
