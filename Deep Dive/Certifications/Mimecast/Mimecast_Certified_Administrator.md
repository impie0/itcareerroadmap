## About

The Mimecast Certified Administrator certification (Secure Email Gateway Advanced) is Mimecast's professional-level credential for email security and cloud gateway administrators. It builds on foundational knowledge to validate the ability to configure, deploy, and troubleshoot Mimecast's email security solutions in production environments. The exam emphasizes real-world scenarios, security policy design, threat mitigation, and cloud gateway administration. Candidates demonstrate expertise in protecting against phishing, malware, data loss, and email-based compliance risks. The certification is free for active Mimecast customers and delivered entirely through Mimecast University.

---

## Domain context — Email Security / Cloud Protection

Email security and cloud gateway services protect organizations against phishing, malware, business email compromise, and data exfiltration. Email remains the primary attack vector for ransomware and credential theft. Cloud-native email security solutions like Mimecast provide centralized threat detection, policy enforcement, URL rewriting, sandboxing, and compliance archiving without on-premises hardware.

## Topics covered

The Secure Email Gateway Advanced exam assesses knowledge across these domains (weights not formally published by Mimecast):

- Email threat defense (phishing, malware, BEC, zero-day)
- Cloud gateway configuration and deployment
- Policy management and routing rules
- DMARC, SPF, DKIM authentication and enforcement
- Data loss prevention (DLP) policies and tuning
- Inbound and outbound filtering
- Secure messaging and encryption
- User authentication and authorization
- Reporting, logging, and forensics
- Incident response and troubleshooting
- Integration with Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and legacy on-premises mail servers
- Compliance requirements (GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, industry-specific)
