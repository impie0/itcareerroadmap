## About

The Mimecast Secure Email Gateway Fundamentals Certification validates foundational knowledge of email security and Mimecast platform configuration for IT administrators and security professionals new to email gateway solutions. Designed for anyone entering the email security field or learning core Mimecast product capabilities, this entry-level cert is the gateway to Mimecast's two-tier certification program. Released in its current form in September 2024, the cert is maintained by Mimecast Education and delivered via Mimecast University, Mimecast's official partner-training platform.

---

## Domain context — Email Security

Email remains the primary attack vector for phishing, data exfiltration, ransomware delivery, and business email compromise (BEC). Modern email security platforms extend beyond gateway filtering to include advanced threat protection, data loss prevention (DLP), archiving, user training, and incident response—integrated into a unified human risk management strategy.
