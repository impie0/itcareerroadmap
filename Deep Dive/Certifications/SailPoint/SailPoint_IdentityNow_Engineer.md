## About

The SailPoint Certified IdentityNow Engineer (CINE) validates professional-level expertise in deploying and managing Identity Security Cloud (formerly IdentityNow), SailPoint's flagship SaaS identity governance and administration (IGA) platform. Launched in 2018 alongside the IdentityNow product, CINE has become the de facto certification for cloud-first identity engineers. The exam reflects 2026 platform reality: the rebranding of IdentityNow to Identity Security Cloud (ISC) on the Atlas platform in late 2024 did not change the cert name or exam blueprint; both continue as CINE. The certification covers cloud-native architecture, source and connector management (including native cloud connectors), account aggregation and identity profiles, provisioning and lifecycle workflows, access certification and governance, role engineering, and API-driven integrations. Suitable for engineers implementing identity access management in cloud-first enterprises and organizations migrating from legacy on-premises IdentityIQ. CINE stacks alongside legacy IdentityIQ credentials and leads into architect and specialist pathways.

## Domain context — Identity/IGA

Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) manages user authentication, authorization, access rights, and identity lifecycle across on-premises, cloud, and hybrid infrastructure. SailPoint dominates enterprise IGA with IdentityNow/ISC widely deployed in Fortune 500 financial services, healthcare, government, and tech sectors. IGA professionals design policies, automate provisioning, enforce compliance (SOX, GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001), and remediate unauthorized access.

## Topics covered

SailPoint's exam blueprint covers the following domains (approximate weights):

- **Identity Security Cloud Architecture & Atlas Foundation** (15%) — Cloud platform topology, multi-tenancy, Identity Security Cloud suite components, API-first design, tenant provisioning, SaaS deployment models, cloud-native scaling.
- **Source & Connector Management** (18%) — Source configuration and lifecycle, connector deployment (cloud-hosted vs. on-premises proxy), native cloud connectors, custom connector development, source health monitoring, data sync troubleshooting.
- **Account Aggregation & Identity Profiles** (15%) — Account discovery and correlation, identity profile construction and refresh, attribute mapping and normalization, matching rules, data quality enforcement, identity reconciliation at scale.
- **Provisioning & Lifecycle Management** (18%) — Provisioning policy design and workflows, entitlement synchronization, role-based provisioning logic, off-boarding automation, request routing, approval chains, exception handling.
- **Access Governance & Certification** (14%) — Access review campaign design, certification workflows, access policies, risk-based remediation, compliance evidence collection, attestation audit trails.
- **Roles, Policies & Governance** (12%) — Role mining and curation, role lifecycle management, segregation of duties (SoD) policy definition, compliance mapping (SOX, GDPR, HIPAA), role-based entitlement strategy.
- **Workflows, Automation & Integrations** (8%) — Workflow Designer, conditional logic, REST API consumption, third-party integrations, automation best practices, webhook triggers.

## Common skills at Identity/IGA · Professional

*Shared competencies across the Identity/IGA domain at professional level — not specific to IdentityNow.*

- **Source integration and data quality** — Connect diverse on-premises and cloud systems; manage account correlation, reconciliation, and error recovery at scale.
- **Identity data governance** — Normalize and maintain authoritative identity data; enforce attribute lineage and audit trails; handle multi-source reconciliation.
- **Access certification and compliance** — Design and run access review campaigns; document findings for SOX, SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and HIPAA audits.
- **Provisioning workflow orchestration** — Design multi-system provisioning logic; handle entitlement synchronization, role-based provisioning, and off-boarding at scale.
- **Role engineering and access policy** — Mine and curate role taxonomies; enforce segregation of duties; translate business rules into technical policies.
- **Incident response and access remediation** — Identify over-provisioned access; coordinate rapid remediation across disparate systems; document compliance evidence.

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what the CINE exam actually tests, distinct from shared "Common skills" above.*

- **Identity Security Cloud (formerly IdentityNow) platform architecture** — Multi-tenancy, Atlas infrastructure, cloud-native design, API surface, identity profile and connector topology.
- **Source and connector deployment and troubleshooting** — Native cloud connectors, on-premises proxy deployment, correlation logic, sync health monitoring, error recovery.
- **Identity data aggregation and normalization** — Account correlation rules, identity profile construction, attribute mapping, data quality enforcement, multi-source reconciliation.
- **Provisioning workflows and lifecycle automation** — Workflow Designer, role-based provisioning, entitlement synchronization, off-boarding automation, approval routing, exception handling.
- **Access governance and certification workflows** — Access review campaign design, remediation tracking, compliance evidence, attestation audit trails.
- **Role mining, curation, and policy frameworks** — Using analytics to identify roles; enforcing segregation of duties; compliance policy mapping (SOX, GDPR, HIPAA).
- **REST API and third-party integrations** — Consuming Identity Security Cloud APIs, building custom integrations, webhook triggers, identity federation patterns.
- **Monitoring, troubleshooting, and audit reporting** — Using platform logs and dashboards, identifying and resolving sync failures, generating compliance reports.
