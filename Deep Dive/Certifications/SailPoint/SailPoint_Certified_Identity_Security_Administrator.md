## About

The SailPoint Certified Identity Security Administrator certification was launched on February 17, 2026, as part of SailPoint's comprehensive certification expansion program. This new professional-level credential enables identity security professionals, system administrators, and cloud platform specialists to validate real-world administrator expertise in managing cloud-based identity security solutions.

This certification is designed for practitioners with hands-on experience deploying, configuring, and maintaining SailPoint Identity Security Cloud (ISC) platforms in production environments. It recognizes professionals who can effectively manage the operational and administrative aspects of identity governance implementations.

### Context: Why This Certification Was Created

SailPoint launched this new certification and a comprehensive recertification program as part of their initiative to address the identity security skills gap. The move reflects the industry-wide shift from on-premises IdentityIQ deployments to cloud-based Identity Security Cloud solutions, with this administrator-level credential filling the gap between professional and engineer-level expertise.

This certification is the seventh exam offering within the SailPoint Professional Certification and Credentialing Program, serving a community of over 12,000 SailPoint certified professionals.

## Domain context — Identity / IGA

Identity and Access Governance (IGA) is a critical cybersecurity domain focused on managing user identities, access rights, and compliance across enterprise systems. SailPoint is a market leader in IGA solutions, offering both on-premises (IdentityIQ) and cloud-based (Identity Security Cloud) platforms.

The administrator role sits at the operational level, responsible for:
- Daily platform operations and maintenance
- User and access provisioning/deprovisioning
- Policy enforcement and compliance monitoring
- Virtual appliance management
- System configuration and optimization
- Incident response and troubleshooting
- Stakeholder support and escalation handling

Cloud-based IGA (ISC) requires administrators to understand cloud-native architecture, multi-tenancy, API-driven workflows, and SaaS operational best practices.

## Related certs

### SailPoint Certification Pathway

**Below CISA (Entry-Level)**
- **SailPoint Certified Identity Security Professional** (Knowledge Credential)
  - Foundation-level understanding of ISC
  - No exam; earned through training completion

**Peers to CISA**
- **SailPoint Certified IdentityIQ Engineer** (on-premises platform, engineer level)
  - Alternative for organizations using on-premises IdentityIQ
- **SailPoint Certified Identity Security Cloud Professional** (cloud platform, professional level)
  - Alternative professional credential for ISC

**Above CISA (Advanced)**
- **SailPoint Certified Identity Security Engineer** (cloud platform, engineer level)
  - Advanced role requiring 1+ year hands-on experience
  - Broader technical depth across ISC platform
- **SailPoint Certified Identity Security Cloud Architect** (if available; status unclear as of 2026)
  - Highest SailPoint cloud credential (if available)
  - Requires CISSP, CISM, or IdentityIQ Architect prerequisite
