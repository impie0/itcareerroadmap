## About

The Zscaler Digital Transformation Administrator (ZDTA) is an **Associate-level** credential validating operational expertise in deploying, configuring, and managing the **Zscaler Zero Trust Exchange (ZTE)** platform across enterprise environments. Candidates demonstrate hands-on competency across ZIA (Internet Access), ZPA (Private Access), ZDX (Digital Experience), and emerging modules (ITDR, Risk360). The ZDTA is the gateway between foundational zero-trust knowledge (ZTCA entry-level) and advanced architecture (ZDTE / architect track). Designed for security operations teams, infrastructure engineers, and solutions engineers shipping Zscaler at mid-to-large enterprises. Zscaler leads the SASE market (Gartner Magic Quadrant) with 160+ global data centre nodes; the ZDTA validates practical mastery of this dominant platform.

## Topics covered

The ZDTA exam blueprint distributes weight across **9 domains**:

- **Identity Services** (4%) — SAML, SCIM, OIDC; user authentication, identity policy administration, audit logs
- **Connectivity Services** (20%) — Device posture, trusted networks, browser controls, TLS/SSL inspection, internet access policy frameworks
- **Platform Services** (15%) — Zscaler Zero Trust Exchange architecture, module licensing, platform administration
- **Access Control Services** (15%) — Micro-segmentation, ZTNA least-privilege policies, application-level controls
- **Cyberthreat Protection Services** (20%) — Threat prevention, antimalware, phishing, URL filtering, advanced threat detection
- **Data Protection Services** (13%) — DLP policy design, data classification, exfiltration prevention, encryption
- **Risk Management** (3%) — Risk360, cyber risk quantification, threat scoring
- **Zscaler Digital Experience** (7%) — ZDX monitoring, performance correlation, user experience metrics
- **Zscaler Zero Trust Automation** (3%) — Orchestration, automation frameworks, advanced tooling (UVM, EASM, Deception)
