## About

The Zscaler Certified Associate (ZCA) is a foundational partner certification that validates understanding of Zscaler's platform goals, vision, and value proposition. Designed for pre-sales engineers, sales professionals, and technical partners, ZCA covers Zscaler's mission to disrupt hub-and-spoke network security and drive zero trust adoption. Launched in 2024–2025 to replace the Zscaler Certified Sales Specialist (ZCSS), it serves as a mandatory prerequisite for both the Zscaler Certified Sales Professional (ZCSP) and Zscaler Certified Sales Engineer (ZCSE) certifications. Free to eligible partners through the Zscaler Partner Academy.

---

## Domain context — Cloud Security / Zero Trust

Enterprise security is shifting from perimeter-based (hub-and-spoke) defense to zero trust architectures: verify every identity, validate every request, inspect all traffic, assume breach. Zscaler leads this transition with secure internet gateway (SIG), zero trust network access (ZTNA), and cloud native security services.

## Topics covered

ZCA focuses on Zscaler's strategic positioning and platform overview:

- **Company Vision & Mission** — Zscaler's role in disrupting legacy network security; strategic roadmap
- **Problem Statement** — Limitations of hub-and-spoke, perimeter-only models; business & technical drivers for zero trust
- **Zscaler Platform Overview** — Core architecture, service portfolio (SIG, ZTNA, DLP, threat prevention), cloud-native design
- **Value Proposition** — Economic benefits vs. traditional appliances; customer outcomes and ROI indicators
- **Solution Areas** — Deep dive into Zscaler's four key solution pillars (application access, web security, network security, advanced threat)
- **Competitive Positioning** — Competitive advantages vs. Palo Alto Networks, Fortinet, Cisco; differentiation strategy
- **Partner Ecosystem** — Integration partnerships, channel programs, go-to-market strategy
