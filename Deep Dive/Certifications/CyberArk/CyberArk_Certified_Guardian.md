## About

The CyberArk Certified Guardian (CGE) is an **invitation-only, elite-level credential** validating mastery of Privileged Access Management (PAM) architecture and identity security strategy. Positioned as the mid-tier (Associate-level) certification within CyberArk's four-level stack—below the entry-level Trustee and foundational Defender/Sentry—it targets experienced professionals architecting enterprise-wide PAM deployments. Released circa 2024, Guardian holders have proven advanced technical competency in CyberArk Vault configuration, Central Policy Manager (CPM) automation, Password Vault Web Access (PVWA) deployment, session management, and least-privilege enforcement across large organizations. The exam costs $2,250 and requires 24-month active maintenance via re-certification. Exclusive by design: candidates must hold 2+ years of both Defender and Sentry certifications plus carry two CyberArk technical references, making it a credential for senior practitioners and consultants.

## Domain context — Security · Privileged Access Management (PAM)

Comprehensive protection of high-risk accounts and credentials through centralized vaults, automated password rotation, session recording, and identity federation. PAM sits at the intersection of identity governance (IAM) and network security, focusing on non-human and elevated human accounts.

## Topics covered

Exam blueprint (Official CyberArk exam guide):

- **Privileged Access Management (PAM) Fundamentals** — PAM architecture, threat models, least privilege, credential lifecycle, separation of duties
- **CyberArk Vault Installation & Configuration** — Vault setup, hardware/OS requirements, clustering, disaster recovery, secure communication
- **Central Policy Manager (CPM) Deployment** — CPM architecture, password policy configuration, target system integration, automated rotation, verification
- **Password Vault Web Access (PVWA)** — PVWA installation, SSO/LDAP/MFA integration, access control policies, audit logging
- **Privileged Session Manager (PSM)** — Session recording, protocol support, threat analytics, user activity monitoring
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) & Identity Federation** — MFA integration, SAML/OAuth, Conjur for credential injection
- **Compliance & Audit** — SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001 alignment; audit trail management; threat detection
- **Advanced Integration & Automation** — CyberArk API, REST/SOAP, third-party integrations (Salesforce, ServiceNow, cloud platforms)
- **Troubleshooting & Performance Tuning** — Vault diagnostics, CPM logs, PVWA issues, high-availability optimization
- **Architecture & Enterprise Design** — Multi-tier deployments, hub-spoke models, geographic redundancy, scaling for enterprise
