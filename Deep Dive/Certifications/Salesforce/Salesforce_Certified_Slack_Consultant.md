# Salesforce Certified Slack Consultant

**`Slack-Con-201`** · ● **Active** · **Associate** · _Salesforce / Slack_

---

## Note on Slack Sales Engineering

No dedicated "Slack Certified Sales Engineer" credential exists. Slack certifications focus on three tracks: **Administrator**, **Consultant**, and **Developer**. This guide documents the **Slack Certified Consultant** credential, which is the closest fit for customer-facing technical professionals, solution designers, and sales engineers who implement Slack across enterprises. Sales engineers at Slack (and Slack partners) pursuing certification typically pursue the Consultant track rather than a sales-specific certification.

## About

The Salesforce Certified Slack Consultant credential validates expertise in enterprise Slack implementation, solution design, change management, and user adoption strategies. Aimed at implementation consultants, solution architects, and technical sales professionals, this certification demonstrates the ability to deliver complex Slack engagements for Enterprise Grid customers. Launched in 2021, it has become the primary credentials path for Slack partners and consultants; renewal is required every 24 months.

## Topics covered

Per the official exam blueprint, candidates are tested on four major domains:

- **Discovery & Vision (15–20%)**: Articulating Slack value proposition, researching client org structure and workflows, conducting stakeholder interviews, establishing vision statements and success metrics
- **Design & Platform (25–30%)**: Configuring identity management (SAML, SSO, OAuth), workspace administration, permissions, security settings, governance, and compliance capabilities (SOC 2, HIPAA, FedRAMP)
- **Learning & Enablement (20–25%)**: Curriculum design, creating learning materials, designing rollout communication plans, facilitating live training, supporting user adoption, troubleshooting common issues
- **Delivery & Migration (20–25%)**: Best practices for engagement delivery, defining roles/responsibilities between Slack, partner, and client, leading migrations, pre-migration activities, execution, issue management

*Source:* [Slack Certified Consultant Exam Guide ↗](https://forcedigest.com/slack-certified-consultant-exam-guide/)
