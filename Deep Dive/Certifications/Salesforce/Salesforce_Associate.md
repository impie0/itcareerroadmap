## About

The Salesforce Certified Associate certification validates foundational knowledge of the Salesforce platform, CRM concepts, and basic administration. Designed as an entry point to the Salesforce ecosystem, it assesses understanding of Salesforce navigation, data models, configuration, and reporting fundamentals. This cert is ideal for new users, coordinators, and career changers entering the Salesforce field.

---

## Domain context — CRM/Foundation

Foundational certification covering core CRM principles, Salesforce platform basics, and end-user competency. This level typically gates entry into junior Salesforce roles and is the natural prerequisite path toward Salesforce Administrator and other specialized credentials.

[Read full deep dive — Salesforce Ecosystem →](../../Ecosystems/Salesforce_Ecosystem.md)

## Topics covered

- **Salesforce Ecosystem (32%)** — Platform overview, cloud services, Salesforce products and editions, user roles and permissions
- **Navigation (28%)** — Core UI navigation, record access, list views, global search, customization basics
- **Data Model (25%)** — Objects, fields, relationships (lookup/master-detail), record types, field dependencies
- **Reports & Dashboards (15%)** — Report creation, filtering, dashboard widgets, reporting fundamentals
