## About

The Salesforce Certified Data Cloud Consultant validates expertise in designing, configuring, and deploying Salesforce Data Cloud (formerly Customer Data Cloud). This professional-level certification is for solution architects and consultants who architect unified customer data platforms on Salesforce, enabling real-time segmentation, activation, and insights across Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud, and Sales Cloud. Launched in 2023, it addresses growing demand for CDP specialists as Salesforce's data strategy has matured.

---

## Domain context — Data/CDP

Customer Data Platforms (CDPs) unify disparate data sources into a single source of truth for customer identity and analytics, enabling personalization and activation across channels.

## Topics covered

- **Solution Design & Architecture** — designing Data Cloud solutions aligned with business requirements
- **Data Sources & Bring-Together** — connecting and ingesting first-party, second-party, and third-party data sources; Streaming, Batch, and API ingest
- **Data Mapping & Transformation** — modeling customer data and mapping sources to unified schemas
- **Identity Resolution** — configuring match strategies and identity graphs for unified customer views
- **Calculated Insights** — building derived attributes and scoring models for segmentation and activation
- **Segmentation & Audiences** — creating dynamic segments and managing audience activation
- **Activation & Integration** — activating audiences to Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud, Sales Cloud, and external channels
- **Data Security & Governance** — field-level encryption, PII handling, compliance (GDPR, CCPA), and data retention
- **Monitoring & Optimization** — performance tuning, error handling, and data quality
