## About

The Salesforce Certified Revenue Cloud Consultant credential validates expertise in designing, building, and implementing Revenue Cloud solutions that manage the entire Product-to-Cash lifecycle. Certified professionals demonstrate the ability to scope, design, configure, and deploy next-level Revenue Cloud implementations that meet complex customer business requirements.

This certification is for consultants, solutions architects, and implementation specialists who work with Revenue Cloud—Salesforce's comprehensive solution for managing CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote), billing, subscriptions, and revenue lifecycle management in a single unified platform.

## Domain context

Revenue Cloud addresses the complex requirements of modern B2B businesses managing sophisticated product offerings, dynamic pricing, complex subscription models, and multi-currency billing at scale. Organizations use Revenue Cloud to:

- Streamline the Quote-to-Cash process
- Implement complex pricing and discounting strategies
- Manage product bundles and configurations
- Handle subscriptions, renewals, and amendments
- Automate billing and revenue recognition
- Ensure compliance with revenue recognition standards
- Integrate revenue operations across the enterprise

This certification validates the ability to architect and implement solutions across CPQ, Billing, and Subscriptions capabilities.

## Topics covered

The certification exam covers the following major topic areas:

1. **Revenue Cloud Architecture & Foundation** (15%)
   - Revenue Cloud product overview and capabilities
   - Product-to-Cash lifecycle
   - Integration with Sales Cloud and other systems
   - Data model and object relationships

2. **CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) Configuration** (25%)
   - Product setup and catalog management
   - Bundling and configuration options
   - Quote generation and management
   - Contract management
   - Amendment and renewal processes

3. **Pricing & Discounting Strategies** (15%)
   - Price rules and automation
   - Discount strategies and approval workflows
   - Price adjustments and overrides
   - Complex pricing scenarios

4. **Subscriptions & Recurring Revenue** (15%)
   - Subscription setup and management
   - Recurring products and services
   - Renewal and amendment workflows
   - Usage-based and consumption billing

5. **Billing & Revenue Lifecycle** (15%)
   - Billing schedules and cycles
   - Invoice generation and management
   - Revenue recognition principles
   - Credit memos and adjustments
   - Multi-currency billing

6. **Implementation & Integration** (15%)
   - Implementation best practices
   - Integration patterns and considerations
   - API and middleware integrations
   - Data migration strategies
   - Performance and optimization
