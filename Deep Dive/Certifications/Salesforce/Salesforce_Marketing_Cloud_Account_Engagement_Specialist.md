# Salesforce Certified Marketing Cloud Account Engagement Specialist

**`MC-201`** · ● **Active** · **Associate** · _Salesforce_

> **Credential overview:** This certification validates expertise in designing, building, and implementing B2B marketing automation workflows using Salesforce Account Engagement (formerly Pardot). Candidates demonstrate foundational knowledge of lead generation, nurturing, qualification, and reporting in a marketing automation context. Originally launched as the Pardot Specialist credential; rebranded to Account Engagement Specialist in 2022 following Salesforce's product naming consolidation.

## About

Salesforce Certified Marketing Cloud Account Engagement Specialists are validated to design and manage B2B marketing automation workflows, lead nurturing campaigns, and reporting within the Account Engagement platform. The credential emerged from the Pardot Specialist certification (2016+) and was rebranded in 2022 when Salesforce consolidated Pardot into its Marketing Cloud Account Engagement product family. This associate-level certification serves marketing professionals and marketing ops specialists who implement campaigns, manage leads, and build automated journeys, but stops short of full implementation consulting (that belongs to the Professional-level Account Engagement Consultant credential). Pass rates are generally 60–70% on first attempt; study time typically 40–60 hours for those with 1+ years of hands-on experience.

## Domain context — Marketing/CRM

Marketing Automation is a subdomain of CRM/MarTech, focusing on B2B lead management, nurturing, scoring, and conversion workflows. Account Engagement is Salesforce's entry-level offering in this space; the Professional Consultant credential targets deeper implementation. Related domains include Cloud (Salesforce infrastructure), IT Management (automation governance), and Data/AI (reporting & analytics).

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint covers the following domains and approximate weightings (per official Salesforce exam guide):

- **Visitors and Prospects** (14%) — Anonymous visitor tracking; prospect creation and lifecycle; prospect audits; list imports; data quality and dedupe
- **Administration** (14%) — Field mapping and custom fields; data sync between Account Engagement and Salesforce CRM; user roles and security; account hierarchy; multi-org setup
- **Engagement Programs and Personalization** (16%) — Engagement Studio (visual automation builder); drip campaigns; dynamic list membership; nurturing workflows; trigger logic
- **Forms and Landing Pages** (16%) — Form design and configuration; progressive profiling; form handlers; landing page creation; list capture mechanics
- **Email Marketing** (14%) — Email template design and rendering; dynamic content insertion; email performance metrics; deliverability; CAN-SPAM and GDPR compliance; list management; bounce handling
- **Lead Management** (16%) — Lead scoring (explicit, implicit, model-based); lead grading; scoring rules; prospect-to-lead conversion; MQL/SQL lifecycle
- **Reporting** (10%) — Campaign reporting and analytics; ROI attribution (multi-touch, first-touch, last-touch); custom reports and dashboards; opportunity influence

*Source:* [Salesforce Account Engagement Specialist Exam Objectives ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/marketingcloudaccountengagementspecialist)

## Skills validated

*Concrete technologies and workflows this exam directly tests:*

- Account Engagement (Pardot) platform UI and Lightning App
- Salesforce CRM integration and data flow (lead objects, sync rules)
- Engagement Studio (visual automation, drip campaigns)
- Email template design and dynamic content rules
- Landing pages, forms, and form handlers
- Lead scoring models (explicit + implicit)
- List imports, list exports, and data management
- Campaign attribution and multi-touch ROI reporting
- Custom fields and object relationships
- User management and security roles within Account Engagement
- CRM synchronization and prospect/account mapping
- Compliance: CAN-SPAM, GDPR, anti-spam regulations
- Basic Salesforce admin concepts (fields, workflows, approval processes)
