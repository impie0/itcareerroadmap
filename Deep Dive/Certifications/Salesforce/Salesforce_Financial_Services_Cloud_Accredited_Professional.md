## About

The Salesforce Financial Services Cloud (FSC) Accredited Professional certification validates expertise in implementing and configuring Salesforce Financial Services Cloud solutions for wealth management, banking, and insurance organizations. This professional-level credential is designed for consultants, administrators, and solutions architects with 2–3 years of hands-on experience deploying FSC to meet financial institutions' specific needs. Candidates must demonstrate competency in FSC data models (Person, Account, Households, Financial Goals), wealth and banking features, compliance frameworks (GDPR/CCPA/POPIA), and integration with financial systems. The credential emphasizes practical implementation over foundational knowledge.

## Domain context — Finance/Industries (Salesforce Ecosystem)

Salesforce Financial Services Cloud is a purpose-built vertical solution for banking, wealth management, and insurance sectors, separate from general Sales Cloud or Service Cloud. This certification sits within Salesforce's broader ecosystem of industry-specific clouds (Health Cloud, Public Sector Cloud, Manufacturing Cloud, etc.) but is distinct in its focus on financial compliance, household-based customer modeling, and wealth management workflows.

## Topics covered

The exam is organized around four main domain areas with published weightings:

- **FSC Fundamentals & Data Model (25%)** — Financial Services Cloud architecture, core data objects (Person, Account, Household, Financial Account, Financial Goal), org setup, integration patterns
- **Wealth Management Features (25%)** — Household management, financial account aggregation, goal-based planning, action plans, advisor collaboration, goal tracking and outcomes
- **Banking & Insurance Features (20%)** — Industry-specific features for bank product management, insurance claim workflows, policy management, regulatory compliance modules
- **Compliance, Privacy & Security (20%)** — GDPR, CCPA, POPIA compliance implementation, field-level security, audit trails, data retention policies, encryption, consent management
- **Financial Account Aggregation & Integration (10%)** — Third-party data aggregation, external system integration, Salesforce Industries CPQ for FSC, action plan execution
