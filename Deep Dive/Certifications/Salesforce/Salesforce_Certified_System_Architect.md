## About

The Salesforce Certified System Architect is a domain-level expert credential that does not require a standalone exam. Instead, it is automatically awarded upon earning all four architect-level designer certifications: Certified Platform Developer I (PDI), Certified Development Lifecycle and Deployment Architect (DLDA), Certified Identity and Access Management Architect (IAM), and Certified Integration Architect. The credential validates expertise in designing scalable, integrated Salesforce solutions with robust security and governance. System Architects focus on off-platform systems integration, identity and access management, deployment governance, and continuous platform modification. The credential is a prerequisite pathway toward the Salesforce Certified Technical Architect (CTA) — the pinnacle of Salesforce certifications.

## Topics covered

### Salesforce Certified Platform Developer I (PDI)

- **Apex language fundamentals** (loops, conditional logic, object-oriented principles)
- **Apex testing** (test methods, assertions, code coverage)
- **Visualforce** (markup, controllers, component reuse)
- **Triggers and asynchronous processing** (batch apex, scheduled jobs, queueable jobs)
- **Data modeling and relationships** (custom objects, fields, relationships)
- **Governor limits** and performance optimization

*Source:* [Platform Developer I Exam Blueprint ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/platformdeveloperone)

### Salesforce Certified Development Lifecycle and Deployment Architect (DLDA)

- **Environment strategies** (sandbox management, deployment planning)
- **Change management** (release cycles, testing protocols, rollback procedures)
- **Version control and DevOps** (source control integration, continuous integration/deployment)
- **Testing frameworks** (unit testing, integration testing, system testing)
- **Data deployment** (data migration, data validation)
- **Monitoring and governance** (logs, debugging, audit trails)

*Source:* [Development Lifecycle and Deployment Architect Exam Blueprint ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/en/credentials/developmentlifecycledeploymentarchitect)

### Salesforce Certified Identity and Access Management Architect (IAM)

- **Single Sign-On (SSO)** (SAML, OAuth 2.0 flows)
- **User identity and authentication** (password policies, multi-factor authentication, community user management)
- **Authorization and permissions** (role hierarchies, sharing rules, field-level security, object-level security)
- **External identity** (customer identity, partner access)
- **Federation and provisioning** (directory integration, SCIM, user lifecycle)

*Source:* [Identity and Access Management Architect Exam Blueprint ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/identityandaccessmanagementarchitect)

### Salesforce Certified Integration Architect

- **Integration patterns and middleware** (point-to-point, hub-and-spoke, event-driven)
- **API-led connectivity** (REST, SOAP, GraphQL)
- **Salesforce Integration Cloud** (MuleSoft Anypoint, connector frameworks)
- **Data synchronization** (master data management, eventual consistency)
- **Error handling and resilience** (retry logic, monitoring, alerting)
- **Performance and scalability** (throughput optimization, rate limits)

*Source:* [Platform Integration Architect Exam Blueprint ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/integrationarchitect)
