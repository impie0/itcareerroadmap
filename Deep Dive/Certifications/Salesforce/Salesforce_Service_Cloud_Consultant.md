## About

The Salesforce Certified Service Cloud Consultant certification validates expertise in designing, implementing, and optimizing Service Cloud solutions for customer service organizations. Launched in 2016, this professional-level credential is designed for consultants, architects, and implementation specialists responsible for translating customer requirements into scalable service delivery models. It serves both large enterprise implementations and mid-market rollouts, covering case management, knowledge management, omni-channel intake, contact center operations, and integration patterns critical to modern customer service platforms.

## Domain context — Service/Support

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint spans eight major topic areas:

- **Industry Knowledge & Implementation Strategies** (12%) — Service delivery models, vertical requirements, organizational change management
- **Service Cloud Solution Design** (20%) — Requirements analysis, design patterns, capability mapping, trade-offs
- **Intake and Interaction Channels** (15%) — Email-to-Case, Web-to-Case, Live Agent, Chat, Voice, Messaging strategies
- **Case Management** (18%) — Case routing, assignment, escalation logic, field service integration, lifecycle management
- **Knowledge Management** (10%) — Knowledge base design, article publishing, search optimization
- **Contact Center Analytics & Reporting** (12%) — Dashboards, KPIs, queue metrics, agent productivity
- **Integration and Data Management** (8%) — APIs, middleware, data sync, external system connection
- **Service Console & Tools** (5%) — Workspace customization, UI configuration, advanced features

*Source:* [Official Service Cloud Consultant Exam Guide ↗](https://developer.salesforce.com/resources2/certification-site/files/SGCertifiedServiceCloudConsultant.pdf)
