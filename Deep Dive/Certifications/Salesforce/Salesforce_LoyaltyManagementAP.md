## About

The Salesforce Loyalty Management Accredited Professional credential demonstrates expertise in designing, planning, and delivering customer loyalty solutions that create measurable business value. Certified professionals have 2-3 years of experience designing solutions using Loyalty Management functionality and can lead implementations within customer organizations.

This certification is designed for solution designers, trusted advisors, facilitators, and implementation specialists who use both technical expertise and delivery experience to help customers achieve measurable results through loyalty programs. It validates the ability to discover, design, plan, and deliver business value using the Salesforce Loyalty Management platform.

## Domain context

Customer loyalty is critical to business success in competitive markets. Loyalty Management enables organizations to:

- Create and manage comprehensive loyalty programs
- Design personalized customer experiences
- Increase customer lifetime value
- Improve customer retention and engagement
- Integrate loyalty with Service Cloud, Sales Cloud, and Marketing Cloud
- Leverage data across the Salesforce Customer 360 platform
- Implement tiered loyalty programs with complex rules
- Automate member communications and rewards
- Measure program performance and ROI

This certification validates the ability to architect and implement loyalty solutions that align business strategy with technology.

The certification exam covers the following major topic areas:

1. **Loyalty Management Fundamentals** (15%)
   - Loyalty Management product overview
   - Core concepts and terminology
   - Customer 360 integration
   - Business value and ROI

2. **Program Design & Strategy** (20%)
   - Program design principles
   - Member segmentation strategies
   - Tiered program structures
   - Benefit design and mechanics
   - Gamification elements

3. **Loyalty Configuration** (25%)
   - Member setup and hierarchies
   - Program setup and rules engine
   - Points and rewards configuration
   - Tier management and progression
   - Enrollment and qualification rules

4. **Integration & Cross-Cloud** (20%)
   - Sales Cloud integration
   - Service Cloud integration
   - Marketing Cloud integration
   - Customer 360 data model
   - API and system integration

5. **Member Experience & Engagement** (10%)
   - Member communications
   - Digital engagement
   - Mobile and self-service experiences
   - Omnichannel touchpoints

6. **Analytics & Performance** (10%)
   - Program analytics and reporting
   - Member journey tracking
   - KPI measurement
   - Business intelligence and dashboards
