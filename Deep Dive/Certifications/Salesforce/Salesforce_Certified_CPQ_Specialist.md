## About

The Salesforce Certified CPQ Specialist credential validates expertise in designing, configuring, and implementing Salesforce Configure-Price-Quote (CPQ) solutions. CPQ is a specialized Salesforce product that automates complex quote generation, bundle configuration, dynamic pricing, and contract management for enterprise sales organizations. Holders demonstrate hands-on proficiency in product bundling, pricing rules, quote template design, and the renewal/amendment workflow. This professional-level certification targets Salesforce functional consultants, sales operations specialists, and technical architects who lead CPQ implementations.

## Domain context — Sales/Quoting

Sales and Quoting automation spans the revenue-operations function: managing product hierarchies, bundling, pricing complexity, quote generation, and contract lifecycle. Salesforce CPQ is the market-leading platform for mid-market and enterprise quoting workflows, integrated with Sales Cloud and order-to-cash processes.

## Topics covered

The CPQ Specialist exam blueprint emphasizes configuration and quoting design:

- **CPQ Platform Foundations** (~9%) — CPQ architecture, licensing, admin console, field mapping, org setup
- **Bundle Configurations** (~28%) — Feature bundles, option hierarchies, option constraints, configuration attributes, bundle-level rules
- **Pricing** (~25%) — Price rules, price dimensions, tiered pricing, summary variables, discount rules, conditional pricing
- **Quote Templates** (~6%) — Dynamic document generation, output sections, field merging, calculated fields, rendering logic
- **Product Selection** (~14%) — Product hierarchies, products, pricebooks, pricebook entries, product rules, feature selection
- **Orders, Contracts, Amendments, and Renewals** (~18%) — Order management, contract creation, amendment workflows, renewal quoting, term management

## Skills validated

*What this exam concretely tests, beyond the shared "Common skills" domain competencies.*

- Salesforce CPQ platform architecture and configuration
- Product bundling, feature options, option constraints, and configuration attributes
- Price rule design (conditional logic, tiered pricing, discount rules, price dimensions)
- Summary variables and calculated fields for dynamic pricing
- Dynamic quote template design and output document generation
- Quote field customization and calculated quote fields
- Product hierarchy, product rules, and product selection workflows
- Order and order product management
- Contract creation, amendment, and renewal workflows
- Renewal quoting and term-management logic
- CPQ admin console: field mapping, field sets, page layouts, record types
- User permissions and sharing models for CPQ objects
- Troubleshooting product rules, pricing rules, and template rendering
- Integration with Salesforce Sales Cloud and order-to-cash workflows
