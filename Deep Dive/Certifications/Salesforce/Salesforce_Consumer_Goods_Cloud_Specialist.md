## About

The Salesforce Certified Consumer Goods Cloud Accredited Professional validates expertise in deploying, configuring, and managing Salesforce Consumer Goods Cloud for retail and FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods) organizations. This certification demonstrates proficiency in the specialized data models, trade promotion management workflows, field sales execution, and demand planning capabilities that distinguish Consumer Goods Cloud from the core Salesforce platform. Released in 2020 as part of Salesforce's vertical Industry Cloud portfolio, it targets consultants, architects, and practitioners implementing CPG solutions for retailers, distributors, and manufacturers.

---

## Domain context — Industries / Retail

Salesforce Industries (vertical cloud solutions) combines core Salesforce capabilities with prebuilt data models, templates, and processes tailored to specific sectors. Consumer Goods Cloud specializes in FMCG and retail execution for manufacturers, distributors, and channel partners managing field sales, promotions, and customer visibility.
