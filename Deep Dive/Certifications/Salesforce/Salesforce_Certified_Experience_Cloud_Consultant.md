# Salesforce Certified Experience Cloud Consultant

**`EX-Con-101`** · ● **Active** · **Professional** · _Salesforce_

> The Salesforce Experience Cloud Consultant credential validates expertise in designing, configuring, building, and implementing Salesforce Experience Cloud solutions in customer-facing consulting roles. Requires Salesforce Administrator certification as a prerequisite and a minimum of 6 months hands-on implementation experience.

## About

The Salesforce Experience Cloud Consultant credential, formerly known as Community Cloud Consultant, validates professional-level expertise in implementing Salesforce Experience Cloud solutions. Rebranded in April 2021 as Salesforce expanded the platform beyond community-focused sites to broader customer experience use cases, the cert now covers the full breadth of Experience Cloud capabilities including customer communities, partner networks, supplier communities, and employee networks. It is designed for consulting professionals, solutions architects, and implementation specialists who work hands-on with organizations deploying Experience Cloud at scale.

---

## Domain context — Web/Communities (Salesforce Ecosystem)

Salesforce Experience Cloud is the primary platform for building branded, customer-facing web experiences and community portals directly on Salesforce infrastructure. It spans customer communities, partner communities, supplier portals, knowledge bases, and employee networks — covering digital transformation use cases where organizations need to deliver personalized, authenticated web experiences to external or internal audiences while maintaining governance, security, and integration with Salesforce core data.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint is organized into seven major domains with defined weightings:

- **Experience Cloud Basics** (13%) — Creating experiences for specific use cases, identifying personas, choosing appropriate experience types
- **Sharing, Visibility, and Licensing** (17%) — User visibility rules, object permissions, member access control, license allocation
- **Branding, Personalization, and Content** (15%) — Theme customization, dynamic content, Experience Builder configuration, personalization rules
- **Templates and Themes** (12%) — Theme selection, responsive design, component placement, template types
- **User Creation and Authentication** (13%) — Member provisioning, SSO integration, login flows, identity management
- **Adoption and Analytics** (14%) — Site analytics, user adoption strategies, reporting on Experience Cloud usage
- **Administration, Setup, and Configuration** (25%) — Site creation, domain management, security settings, role/profile mapping, Experience Cloud limits and best practices

*Source:* [Official exam guide ↗](https://developer.salesforce.com/resources2/certification-site/files/SGCertifiedCommunityCloudConsultant.pdf)

## Skills validated

- Experience Cloud site creation, configuration, and lifecycle management
- Sharing rules, role-based access control, and member visibility enforcement
- Experience Builder theme customization and responsive design implementation
