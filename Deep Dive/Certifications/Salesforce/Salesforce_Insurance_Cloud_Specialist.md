## About

The Salesforce Financial Services Cloud Accredited Professional (AP-208) certification, when focused on the Insurance module, validates that professionals can architect, configure, and implement insurance solutions using Salesforce's industry-specific data model. Insurance professionals demonstrate mastery of policy lifecycle management, producer networks, claims workflows, regulatory compliance, and OmniStudio integration. Designed for Salesforce partners, consultants, and solution architects implementing insurance systems for carriers, brokers, and agencies. Launched in 2021 as part of Salesforce's industry-cloud expansion, the Insurance module represents one of the fastest-growing Financial Services Cloud specializations.

---

## Domain context — Insurance/Industries

Salesforce Industries represents vendor-specific industry-cloud solutions: pre-built data models, processes, and compliance frameworks for banking, wealth management, insurance, healthcare, communications, energy, automotive, and government sectors. Insurance Cloud is the dedicated platform for property-casualty, life, health, and specialty insurance carriers and their distribution networks.

## Topics covered

Insurance Cloud implementation spans the full policy lifecycle and operational ecosystem:

- **Insurance Data Model** (15%) — Policy, Producer, Claim, Account, Coverage, Participant, Asset, Transaction objects and relationships
- **Policy Management** (15%) — New business workflows, policy configuration, household relationships, multi-policy rollups
- **Producer Management** (12%) — Agent/broker networks, licensure, commission hierarchies, referral tracking
- **Underwriting** (12%) — Risk assessment, case workflows, approval routing, documentation handling
- **Claims Processing** (15%) — Claim registration, assessment, reserves, settlements, subrogation tracking
- **Servicing & Operations** (13%) — Service requests, policy servicing, payment processing, statement generation
- **Compliance & Governance** (10%) — Regulatory reporting, audit trails, data residency, documentation
- **OmniStudio Integration** (8%) — Flex Card configuration, data Raptors, integration procedures, user experience orchestration
