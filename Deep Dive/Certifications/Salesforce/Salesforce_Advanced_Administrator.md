## About

The Salesforce Certified Advanced Administrator credential is for seasoned Salesforce administrators who master advanced features, security controls, automation, and system optimization. This exam validates the ability to implement complex configuration, manage record access, design process automation, maintain data quality, and oversee environment governance. ADM-211 is the professional-level follow-up to the foundational ADM-201 Administrator certification and is mandatory prior to pursuing this credential.

## Topics covered

The exam covers seven weighted domains of Salesforce administration knowledge:

- **Security & Access Control (20%)** — Custom profiles, permission sets, delegated administration, field-level security (FLS), record access models (sharing rules, role hierarchy, organization-wide defaults), territory management, and data security implications
- **Objects & Apps (19%)** — Relationship types (master-detail, many-to-many, lookup), related lists, record types, schema builder, object management, Lightning App Builder, dynamic forms, standard Lightning components, console configuration, and Lightning page layout
- **Auditing & Monitoring (8%)** — Setup audit trail, login history, record modification fields, field history tracking, debug logs, and system monitoring
- **Cloud Apps Integration (7%)** — Service Cloud features, integration patterns, and extending native Salesforce solutions
- **Data Management (10%)** — Data import (Data Import Wizard vs. Data Loader), duplicate detection, validation rules, data enrichment, and maintaining data quality
- **Analytics & Reporting (10%)** — Custom report types, reporting snapshots, complex charting, custom summary formulas, bucketing, joined reports, cross-filters, dynamic dashboards, and dashboard filters
- **Environment Management & Deployment (8%)** — Change sets, sandboxes, managed/unmanaged packages, Salesforce Extensions for Visual Studio Code, and deployment strategies
