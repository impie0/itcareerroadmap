## About

The Energy & Utilities Cloud Accredited Professional credential validates 2–4 years of Salesforce consulting experience in the energy and utilities sector. Holders demonstrate foundational to intermediate knowledge of the Energy & Utilities Cloud platform, including discovery processes, solution architecture, data model configuration, and deployment practices. The credential is designed for Salesforce partners and consultants delivering customer value in a rapidly digitizing energy industry facing grid modernization, renewable integration, smart metering, and customer engagement transformation.

## Domain context — Industries / Energy & Utilities

Energy and utilities companies manage critical infrastructure across generation, transmission, distribution, and customer care. Salesforce Energy & Utilities Cloud provides industry-specific data models, customer engagement workflows, field service integration, billing connectors, and regulatory compliance features for both residential and commercial/industrial customers in competitive and regulated markets.

## Topics covered

The exam is split into four sections testing competency across the full consulting lifecycle:

- **Discovery (25%)** — Outline technical scope for statements of work (SOWs); map use cases to business outcomes; document current-state and desired-state process flows; identify key customer stakeholders and pain points; articulate Energy & Utilities Cloud fit.
- **Solution Design (25%)** — Design future-state business processes aligned to customer vision; validate Energy & Utilities Cloud data model against requirements; define customer segmentation (residential, C&I); design billing and service delivery workflows; plan Field Service integration; map back-office system integration points.
- **Implementation (25%)** — Execute initial product setup and configuration; enable key cloud components (Customer Engagement, Billing Integration, Field Service); create custom objects and process automation; configure Industries CPQ for energy; manage metadata and sandbox promotion; govern configuration changes.
- **Test & Deploy (25%)** — Plan testing strategy and execute test cases; manage customer acceptance testing (CAT); handle UAT sign-off; execute go-live cutover plan; manage post-deployment support; update and sustain instances; track service delivery KPIs.
