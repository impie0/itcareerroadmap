## About

The Salesforce Manufacturing Cloud Accredited Professional credential demonstrates expertise in designing, implementing, and managing industry-specific solutions using the Salesforce Manufacturing Cloud. Certified professionals have the knowledge, skills, and experience to implement Manufacturing Cloud with industry-specific data models, addressing the unique operational challenges of manufacturing organizations.

This certification is exclusively available for Salesforce partners and is designed for consultants, solutions architects, and implementation specialists who focus on manufacturing industry solutions and leverage Manufacturing Cloud capabilities to deliver business value.

## Domain context

Manufacturing organizations face distinct operational challenges including:

- Supply chain and procurement management
- Order-to-cash processes
- Product lifecycle management
- Production planning and scheduling
- Quality management and compliance
- Inventory and warehouse management
- Regulatory compliance and traceability
- Customer relationship management specific to manufacturing
- Integration with ERP and MES systems
- Visibility across distributed operations

Manufacturing Cloud provides industry-specific data models and functionality built on Salesforce to address these requirements. This certification validates the ability to architect and implement comprehensive manufacturing solutions.
