## About

The Salesforce Certified Financial Services Cloud Specialist (AP-208) validates expertise in designing, implementing, and managing Salesforce Financial Services Cloud (FSC) solutions for banking, wealth management, and insurance organizations. Released in 2019, this professional-level credential targets consultants, architects, and administrators with 2–3 years of hands-on FSC deployment experience. Candidates demonstrate competency in FSC data models (Person Account, Household, Financial Account, Financial Goal), wealth and banking workflows, advisor collaboration features, and compliance frameworks (GDPR, CCPA, POPIA). The exam emphasizes practical implementation and domain-specific compliance rather than foundational Salesforce knowledge.

---

## Domain context — Finance/Industries (Salesforce Ecosystem)

Salesforce Financial Services Cloud is a purpose-built vertical solution for banking, wealth management, and insurance sectors. Separate from general Sales Cloud or Service Cloud, FSC specializes in customer household modeling, financial account aggregation, advisor-based wealth workflows, and financial compliance. This certification sits within Salesforce's broader ecosystem of industry-specific clouds (Health Cloud, Public Sector Cloud, Manufacturing Cloud, Energy/Utilities Cloud) but is distinct in its focus on multi-account household relationships, GDPR/CCPA/POPIA compliance, financial data governance, and advisor collaboration.

## Topics covered

The exam is organized around five main domain areas with published weightings:

- **FSC Data Model & Fundamentals (25%)** — Financial Services Cloud architecture, core data objects (Person Account, Account, Household, Financial Account, Financial Goal), org setup, data relationships, Salesforce Industries integration
- **Wealth Management Features (25%)** — Household structure and relationship management, financial account aggregation and reconciliation, goal-based wealth planning, action plans, advisor collaboration workflows, household hierarchy modeling
- **Banking & Insurance Industry Modules (20%)** — Bank product management, account opening workflows, insurance policy management, claim workflows, industry-specific process automation, financial products configuration
- **Compliance, Privacy & Security (20%)** — GDPR, CCPA, and POPIA compliance implementation, field-level security, data retention policies, audit trails and logging, encryption for financial data, consent management, compliance reporting
- **Financial Account Integration & External Data (10%)** — Third-party financial data aggregation, external system connectors, open banking APIs, data mapping and reconciliation, Salesforce Industries CPQ, third-party integration patterns
