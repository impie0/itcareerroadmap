## About

The Salesforce Advanced Field Service Accredited Professional credential validates expertise in designing, configuring, building, and implementing Advanced Field Service solutions. Certified professionals demonstrate deep knowledge of field service operations and the ability to architect and deliver complex field service implementations that improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

This certification is exclusively available for Salesforce partners and is designed for consultants and implementation specialists who specialize in field service automation, scheduling optimization, asset management, and mobile workforce management.

## Domain context

Advanced Field Service enables organizations to optimize field operations including:

- Service scheduling and resource management
- Mobile workforce management and tracking
- Asset and equipment lifecycle management
- Preventive and predictive maintenance
- Service territory management
- Field service planning and optimization
- Real-time scheduling and dispatching
- Mobile access and offline functionality
- Integration with IoT and field data collection
- Analytics and performance optimization

This certification validates the ability to implement comprehensive field service solutions that reduce operational costs and improve service delivery.

## Topics covered

The certification exam covers the following major topic areas:

1. **Field Service Fundamentals** (10%)
   - Field Service architecture and components
   - Core concepts and terminology
   - Industry use cases and best practices
   - Integration with Sales Cloud

2. **Asset & Equipment Management** (11%)
   - Asset tracking and hierarchy
   - Equipment configuration
   - Asset lifecycle management
   - Warranty and maintenance tracking

3. **Scheduling & Optimization** (22%)
   - Service scheduling setup
   - Scheduling rules and constraints
   - Resource optimization
   - Capacity planning
   - Territory management

4. **Advanced Scheduling & Automation** (15%)
   - Automated scheduling
   - Optimization algorithms
   - Work order and service appointment management
   - Dynamic rescheduling
   - Flow and process automation

5. **Mobile & Fieldwork Optimization** (12%)
   - Mobile app configuration
   - Offline functionality
   - Real-time field updates
   - GPS tracking and mapping
   - Mobile data synchronization

6. **Configuration & Customization** (15%)
   - Custom objects and fields
   - Business rules and validation
   - Integration patterns
   - API and middleware configuration

7. **Analytics & Reporting** (15%)
   - Field service analytics
   - KPI tracking
   - Dashboard creation
   - Performance measurement
   - Predictive analytics
