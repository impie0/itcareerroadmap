## About

The Salesforce Industries Communications Cloud Accredited Professional credential (AP-203) validates architects', consultants', and specialists' expertise in designing, planning, and delivering Communications Cloud solutions for telecommunications, media, and communications service providers. Launched in 2024 as part of Salesforce's Accredited Professional program, this professional-level certification targets consultants and partners implementing Salesforce's purpose-built communications industry platform. It requires demonstrated knowledge of Communications Cloud's data model, order management, OmniStudio integration, CPQ/catalog configuration, contract lifecycle management, and B2B/B2C workflow automation.

## Domain context — Salesforce Industries Ecosystems

Salesforce Industries provides pre-built, industry-specific solutions for healthcare, financial services, nonprofit, public sector, and communications. Communications Cloud is the AI-powered CRM designed for telecom operators, media, and CSP (Communications Service Provider) business models.

## Topics covered

**Discovery (24%)**
- Assess current IT ecosystem architecture using architectural guiding principles
- Determine KPIs based on business pain points and budget constraints
- Select appropriate Salesforce products for Communications Cloud workloads
- Map and document business processes, requirements, and current state workflows
- Evaluate platform constraints based on data volumes and performance thresholds
- Map legacy processes to native Communications Cloud functionality

**Planning and Design (~45%)**
- Design data model (Account, Service, Contact, Order hierarchies)
- Configure Orders and Order Management for telecom service lifecycle
- Implement OmniStudio for dynamic form and interaction orchestration
- Design B2B and B2C customer journey workflows
- Configure Communications Cloud Catalog and CPQ for service offerings
- Map contract lifecycle management (CLM) and compliance requirements

**Delivery (~31%)**
- Plan data migration from legacy billing, CRM, or order systems
- Implement out-of-the-box (OOTB) configurations vs. custom extensibility trade-offs
- Integrate Communications Cloud with billing, provisioning, and network systems
- Deploy OmniStudio components and user experience flows
- Validate and test order-to-fulfillment workflows
- Support go-live readiness and production handoff
