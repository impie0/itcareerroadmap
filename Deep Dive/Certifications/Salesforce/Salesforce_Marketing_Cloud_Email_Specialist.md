## About

The Salesforce Certified Marketing Cloud Email Specialist certification validates expertise in designing, building, and managing email marketing campaigns within Salesforce Marketing Cloud. This Associate-level credential is for marketing professionals and Marketing Cloud administrators who specialize in email delivery, subscriber management, segmentation, and compliance. Released in 2019, it remains the foundational certification for email-focused practitioners in the Salesforce ecosystem.

---

## Domain context — Marketing Cloud

Salesforce Marketing Cloud is a customer experience marketing platform for email, SMS, social, mobile, and advertising. Email remains the core workload; this cert focuses exclusively on the email channel.

## Topics covered

Exam blueprint breakdown (per Salesforce Trailhead):

- **Email Marketing Best Practices** (11%) — compliance, deliverability, list hygiene, unsubscribe management
- **Content Creation & Delivery** (24%) — email builder, templates, dynamic content, HTML/text personalization
- **Marketing Cloud Email Subscribers & Targeting** (16%) — subscriber profiles, list segmentation, preference centers, audience building
- **Marketing Cloud Tools & Features** (16%) — Journey Builder, Automation Studio, Content Blocks, Asset management
- **Marketing Cloud Tracking & Reporting** (11%) — tracking opens/clicks, reports, analytics, engagement metrics
- **Marketing Cloud Setup & Administration** (11%) — account configuration, user roles, data extensions, imports/exports
- **Compliance & Permissions** (11%) — CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL, data governance, security best practices

*Source:* [Marketing Cloud Email Specialist Exam Guide ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/marketingcloudemailspecialist)
