## About

The Salesforce Certified Tableau CRM Architect (formerly Einstein Analytics+ Architect) validates expert-level expertise in designing, deploying, and scaling enterprise CRM analytics platforms. Originally launched in 2021 for Einstein Analytics administrators, it was rebranded in 2023 to reflect Salesforce's unified Tableau product portfolio. Candidates demonstrate mastery of multi-org embedded analytics, data architecture, performance optimization, and governance across both Tableau Cloud and on-premises deployments. This is the highest-tier CRM analytics certification in the Salesforce ecosystem.

## Topics covered

- **Data architecture & modeling** – CRM Analytics data layer, fact/dimension tables, SAQL fundamentals
- **Data Manager & Dataflows** – ETL pipeline design, transformation recipes, connectors, replication
- **Dashboard & binding design** – layout optimization, dynamic bindings, parameterization, drill-through
- **Multi-org analytics & embedded analytics** – cross-org data governance, embeddable dashboards, app integration
- **Performance tuning & optimization** – query acceleration, indexing, materialized views, caching strategies
- **Security & governance** – row-level security (RLS), sharing rules, field-level security, audit trails
- **Migration & deployment** – org-to-org replication, change management, disaster recovery
- **Einstein Discovery & AI integration** – predictive insight layer, model deployment, recommendation engines
