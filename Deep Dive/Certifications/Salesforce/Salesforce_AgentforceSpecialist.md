## About

The Salesforce Certified Agentforce Specialist credential demonstrates expertise in managing, optimizing, and implementing Agentforce—Salesforce's agentic AI platform. Certified professionals understand both Salesforce platform configuration and Agentforce capabilities, and have the ability to design, configure, and optimize AI agents that deliver real business value.

This certification is designed for professionals who manage or implement Agentforce within their organizations and want to validate their expertise in leveraging AI agents to automate workflows, enhance customer experiences, and drive business outcomes. The Agentforce Specialist certification replaces the former AI Specialist exam and reflects the evolution toward agentic AI.

## Domain context

Agentforce represents Salesforce's move into production-grade agentic AI. Organizations use Agentforce to:

- Automate complex business processes with AI agents
- Enhance customer service and support automation
- Streamline sales processes with intelligent agents
- Improve operational efficiency
- Deliver personalized customer experiences at scale
- Integrate AI capabilities across the Salesforce platform
- Leverage Einstein AI and generative AI capabilities
- Build autonomous workflows and decision-making
- Reduce manual effort and accelerate business processes
- Ensure compliance and governance in AI implementations

This certification validates the ability to architect and implement Agentforce solutions that create measurable business impact.

## Topics covered

The certification exam covers the following major topic areas:

1. **Agentforce Fundamentals** (15%)
   - Agentforce overview and capabilities
   - Agentic AI concepts and terminology
   - Use cases and business value
   - Agentforce product roadmap
   - Integration with Salesforce ecosystem

2. **AI & Generative AI in Salesforce** (20%)
   - Einstein AI overview
   - Generative AI capabilities
   - Prompt engineering basics
   - Model Builder and AI customization
   - Responsible AI principles

3. **Copilot & Agent Design** (20%)
   - Copilot Builder overview
   - Creating and configuring AI agents
   - Natural language processing
   - Intent recognition and routing
   - Agent training and refinement
   - Conversation design

4. **Prompt Builder & Model Builder** (15%)
   - Prompt Builder functionality
   - Prompt engineering best practices
   - Custom prompt creation
   - Model Builder for custom AI models
   - Fine-tuning and optimization
   - RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

5. **Workflow Automation & Integration** (15%)
   - Automating processes with Agentforce
   - Flow integration with agents
   - System integration
   - APIs and middleware
   - Data input and output handling

6. **Implementation & Administration** (10%)
   - Agent deployment
   - Configuration best practices
   - User adoption strategies
   - Monitoring and optimization
   - Governance and compliance
   - Performance tuning

7. **Analytics & Performance** (5%)
   - Agent analytics and monitoring
   - Performance metrics
   - Continuous improvement
   - Usage and adoption tracking
