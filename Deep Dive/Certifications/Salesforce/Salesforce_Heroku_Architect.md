## About

The Salesforce Certified Heroku Architect certification validates expert-level knowledge of designing, architecting, and deploying applications on the Heroku platform. It demonstrates mastery of Heroku platform architecture (dynos, buildpacks, PostgreSQL, Kafka), Heroku Connect bi-directional Salesforce integration, enterprise features (Private Spaces, Shield), security and compliance, DevOps practices, and production-grade scaling patterns. Candidates must demonstrate expertise in application design, data architecture, performance optimization, and team-scale deployment workflows. The credential is for senior architects and solutions engineers who design Heroku-based solutions in enterprise environments.

## Topics covered

- **Heroku Platform Architecture** — Dynos, process types, buildpacks, slugs, Procfiles, config management, runtime lifecycle
- **Application Design and Patterns** — 12-factor methodology, microservices, design patterns, stateless application design
- **Data Architecture** — Heroku Postgres (replication, failover, backups), Heroku Connect (bi-directional Salesforce sync), data modeling, Kafka on Heroku
- **Security and Compliance** — TLS/SSL, secrets management, environment variables, Private Spaces, network isolation, HIPAA/SOC 2, Shield compliance
- **DevOps and Continuous Delivery** — CI/CD pipelines (Heroku Pipelines, review apps), monitoring, logging integration (Datadog, New Relic, Papertrail)
- **Performance and Scalability** — Dyno sizing, auto-scaling strategies, caching, database optimization, connection pooling
- **Heroku Connect with Salesforce** — Bi-directional data sync, mapping, conflict resolution, Salesforce integration patterns
- **Heroku Enterprise Features** — Private Spaces, VPN peering, dedicated resources, Heroku Shield, multi-region failover
