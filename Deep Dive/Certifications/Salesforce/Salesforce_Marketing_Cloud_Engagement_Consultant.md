## About

The Salesforce Certified Marketing Cloud Engagement Consultant certification validates expertise in designing, implementing, and optimizing marketing solutions across all Salesforce Marketing Cloud channels — email, mobile push, SMS, social, and advertising. This Professional-level credential is for experienced marketing architects, consultants, and strategic practitioners who advise on multi-channel campaign strategy, subscriber data architecture, and customer engagement workflows. Requires foundational Salesforce Administrator knowledge; distinguishes practitioners capable of owning end-to-end marketing transformation projects.

## Topics covered

Exam blueprint breakdown (per Salesforce Trailhead):

- **Discovery and Solution Design** (~12%) — stakeholder alignment, requirements gathering, roadmap planning, business case development
- **Marketing Cloud Capabilities** (~26%) — platform architecture, multi-channel orchestration (email, SMS, push, social), feature differentiation
- **Email Sends** (~14%) — advanced email configuration, deliverability optimization, compliance frameworks, inbox placement
- **Subscriber Data Management** (~16%) — data extension modeling, attribute organization, profile segmentation, identity resolution
- **Set up and Configure Marketing Cloud** (~16%) — account structures, user roles, security, integrations, testing protocols
- **Automation and Optimization** (~16%) — Journey Builder advanced patterns, Automation Studio, A/B testing, performance analytics, personalization engines

## Skills validated

*Concrete competencies this exam specifically measures.*

- Multi-channel marketing architecture and strategy across email, SMS, push, social, and advertising
- Advanced data extension design, attribute modeling, and subscriber profile segmentation
- Journey Builder workflow design, automation triggers, and decision-tree logic
- Email optimization: deliverability tuning, compliance implementation, inbox placement
- Automation Studio: scheduled campaigns, triggered sends, data jobs, and batch orchestration
- Integration patterns: API connectivity, CRM sync, first-party data ingestion, CDP alignment
- A/B testing methodology, statistical significance, and performance analytics
- Business case development, ROI modeling, and stakeholder communication
- Governance and best-practice documentation for multi-team marketing operations
- Personalization at scale: dynamic content, real-time behavioral triggers, predictive sends
