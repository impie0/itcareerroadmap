## About

The Salesforce Certified Sharing and Visibility Architect (formerly titled Sharing and Visibility Designer) is a specialist expert-level credential that validates deep expertise in designing and implementing security governance, data access control, and visibility models at enterprise scale on the Salesforce platform. The exam tests both declarative (org-wide defaults, role hierarchies, sharing rules, field-level security) and programmatic (sharing API, apex-driven sharing, platform events) approaches to controlling who can see and modify what data.

Introduced as a standalone credential in the mid-2010s and rebranded as "Architect" in 2024 to reflect its strategic scope, this certification is **required for both Application Architect and System Architect designations**—making it a mandatory building block toward Salesforce Certified Technical Architect (CTA), the platform's highest credential. Organizations depend on Sharing and Visibility Architects to enforce compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, FedRAMP), prevent data leakage, optimize query performance in large multi-tenant environments, and design secure customer/partner communities. The scope spans traditional org-wide defaults through advanced patterns like Communities sharing, Territory Management at scale, and external object visibility.

## Topics covered

Per the official Salesforce exam blueprint, the Sharing and Visibility Architect exam emphasizes:

- **Declarative Sharing (~30%):** Org-wide defaults, role hierarchies, sharing rules, criteria-based sharing, manual sharing, public groups, queues, team sharing
- **Programmatic Sharing (~30%):** Apex sharing API, sObject sharing classes, batch sharing operations, programmatic evaluation of sharing rules, governor limits in sharing contexts
- **Performance and Scale (~15%):** Impact of sharing rules on query performance and SOQL optimization, bulk sharing operations, audit considerations, monitoring sharing rule performance
- **Communities and External Visibility (~10%):** Community user sharing, guest user access, external object visibility, partner portal data governance, account-based access control
- **Enterprise Territory Management (~15%):** Territory hierarchies, record assignment, territory allocation rules, performance at scale with large territory structures

## Skills validated

- **Declarative sharing models** — Org-wide defaults, role hierarchies, sharing rules (standard & criteria-based), public groups, team sharing, manual sharing
- **Programmatic sharing via Apex** — Sharing API (`SObjectShare` classes), batch sharing operations, dynamic SOQL for shared records, governor limits in sharing contexts
- **Field-level security (FLS)** — Field accessibility control, encryption, masked field hiding, encrypted field sharing
- **Object-level security** — CRUD permissions, FLS interaction with sharing rules, read-only vs. read-write visibility
- **Territory Management** — Territory hierarchies, territory allocation rules, performance tuning in large territory structures
- **Communities and external visibility** — Community user sharing models, guest user access, external object visibility, partner portals, customer clouds
- **Performance optimization** — Query optimization under complex sharing rules, bulk operations, monitoring, audit trail usage
- **Compliance and governance** — GDPR data access logging, HIPAA audit controls, FedRAMP data residency, tenant isolation
- **Data leakage prevention** — Sensitive record visibility design, preventing over-sharing, audit patterns
- **Enterprise-scale architecture** — Sharing rule complexity at 10M+ records, relationship cardinality impact, data retention with shared records
