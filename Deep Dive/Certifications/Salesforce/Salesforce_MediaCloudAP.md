## About

The Salesforce Media Cloud Accredited Professional credential demonstrates expertise in planning, designing, and implementing Media Cloud solutions that deliver business value to media and advertising organizations. Certified professionals have knowledge, skills, and experience in leveraging Media Cloud capabilities to manage advertising operations, audience management, and media sales workflows.

This certification is exclusively available for Salesforce partners and is designed for consultants, solutions architects, and implementation specialists who focus on media and advertising industry solutions and help organizations optimize their media operations, sales processes, and customer relationships.

## Domain context

Media and advertising organizations face distinctive operational challenges including:

- Media sales and order management
- Advertising campaign management
- Audience segmentation and targeting
- Ad inventory management
- Revenue recognition and billing
- Advertiser and publisher relationship management
- Proposal and contract management
- Real-time reporting and analytics
- Cross-platform audience insights
- Programmatic advertising integration
- Content and asset management

Media Cloud provides industry-specific functionality built on Salesforce to address these requirements. This certification validates the ability to architect and implement comprehensive media industry solutions.

## Topics covered

The certification exam covers the following major topic areas:

1. **Media Cloud Fundamentals** (10%)
   - Media Cloud overview and capabilities
   - Media industry terminology
   - Advertising and publishing business models
   - Salesforce Media Cloud value proposition

2. **Media Industry Processes & Requirements** (15%)
   - Media sales workflows
   - Advertising operations
   - Proposal and contract management
   - Audience and targeting strategies
   - Inventory management

3. **Media Cloud Architecture & Data Model** (15%)
   - Media Cloud data model
   - Industry-specific objects
   - Hierarchies and relationships
   - Integration architecture
   - Custom extensions

4. **Sales & Order Management** (20%)
   - Media sales configuration
   - Opportunity and quote management
   - Order and contract management
   - Line item management
   - Approval workflows

5. **Advertising & Campaign Management** (20%)
   - Campaign setup and management
   - Creative and asset management
   - Media placements and trafficking
   - Audience segmentation and targeting
   - Campaign performance tracking

6. **Analytics & Reporting** (10%)
   - Media dashboards
   - Revenue reporting
   - Campaign analytics
   - Audience insights
   - Business intelligence

7. **Integration & Administration** (10%)
   - System integration
   - Data management
   - Security and permissions
   - User adoption and training
   - Implementation best practices
