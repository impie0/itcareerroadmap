## About

The Salesforce Certified Administrator (ADM 201) is the baseline professional credential for Salesforce platform administration. Launched in 2013 and continuously updated with each Salesforce release, it validates configuration, customization, and management skills on the core Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud. Recommended for IT professionals transitioning to CRM administration, customer success engineers, and business analysts supporting Salesforce implementations. No prerequisites; self-study via Trailhead is the primary preparation path.

## Topics covered

Salesforce publishes an official exam blueprint. Study approximately these domain weights:

- **Configuration and Setup (20%)** — Organization setup, user accounts, licenses, security model, password policies, IP restrictions.
- **Object Manager and Lightning App Builder (20%)** — Custom objects, fields, relationships, page layouts, Lightning App Builder, responsive design.
- **Sales and Marketing Applications (12%)** — Leads, accounts, contacts, opportunities, forecasting, sales processes, campaigns.
- **Service and Support Applications (11%)** — Cases, service processes, knowledge articles, entitlements, service channels.
- **Productivity and Collaboration (7%)** — Chatter, Einstein Activity Capture, Files, collaboration features.
- **Data and Analytics Management (14%)** — Reports, dashboards, Einstein Analytics (Tableau CRM), data import/export, data quality.
- **Workflow and Process Automation (16%)** — Flow (Cloud Flows), Process Builder, workflow rules, approvals, automation edge cases.

## Skills validated

*What the ADM 201 exam specifically tests, distinct from shared Associate-level CRM skills.*

- **Org setup and governance** — Password policies, IP whitelisting, org-wide sharing defaults, delegated administration.
- **User and license management** — License types, seat assignments, profile-based provisioning, permission sets, role hierarchy.
- **Object and field customization** — Custom objects, field types (text, picklist, lookup, formula, rollup summary), field dependencies.
- **Page layout and component design** — Lightning page layouts, compact layouts, mobile card layouts, related lists, list views.
- **Lightning App Builder** — App creation, Lightning components, utility bar, custom components.
- **Sales Cloud configuration** — Lead management, account/contact records, opportunity stages, forecasting, price books.
- **Service Cloud configuration** — Case management, service processes, queues, response times, entitlements.
- **Reports and dashboards** — Report types, filters, grouping, dashboard components, Einstein Analytics (Tableau CRM).
- **Data management** — Bulk API, Data Loader, CSV import, duplicate management, data validation rules.
- **Flow automation** — Screen flows, decision logic, subflows, Salesforce-to-Salesforce sync.
- **Process Builder and Workflow** — Workflow rules, immediate actions, scheduled actions, approval processes.
- **Security and sharing** — Organization-wide sharing, role hierarchies, sharing rules, manual sharing, sharing sets.
- **Compliance and audit** — Org setup audit trail, field audit trail, login forensics, GDPR/CCPA features.
