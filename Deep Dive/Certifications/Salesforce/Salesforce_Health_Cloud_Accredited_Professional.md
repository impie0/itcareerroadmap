## About

The Salesforce Health Cloud Accredited Professional certification validates expertise in implementing and managing Salesforce Health Cloud solutions for healthcare providers and payers. This professional-level credential is designed for consultants, administrators, and architects who have hands-on experience configuring Health Cloud to meet healthcare organizations' specific needs. Candidates should have 2–3 years of healthcare industry experience and demonstrated competency in Health Cloud data models, compliance, and care coordination workflows. The credential is heavily focused on practical implementation rather than foundational knowledge.

## Domain context — Healthcare/Industries (Salesforce Ecosystem)

Salesforce Health Cloud is a purpose-built vertical solution for the healthcare sector, separate from general Sales Cloud or Service Cloud. This certification sits within Salesforce's broader ecosystem of industry-specific clouds (Financial Services Cloud, Public Sector Cloud, Manufacturing Cloud, etc.) but is distinct in its healthcare compliance and data governance focus.

## Topics covered

The exam is organized around three main domain areas with published weightings:

- **Health Cloud Basics (20%)** — Health Cloud architecture, data model overview, org setup, HIPAA compliance fundamentals
- **Patient and Member Management (20%)** — Patient object, member records, account hierarchies, care teams, household structures
- **Integration with Systems (20%)** — HL7/FHIR standards, EHR integration patterns, third-party system connectors, data mapping
- **Care Coordination (15%)** — Care plans, goals/outcomes, action items, team-based care workflows, referral management
- **Data Management and Compliance (15%)** — HIPAA security rules, encryption, field-level security, audit trails, data retention
- **Analytics and Insights (10%)** — Tableau dashboards, Health Cloud-specific analytics, reporting on patient outcomes and provider performance

*Source:* [Salesforce Health Cloud Accredited Professional Exam Guide ↗](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=sf.exam_health_cloud_accredited_professional.htm)
