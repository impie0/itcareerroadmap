## About

The Salesforce Certified Marketing Cloud Account Engagement Consultant credential validates expertise in designing and implementing Account Engagement (formerly Pardot) solutions that align with customer business requirements. This professional-level cert builds directly on the Specialist credential, requiring 12+ months of hands-on experience with the Account Engagement Lightning App and Salesforce Platform. Consultants demonstrate the ability to architect scalable, maintainable solutions and translate business strategy into automation, lead management, and reporting frameworks. The cert was relaunched in 2021 following Salesforce's rebrand of Pardot as Account Engagement.

---

## Domain context — Marketing/CRM

B2B marketing automation, customer engagement platforms, and Salesforce ecosystem marketing tools. Account Engagement is Salesforce's dedicated platform for multi-touch attribution, lead scoring, account-based marketing, and sales-marketing alignment in complex B2B environments.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint distributes 60 scored questions across eight domains, with the following weight distribution:

- **Account Configuration & Setup** (20%) — account settings, users, roles, permissions, Sender Authentication Package (SAP), data retention policies
- **Automating Business Processes** (17%) — Automation Studio, Journey Builder, drip campaigns, triggered sends, conditional logic, dynamic content
- **Evaluation & Strategy** (17%) — analyzing customer use cases, mapping business goals to features, recommending KPIs, designing account engagement strategies
- **Lead Management & Qualification** (14%) — lead scoring, qualification, grading, prospect segmentation, lifecycle stages
- **Email Marketing** (10%) — email templates, A/B testing, send-time optimization, deliverability, compliance (CAN-SPAM, GDPR)
- **Reporting, Metrics & Analytics** (11%) — custom reports, dashboards, engagement metrics, ROI analysis, Salesforce integration reporting
- **Personalizing the Prospect Experience** (8%) — dynamic content, personalization tags, AMPscript fundamentals, progressive profiling
- **Sales Emails & Alerts** (3%) — Sales Engage (email tools within Salesforce), sales alert configuration

*Source:* [Salesforce Exam Blueprint ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/marketingcloudaccountengagementconsultant)
