## About

The Salesforce Certified Application Architect designation is an expert-level credential awarded to professionals who have demonstrated mastery of complex application design, data architecture, platform development, and security patterns on the Salesforce platform. Unlike a single-exam certification, it is automatically granted upon successful completion of four separate expert-track certifications. Application Architects design scalable, enterprise-grade solutions that balance business requirements, technical constraints, and Salesforce best practices. The credential is valued across consulting, systems integration, and enterprise customer success roles.

## Four prerequisite certifications (designation components)

The Application Architect designation is earned by completing **all four** of these expert-level exams:

### 1. **Salesforce Certified Platform Developer I (PDI)**

Validates expertise in Apex, Visualforce, Lightning Web Components, and custom development on Force.com.

- **Topics:** Apex programming, triggers, asynchronous processing, Lightning development, testing, deployment
- **Cost:** USD $400
- **Official guide:** [Platform Developer I ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/platformdeveloper_i)

### 2. **Salesforce Certified Platform App Builder**

Validates declarative customization skills — configuration without code.

- **Topics:** Custom objects, fields, relationships, automation (flows, workflows), security, UI/UX design, schema design
- **Cost:** USD $400
- **Official guide:** [Platform App Builder ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/platformappbuilder)

### 3. **Salesforce Certified Platform Data Architect**

Validates expertise in enterprise data modeling, governance, and high-performance design.

- **Topics:** Data models, role hierarchies, sharing mechanisms, large data volume (LDV) patterns, data migration, archival strategies
- **Cost:** USD $400
- **Official guide:** [Platform Data Architect ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/dataarchitect)
- **Recommended experience:** 2–3 years Salesforce platform experience

### 4. **Salesforce Certified Sharing and Visibility Architect**

Validates expertise in security, sharing models, and access control.

- **Topics:** Role hierarchies, record sharing, field-level security, org-wide defaults, encryption, identity and access management
- **Cost:** USD $400
- **Official guide:** [Sharing and Visibility Architect ↗](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/credentials/sharingvisibilityarchitect)

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what these four exams collectively test.*

- **Apex programming** and asynchronous patterns (triggers, batch jobs, future methods, queueable interfaces)
- **Lightning Web Components** and Visualforce development
- **Declarative customization** — custom objects, flows, workflow rules, process builder
- **Data modeling** for enterprise scale (large data volumes, archival, migration)
- **Role hierarchies** and record-sharing mechanisms
- **Field-level and object-level security**
- **Identity and access management** (SSO, OAuth, SAML)
- **Integration patterns** and API design
- **Testing and deployment** lifecycle
- **Performance optimization** and monitoring
- **Org-wide defaults** and sharing rules
- **Encryption** (at-rest and in-transit)
- **Audit trail** and compliance design
