## About

The Salesforce Certified Nonprofit Cloud Consultant credential demonstrates expertise in designing and implementing the Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud Industry solution to meet business requirements for nonprofit organizations. Certified professionals have hands-on experience with Nonprofit Success Pack (NPSP) and understand how to leverage the Salesforce platform to address the unique needs of nonprofit enterprises.

This certification is designed for consultants, solutions architects, and implementation specialists who focus on nonprofit organizations and want to validate their expertise in delivering value through the Nonprofit Cloud.

## Domain context

Nonprofit organizations face distinct operational challenges including donor management, grant tracking, program management, volunteer coordination, and complex financial requirements. The Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud provides industry-specific functionality built on the Salesforce platform to address these needs. This certification validates the ability to:

- Understand nonprofit business processes and requirements
- Design solutions using Nonprofit Cloud capabilities
- Implement NPSP and related nonprofit-specific features
- Integrate Nonprofit Cloud with other Salesforce clouds (Sales Cloud, Service Cloud)
- Manage data and configuration for nonprofit operations
- Address compliance and reporting requirements

## Topics covered

The certification exam covers the following major topic areas:

1. **Nonprofit Cloud Architecture & Components** (15%)
   - NPSP features and capabilities
   - Account and contact hierarchies for nonprofits
   - Record types and custom objects

2. **Program and Engagement Management** (20%)
   - Program setup and tracking
   - Campaign management for nonprofits
   - Engagement records and tracking

3. **Fundraising & Donor Management** (25%)
   - Donation tracking and management
   - Recurring donation configuration
   - Relationship mapping
   - Prospect identification and qualification

4. **Grants & Sponsorships** (15%)
   - Grant management functionality
   - Grant applications and tracking
   - Sponsorship management

5. **Data Model & Customization** (15%)
   - Custom objects and fields
   - Relationships and lookups
   - Data architecture for nonprofits

6. **Integration & Administration** (10%)
   - Integration considerations
   - Security and access control
   - Data management and maintenance
   - Reporting and analytics
