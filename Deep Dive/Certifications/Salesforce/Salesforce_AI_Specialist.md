## About

Salesforce Certified Agentforce Specialist (AI-201) is a newer, high-impact certification launched in 2024 that validates skills in designing and managing AI agents within Salesforce's Agentforce platform. It is distinct from (and newer than) the general Salesforce Administrator or Developer certs — it focuses specifically on generative AI, prompt engineering, agentic reasoning, and Salesforce's proprietary **Atlas** reasoning engine. The cert is **not** a prerequisite pathway but rather a **specialization add-on** for admins, developers, and consultants who wish to pivot into AI-driven solution design. The certification's 1-year renewal cycle reflects the rapid cadence of Salesforce AI feature releases (3 major updates annually). As of May 2026, this cert signals early-adopter expertise in a high-demand niche, with salary premiums of $8K–$12K observed for entry-level roles and $20K–$30K+ for architects and senior consultants.

## Topics covered

*Exam blueprint (5 core areas, roughly equal weighting):*

- **Prompt Engineering** — Prompt Builder syntax, template management, grounding strategies (retrieval, Data Cloud, custom logic), token optimization, context windowing.
- **Agentforce Concepts & Architecture** — Agentforce topology, Atlas reasoning engine, standard topics (FAQ, Guided Engagement, Service, etc.), custom topics, deterministic constraints (filters, variables), sandbox-to-production deployment.
- **Agent Actions & Integrations** — Standard actions (create record, send email, etc.), custom actions, action parameter mapping, integration with Flows and external APIs, multi-agent orchestration.
- **Data Cloud Integration** — Grounding agents with Data Cloud profiles, segments, identity graphs, and calculated insights; Agentforce Data Library for response enrichment.
- **Generative AI in Sales & Service Cloud** — Einstein Generative AI setup (Lead Scoring, Sales Emails, Call Summaries), Agentforce Sales Agents (SDR, Coach), Agentforce Service Agents, Einstein Service Replies, governance, consent, and trust layer enforcement.

## Skills validated

*Concrete, testable capabilities this exam assesses — distinct from general "associate AI/CRM skills."*

- Designing and architecting multi-agent systems using Agentforce (agent routing, context switching, handoff logic).
- Authoring effective prompts in Salesforce Prompt Builder — including grounding strategy selection (retrieval-augmented generation, Data Cloud, custom logic).
- Configuring standard and custom topics, actions, and topic routing logic.
- Integrating Agentforce with Data Cloud for response personalization and fact-grounding.
- Implementing Einstein Trust Layer compliance (consent, PII masking, audit logging).
- Building and testing agents in sandbox; deploying to production with change set or package.
- Configuring Einstein Generative AI features in Sales Cloud (Lead Scoring, Sales Emails) and Service Cloud (Service Replies, Summaries).
- Troubleshooting agent reasoning failures and interpreting Atlas execution logs.
- Selecting and mapping standard actions (create/update/delete records, send email, approve, etc.) to agent workflows.
- Understanding Model Context Protocol (MCP) for multi-agent interoperability and external system integration.
