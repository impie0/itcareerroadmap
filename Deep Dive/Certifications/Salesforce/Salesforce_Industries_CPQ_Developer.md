# Salesforce Certified Industries CPQ Developer

**`Industries-CPQ-Developer`** · ● **Active** · **Professional** · _Salesforce_

> Certified Industries CPQ Developers understand how to develop Industries CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) applications for Salesforce's Communications, Media, and Energy & Utilities Clouds. The certification validates expertise in building industry-specific product catalog configurations, pricing strategies, and quoting workflows. This is the developer-focused counterpart to the CPQ Administrator certification, emphasizing advanced technical implementation using Apex, LWC, and Industries-specific APIs.

## About

The Industries CPQ Developer certification was introduced as Salesforce repositioned Vlocity (acquired in 2019) as its native **Industries Cloud** offering. It validates expertise in building CPQ solutions specifically for vertical industries—telecommunications, media, utilities, insurance, and energy—where complex multi-site quoting, bundle configurations, and industry-specific pricing rules are critical.

Unlike the CPQ Administrator (which focuses on configuration and setup), the Industries CPQ Developer certification targets developers who code integrations, build advanced product configuration rules, and implement Industry Cloud features. As of 2025–2026, this is an **active** credential with 3-year validity and mandatory maintenance modules.

Holders demonstrate hands-on proficiency in Industries data models, multi-site ordering, cart-based APIs, digital commerce, dynamic pricing, and asset-based configuration.

## Domain context — Industries/CPQ (Salesforce ecosystem)

Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) is a specialized subdomain within enterprise sales automation, focusing on complex product-service bundling, dynamic pricing, and quoting workflows. Industries CPQ is the Salesforce variant, built atop the **Industries Cloud** (formerly Vlocity Cloud), serving telecommunications, energy, utilities, insurance, and communications sectors where quoted deals often involve multi-site deployments, usage-based pricing, and regulatory compliance.

## Topics covered

The exam validates hands-on knowledge across these domains (based on official Trailhead learning paths and recent candidate reports):

- **Industries CPQ Architecture & Data Model** — product hierarchy, product bundles, attributes, and object relationships
- **Product Configuration** — identifying concepts required to configure products, product bundles, object type hierarchies, and pricing-driven attributes
- **Pricing & Promotions** — pricing rules, attribute-based pricing, discount strategies, promotion logic, and pricing adjustment techniques
- **Quoting & Order Workflows** — quote generation, multi-site quoting, quote-to-order workflows, and order processing
- **Document Generation** — output document templates and rule-driven document assembly
- **Cart and Digital Commerce APIs** — approximately 20% of the exam; method names, arguments, response values, and API-driven cart operations
- **Multi-site Quoting** — single and multiple group structures, multi-site workflow definition (critical for Telco & Utilities verticals)
- **Troubleshooting** — debugging product, pricing, promotion, rules, and API call failures
- **Industries Cloud Verticals** — Communications (telecom), Media, Energy & Utilities, and Insurance Cloud context and use cases
- **OmniStudio Integration** — integration with OmniScripts, FlexCards, and DataRaptors for enhanced UX in CPQ workflows
