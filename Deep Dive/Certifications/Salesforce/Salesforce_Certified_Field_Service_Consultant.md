## About

The Salesforce Certified Field Service Consultant validates expertise in implementing, administering, and optimizing Salesforce Field Service Lightning solutions. This professional-level certification targets consultants and practitioners who deploy field service workflows, manage scheduling and resource allocation, and configure work order processes for organizations requiring mobile field operations. Certification demonstrates hands-on proficiency with FSL features released through 2025, including optimization engines, mobile app configuration, and asset management integrations.

---

## Domain context — Field Service Management

Field service spans configuration and operational management of mobile workforce, work-order routing, scheduling optimization, resource allocation, and customer asset lifecycle tracking. As a Salesforce specialization within the broader Service Cloud ecosystem, FSL candidates must first master foundational cloud CRM administration before specializing in field logistics.

## Topics covered

- **Manage Resources (22%)** — Service resources, crews, territories, territory members, skills and proficiency levels, operating hours, availability patterns
- **Manage Work Orders (17%)** — Work order configuration, work types, line items, status management, products and pricing, resource preferences
- **Configure Optimization (16%)** — Optimization settings, travel time estimation, arrival windows, service territory overlap, overnight stops
- **Configure Mobile (12%)** — Field service mobile app settings, offline capabilities, user experience customization, crew management
- **Configure Maintenance Plans & Inventory (13%)** — Maintenance plan generation, work order creation cycles, asset tracking, inventory models, return orders
- **Manage Service Appointments (13%)** — Appointment scheduling, slot management, service resource assignment, arrival time windows, appointment status
- **Configuration & Administration (7%)** — Permissions and sharing, field service settings, custom objects, picklist management
