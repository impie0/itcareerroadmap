## About

The Salesforce Certified Marketing Cloud Engagement Consultant certification validates expertise in implementing, designing, and managing multi-channel customer engagement campaigns using Salesforce Marketing Cloud. This professional-level credential is held by solutions architects, consultants, and senior marketing technologists who design complex Marketing Cloud solutions for enterprise clients. The certification requires demonstrated hands-on experience with email marketing, automation, segmentation, data modeling, and multi-cloud Salesforce integrations. It is recognized as an architect-equivalent credential for marketing technology implementation, bridging consultant-level knowledge with solution design responsibilities.

## Topics covered

- **Discovery and Requirements Definition (20%)** — Determining customer needs, gathering business requirements, recommending appropriate Marketing Cloud products and solutions, designing implementation approaches, and planning data architecture for long-term success.

- **Architecture and Integration Design (20%)** — Designing multi-cloud solutions integrating Marketing Cloud with Service Cloud, Commerce Cloud, and external systems; defining data models and segmentation strategies; planning for scalability and compliance.

- **Functional Capabilities and Business Value (18%)** — Demonstrating knowledge of Email Studio, Journey Builder, Automation Studio, segmentation, personalization, content creation, and best practices for multi-channel campaigns.

- **Data Management at Scale (22%)** — Designing data models, managing customer data platforms, implementing audience segmentation, data mapping, ETL patterns, and performance optimization for large data volumes.

- **Migration, Optimization, and Troubleshooting (20%)** — Planning migrations from legacy email systems, optimizing existing campaigns and automations, troubleshooting deployment issues, monitoring performance, and recommending enhancements.
