## About

The Sophos Certified Engineer certification suite validates hands-on expertise in configuring, deploying, and managing Sophos endpoint protection and firewall solutions. Offered through Sophos Academy with free or low-cost partner training, this cert has become the de facto standard in SMB and MSP security operations — particularly for teams managing small-to-medium fleets of endpoints or multi-branch firewalls. The certification is **not vendor-neutral** (unlike CompTIA Security+ or CEH) but carries significant weight in Sophos partner ecosystems, job postings, and career progression toward Sophos-focused security engineering or MSP leadership roles. Active since the Sophos XG generation and regularly updated for current product versions (e.g., Central Endpoint v3.x, Firewall v20+).

## Topics covered

### ET15 (Sophos Central Endpoint)

- Deployment modes (cloud vs. on-premise)
- Endpoint protection features (Intercept X, EDR/XDR)
- Policy and group management
- Threat detection and incident response
- Integration with Central admin console
- Live response and forensics capabilities

### ET80 (Sophos Firewall / XGS)

- Firewall deployment modes and licensing
- Zone and rule configuration
- NAT, port forwarding, and VPN setup
- IPSec and SSL/TLS VPN protocols
- Intrusion prevention (IPS) policies
- Web filtering and content control
- High availability (HA) failover fundamentals
- Central management and orchestration
- Advanced routing (BGP/OSPF for Architect track)

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests, distinct from shared "Common skills" above.*

- **Sophos Central endpoint deployment** and policy configuration (Intercept X layers)
- **Threat detection workflow** (behavioral analysis, sandboxing, live response)
- **EDR/MDR operational practices** (event investigation, remediation, forensic data interpretation)
- **Sophos Firewall rule logic** (zones, policies, logging, implicit-deny architecture)
- **VPN setup and troubleshooting** (IPSec, SSL/TLS, split tunneling, dial-up scenarios)
- **IPS policy tuning** (signature management, false-positive reduction, evasion techniques)
- **High-availability firewall failover** (synchronized state, license management, failback)
- **Sophos Central management console** (deployment, reporting, dashboards, API basics)
- **Incident response using Sophos tools** (alert investigation, containment via Central, evidence collection)
