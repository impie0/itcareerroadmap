## About

The Sophos Certified Architect certification validates expert-level skills in designing, deploying, and managing advanced Sophos endpoint and firewall architectures at enterprise and multi-site scale. Unlike the Professional-level Engineer cert, the Architect tier focuses on infrastructure design, high availability, complex network topology support, and strategic security orchestration across multiple sites and product families. Available through Sophos Academy with free or tiered partner training, this certification has become the gold standard for channel partners pursuing solutions architect roles, pre-sales engineering, and senior technical leadership in Sophos-centric organizations. The certification split into two tracks—AT15 (Central Endpoint) and AT80 (Firewall XGS)—reflects Sophos's modular product strategy; both are commonly held together by architects managing integrated endpoint-and-firewall deployments. Active since the Sophos XG generation and regularly updated for current product versions (SFOS 20.x+, Central v3.x+).

## Topics covered

### AT80 (Sophos Firewall Architect / XGS)

- **Advanced Firewall Architecture & Design** — multi-site network topology, DMZ design, branch/hub-spoke models, failover planning
- **High Availability (HA) Clustering** — active-passive stateful, active-active, license management, state synchronization, failback strategies
- **Advanced Routing Protocols** — OSPF, BGP, VRF, redistribution, multi-site routing design
- **Multi-Firewall Management** — Sophos Central orchestration, Sophos Firewall Manager (SFM) deployment, policy distribution, reporting aggregation
- **Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA) Architecture** — Sophos SecureConnect design, identity-centric access control, micro-segmentation patterns
- **SD-WAN with SD-RED** — integration with Sophos firewall, hybrid cloud routing, application prioritization
- **Advanced WAF & Application Control** — custom policy design, evasion technique handling, rule tuning at scale
- **Sandstorm Cloud Sandboxing** — integration, threat detonation workflows, containment orchestration
- **Synchronized Security** — endpoint-to-firewall threat correlation, response automation, Central ecosystem integration
- **Enterprise Email Security** — Sophos Email at scale, integration with Firewall and Central, advanced threat filtering
- **Cloud Optix (CSPM)** — cloud infrastructure security, compliance automation, Sophos Central integration
- **Complex Network Migrations** — legacy UTM9/SG migration, phased rollouts, coexistence strategies, data center consolidation
- **Reporting & Analytics** — advanced Central dashboards, compliance reporting, threat analytics, SLA/KPI design

### AT15 (Sophos Central Endpoint Architect)

- **Enterprise Endpoint Deployment Models** — cloud-only, hybrid, on-premise, large-scale fleet management
- **Intercept X Advanced EDR/XDR Architecture** — behavioral analysis tuning, threat hunting workflows, response automation
- **MDR Integration & Orchestration** — Sophos Managed Detection and Response workflows, escalation, SLA design
- **Policy Architecture at Scale** — group design, inheritance models, exception handling, multi-tenant scenarios
- **Live Response & Forensics** — investigation workflows, threat artifact collection, evidence chain for incident response
- **Central Admin Console at Enterprise Scale** — multi-site admin delegation, reporting dashboards, API integrations
- **Third-party Integrations** — SIEM integration (Splunk, Azure Sentinel), automation platforms, threat intelligence feeds
- **Compliance & Audit** — reporting for regulatory frameworks, audit trail design, evidence collection
- **Threat Response Orchestration** — cross-product response (firewall + endpoint), containment strategies, eradication planning
