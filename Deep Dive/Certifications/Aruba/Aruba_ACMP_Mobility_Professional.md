## About

The Aruba Certified Mobility Professional (ACMP) validated expertise in designing, deploying, and managing enterprise wireless networks (WLAN) using Aruba/HPE solutions, specifically the AOS8 operating system. The exam tested advanced configuration of Mobility Master controllers, access point deployment, RF planning, security protocols (PSK, 802.1X), ClearPass integration, roaming, VoWiFi, and troubleshooting. ACMP was a Professional-level cert requiring ACMA as a prerequisite (or equivalent vendor certs). The certification was phased out on May 1, 2023, and replaced by the broader [Aruba Certified Professional-Campus Access (ACP-Campus Access, HPE7-A01)](../../Vendors/HPE_Aruba_Vendor_Overview.md), which covers wired, wireless, and security convergence.

## Topics covered

Per official exam blueprint for HPE6-A71 (AOS8 v8.x):

- **Mobility Master & Controller Architecture** — cluster setup, redundancy, failover, high availability
- **Access Point Deployment** — AP modes (Local, Tunneled, MultiZone), RF planning, site survey, interference mitigation
- **WLAN Security** — PSK / WPA2 / WPA3, 802.1X authentication, RADIUS integration, ClearPass policy server
- **Advanced Roaming** — seamless roaming, fast roaming (802.11r/k), band steering, client troubleshooting
- **Guest Networks & Segmentation** — captive portal, VLAN assignment, policy enforcement
- **Voice over WiFi (VoWiFi)** — QoS, codec support, handoff optimization
- **Remote Access** — Remote Access Point (RAP), Virtual Intelligent Access Point (VIA), VPN tunneling
- **Monitoring & Analytics** — AirWave (legacy), Aruba Central dashboards, health metrics, troubleshooting
- **Advanced Topics** — MultiZone networks, Tunneled Node for wired switch management, encryption, certificate management
- **Troubleshooting** — AP connectivity, roaming issues, security validation, controller debugging

*Source:* [Certification & Learning: ACMP Datasheet ↗](https://certification-learning.hpe.com/tr/datasheet/certification/Aruba-ACMP) / [Official Study Guide (Steven Sowell) ↗](https://hpepress.hpe.com/product/Aruba+Certified+Mobility+Professional+Official+Certification+Study+Guide+HPE6-A44+HPE6-A71-eBook-18366)

## Skills validated

*What this exam specifically tested — concrete technologies, not general domain skills.*

- AOS8 architecture (Mobility Master, Controllers, access points)
- Mobility Master configuration: clustering, HA, licensing, backup/restore
- Access Point modes: Local, Tunneled, MultiZone
- RF planning and site surveys (channels, power, throughput modeling)
- PSK networks (SSID, pre-shared keys, WPA2/WPA3)
- 802.1X authentication (RADIUS integration, certificate-based auth)
- ClearPass Policy Server (endpoint profiling, guest onboarding)
- Advanced roaming (802.11r, 802.11k, band steering)
- Voice over WiFi: codec selection, roaming delay, QoS
- Remote Access Point (RAP) and VIA (Virtual IAP) tunneling
- Guest networks and VLAN assignment
- AirWave and Aruba Central monitoring/troubleshooting
- API scripting for automation (basic)
- Log aggregation and syslog parsing
