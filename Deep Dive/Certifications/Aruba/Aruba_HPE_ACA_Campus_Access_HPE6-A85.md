## About

The HPE Aruba Networking Certified Associate — Campus Access (ACA, exam code HPE6-A85) is an entry-to-associate level certification validating foundational knowledge and hands-on skills in deploying, configuring, and managing secure wired and wireless campus access networks using Aruba and HPE Aruba Networking solutions. Launched as part of HPE's post-merger (July 2025) re-branding of the Aruba certification pathway, the ACA serves as the entry credential for IT professionals beginning careers in enterprise campus access and mobile-first networking. It is a prerequisite for or natural stepping stone to the Aruba Certified Professional (ACP) Campus Access (HPE7-A01) and other advanced Aruba track certifications (Mobility, Advanced Switching).

## Domain context — Networking

Vendor-specific enterprise campus and wireless networking via HPE Aruba Networking platforms — switches (AOS-CX), access points, gateways, policy engines (ClearPass), and management planes (Aruba Central). Distinct from core routing/security; focused on LAN/WLAN access, campus architecture, and user mobility.

## Topics covered

Exam blueprint (verified from official certification data sheet):

- **Networking Architectures & Technologies (15%)** — Campus network design, mobile-first architectures, switching and wireless fundamentals, AOS-CX platform overview, reference designs
- **Wired Campus Access — Switching & Routing (25%)** — AOS-CX switch configuration, basic VLANs, STP/RSTP, IP addressing and routing, VSX (Virtual Switching Extension), PoE management
- **Wireless Campus Access (25%)** — AP deployment and configuration, SSID creation, radio frequency (RF) fundamentals, antenna placement, mobility controllers, Instant On setup
- **Security Fundamentals (15%)** — 802.1X authentication, MAC Authentication Bypass (MAB), ACLs, VLAN-based segmentation, ClearPass basics, guest access
- **Management & Monitoring (10%)** — Aruba Central cloud management, device management and provisioning, basic troubleshooting, log monitoring, role-based access control (RBAC)
- **Policy & Optimization (10%)** — QoS principles, bandwidth management, traffic steering, guest VLAN management

*Source:* [Official ACA Certification Data Sheet ↗](https://certification-learning.hpe.com/tr/datasheet/certification/ACA-CamAcss?version=2)
