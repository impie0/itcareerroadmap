## About

The HPE Aruba Certified Expert (ACX) - Campus Access Mobility certification validates that a candidate possesses advanced knowledge and hands-on expertise in designing, deploying, and optimizing complex enterprise wired and wireless networking solutions. This expert-level certification is intended for senior network architects and engineers responsible for multi-site campus deployments, advanced security policies, and performance optimization. Following HPE's $14 billion acquisition of Juniper Networks (closed July 2025), the certification program underwent restructuring; both Aruba and Juniper product lines now operate under unified HPE Networking ownership, and cross-platform expertise is increasingly valued.

## Domain context — Networking / Wireless / Enterprise LAN-WAN

Enterprise campus networking across wired switching, wireless access points, controllers, and cloud-managed platforms (Aruba Central). Advanced topics include multi-vendor integration, security policy enforcement (ClearPass), SD-WAN, and emerging fabric architectures (EVPN-VXLAN).

## Topics covered

Expert-level knowledge spans:

- **Multi-site campus design** — network topology, redundancy, high availability
- **Edge Services Platform (ESP)** — unified policy and management across wired/wireless
- **Aruba Central** — cloud management platform, NetConductor fabric automation
- **ClearPass advanced** — role-based network access control, 802.1X, network segmentation
- **NetEdit** — workflow-driven network design and deployment
- **AOS-CX 10.x** — Aruba's modern switching OS, advanced features
- **Wireless RF expertise** — troubleshooting, site surveys, interference mitigation, high-density deployments
- **EVPN-VXLAN** — overlay fabric architectures, data center and campus interconnect
- **Mist AI integration** — post-Juniper, AI-driven network analytics and proactive troubleshooting (emerging in certification materials)
- **Security and compliance** — authentication, encryption, audit logging, guest access policies
- **Performance optimization** — QoS, traffic engineering, application acceleration
