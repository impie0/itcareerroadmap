## About

VCDX-NV is the highest level of VMware certification, validating enterprise-scale design expertise in network virtualization and NSX architecture. Unlike traditional exams, VCDX requires submission of a production design document (100–300 pages including diagrams), technical review by VMware experts, and a live panel defense where candidates present and justify architectural decisions to senior VMware-accredited architects. The certification path requires VCP-NV as prerequisite and typically takes 18–24 months to complete. VCDX-NV demonstrates world-class capability in designing secure, scalable, resilient NSX-based virtual networking solutions for complex enterprise environments.

---

## Domain context — Network Virtualization & Design

Network virtualization abstracts physical networking into software-defined layers, enabling dynamic, flexible infrastructure. VCDX-NV focuses on **design-level** expertise: translating business requirements into resilient, secure, scalable NSX architectures aligned to industry best practices.

---

## Topics covered

The VCDX-NV Defense evaluates the following core design areas:

- **Customer Requirements & Business Context** — Understanding client needs, constraints, scalability, security, and compliance drivers
- **Solution Architecture** — High-level design that maps requirements to NSX constructs (logical networks, distributed firewalls, gateways, service composition)
- **Engineering Specifications** — Detailed technical decisions: fabric topology, segmentation strategy, east–west security, failover/redundancy, performance tuning
- **Implementation Guidance** — Step-by-step deployment sequence, configuration, testing, and validation protocols
- **Risk Management & Resiliency** — Failure modes, disaster recovery, business continuity, network isolation, backup strategies
- **Technical Communication** — Clear documentation, diagrams (Visio, draw.io, or equivalent), and articulate defense of design choices

*Source:* [VCDX-NV Design Defense Preparation Guide ↗](https://docs.broadcom.com/doc/vmw-vcdx-nv-design-defense-exam-preparation-guide)
