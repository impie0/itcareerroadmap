## About

The VCAP-DCV Design certification validates your ability to architect and design complex VMware vSphere environments. This advanced credential requires you to apply design principles to develop conceptual, logical, and physical designs given customer requirements. Unlike foundational VCP certifications, VCAP-DCV Design emphasizes decision-making across compute, storage, networking, security, capacity planning, and disaster recovery. The exam is scenario-based and tests real-world design judgment. Broadcom (which acquired VMware) maintains this certification as an active, vendor-backed credential for infrastructure architects and senior virtualization engineers.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint topics (per Broadcom documentation):

- **Business and Technical Requirements** — Differentiate business vs. technical requirements; identify business factors affecting design; gather and analyze application and operational requirements
- **Design Principles** — Apply AMPRS (Availability, Manageability, Performance, Recovery, Security) framework; recognize VMware Validated Solutions use cases
- **Conceptual Design** — Create conceptual models; evaluate and document design assumptions
- **Logical Design** — Recognize key information for logical decisions; identify design options; create logical architecture from requirements
- **Physical Design** — Recognize physical design constraints; translate logical design to physical specifications; size infrastructure components
- **Manageability** — Capacity planning; scalability decisions; resource pooling and lifecycle management
- **Performance** — Cluster design; CPU, memory, and storage optimization; workload placement strategies
- **Availability** — Fault tolerance; high-availability (HA) design; business continuity objectives
- **Security** — RBAC design; network segmentation; vSphere security best practices
- **Recovery** — Backup and recovery strategies; RPO/RTO requirements; disaster recovery design

*Source:* [Broadcom exam guide ↗](https://docs.broadcom.com/doc/vmw-vcap-dcv-design-8x-exam-guide)
