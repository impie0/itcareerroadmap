## About

The VCAP-NV Design certification validates advanced expertise in designing and recommending VMware NSX solutions for enterprise network virtualization. This expert-level credential requires candidates to hold an active VCP-NV 2022 or current certification and tests practical design decision-making across NSX architecture, logical networking, security micro-segmentation, and network services. The exam is scenario-focused, emphasizing real-world design challenges rather than memorization, and is delivered through Pearson Professional Assessments. Current version (3V0-42.23) covers NSX 4.x.

---

## Domain context — Network Virtualization

Network virtualization through VMware NSX provides software-defined networking capabilities enabling micro-segmentation, distributed firewalls, logical switching and routing, load balancing, and advanced threat protection independent of physical network infrastructure. Expert-level designers combine NSX architecture knowledge with enterprise networking principles to build scalable, secure, resilient virtualized networks.
