## About

ENSDWI (Implementing Cisco SD-WAN Solutions) is a concentration exam for the CCNP Enterprise certification track. Launched in 2019 as part of Cisco's modernized CCNP redesign, it validates expertise in designing, deploying, configuring, and managing Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN solutions at enterprise scale. Candidates must hold or concurrently pass CCNP Enterprise Core (350-401 ENCOR) to earn the full CCNP Enterprise credential. ENSDWI is designed for mid-level network engineers transitioning into SD-WAN and wide-area network (WAN) specialization, with typical prerequisites of 3–5 years of networking experience.

## Domain context — Networking / SD-WAN

Software-defined wide area networks (SD-WAN) and modern WAN architectures: design, deployment, and management of carrier-agnostic, policy-driven, application-aware WAN overlays. Includes network architecture, multicloud connectivity, branch office consolidation, security integration (SASE), and operational visibility.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint (subject weights):

- **SD-WAN Architecture (20%)** — Architecture models, components (Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager, vSmart, vBond, WAN Edge routers), design principles, redundancy, high availability.
- **Controller Deployment (15%)** — Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager deployment, clustering, Day 0 configuration, licensing, multi-tenant environments.
- **WAN Edge Router Deployment (20%)** — Router provisioning, onboarding, policy enforcement, VPN tunnels, interface configuration, bandwidth shaping.
- **Policies and Traffic Management (20%)** — Application-aware routing, traffic engineering, QoS policies, class maps, policy objects, rate limiting.
- **Security and Quality of Service (15%)** — Firewall integration, intrusion prevention, SSL inspection, SASE concepts, QoS, encryption, threat defense.
- **Management and Operations (10%)** — Monitoring, device health, real-time monitoring, Cisco DNA Center integration, troubleshooting, event logging.

*Source:* [Cisco 300-415 ENSDWI Exam Topics ↗](https://learningnetwork.cisco.com/s/ensdwi-exam-topics)

## Skills validated

*ENSDWI-specific technologies and operational competencies:*

- Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager (formerly vManage) design, deployment, and administration
- Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Controller (vSmart) policy orchestration and tunnel establishment
- Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Border Router (vBond) authentication and discovery
- WAN Edge router (cEdge, vEdge) provisioning and onboarding
- VPN segmentation and multi-tenant deployments
- Application-aware routing (AppRoute) and traffic engineering
- QoS (DiffServ) in SD-WAN overlays
- Security in SD-WAN: firewall integration, intrusion prevention (IPS/IDS), SSL inspection, SASE concepts
- Encryption and authentication protocols (IPsec, TLS)
- Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN policies: traffic, device, and service-level policies
- Bandwidth management, traffic shaping, and flow optimization
- Monitoring and troubleshooting with Cisco DNA Center and real-time telemetry
- Cloud integration (Azure, AWS, Google Cloud) via SD-WAN
- Migration from legacy WAN to SD-WAN architectures
- High availability, redundancy, and disaster recovery in SD-WAN
