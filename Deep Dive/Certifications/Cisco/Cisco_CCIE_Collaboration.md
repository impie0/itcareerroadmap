## About

Cisco's apex voice and collaboration credential. The CCIE Collaboration validates expert-level competency in designing, deploying, and optimizing Cisco Unified Communications infrastructure at enterprise scale. Candidates master CUCM (call control), voice gateways, dial plans, conferencing, contact center integration, Webex hybrid deployments, and real-time media QoS/security. Requires passing a 90-minute written exam (350-801 CLCOR) followed by an 8-hour hands-on lab demonstrating design and deployment under operational constraints. Lifetime certification; expected prerequisite is 5–10 years real-world UC/voice experience or CCNP Collaboration.

## Topics covered

### Written Exam (350-801 CLCOR) — Weighted blueprint

- **CUCM & Call Processing** (30%)
  - Device configuration (phones, gateways, trunks, media resources)
  - Call routing, calling search spaces, dial plans
  - Feature settings (call forwarding, transfer, conference, DND)
  - Serviceability, RTMT, disaster recovery, clustering
  
- **Cisco Unity Connection** (15%)
  - Voice mail configuration, call handlers, auto-attendants
  - Integration with CUCM and redundancy
  
- **IM&P (Messaging & Presence)** (10%)
  - XMPP protocol, federation, web chat, mobile clients
  
- **Meeting Server & Webex** (15%)
  - On-premises meeting platform (CMS) and cloud integration
  - WebEx Calling, Contact Center basics
  
- **Voice Gateways & Protocols** (10%)
  - SIP, MGCP, H.323, SCCP signaling
  - IOS XE gateway config, PSTN trunks, transcoding, MTP
  - Codec selection (G.711, G.729, H.264)
  
- **QoS, Security, Media** (10%)
  - DSCP marking, CAC, bandwidth management
  - SRTP, TLS, authentication, encryption
  - RTP, media flows
  
- **Dial Plans & Features** (8%)
  - Digit analysis, translation rules, E911, routing
  - Call park, intercom, DND, call forwarding
  
- **Mobile Remote Access (MRA)** (2%)
  - Hybrid on-premises + cloud deployments

### Lab Exam (8 hours)

The lab integrates all topics into realistic design and operations scenarios:

- **Design Module (3 hours):** Multi-site voice architecture, technology selection, redundancy/DR, gateway/conferencing/contact-center integration
- **Deployment & Operations Module (5 hours):** CUCM cluster setup, voice gateway config, IP phone provisioning, dial plans, call flows, QoS/security, Webex integration, troubleshooting

*Source:* [Cisco CCIE Collaboration Lab Exam Topics ↗](https://learningnetwork.cisco.com/s/ccie-collaboration-exam-topics)
