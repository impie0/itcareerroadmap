## About

The Implementing Cisco Enterprise Wireless Networks (300-430 ENWLSI) exam certifies professional-level competency in designing, deploying, and securing enterprise wireless infrastructure. Launched in 2019, ENWLSI is a concentration exam within the CCNP Enterprise certification path, validating hands-on skills with Cisco Catalyst 9800 Wireless Controllers, Cisco Identity Service Engine (ISE), Cisco Prime Infrastructure, and Cisco DNA Center. It focuses on real-world wireless implementations in campus, branch, and remote-office environments.

## Topics covered

The exam validates knowledge across eight primary domains:

- **FlexConnect Implementation (15%)** — Deploying FlexConnect architecture; local and centralized switching modes; Office Extend; autonomous access points; roaming and failover.
- **QoS on Wireless Networks (10%)** — Wired-to-wireless QoS mapping; wireless client QoS policies; Application Visibility and Control (AVC); FastLane; traffic classification and rate limiting.
- **Multicast on Wireless (10%)** — Multicast components and design; multicast impact on wireless; WLAN multicast; mDNS (Bonjour); Multicast Direct (MLD).
- **Location Services (15%)** — Cisco DNA Spaces; asset tracking; location analytics; RTLS (Real-Time Location Services); geofencing; presence and proximity.
- **Advanced Location Services (10%)** — Cisco Meraki location intelligence; location-based policies; integration with security and management platforms.
- **Wireless Security (15%)** — Client authentication (802.1X, EAP); encryption standards (WPA2/WPA3); certificate management; guest network segmentation; threat mitigation.
- **Monitoring and Troubleshooting (10%)** — Cisco Prime Infrastructure; DNA Center dashboards; real-time health monitoring; client connectivity diagnostics; performance baselines.
- **Device Hardening (15%)** — Access control lists; secure management access; firmware updates; SNMP v3; logging; compliance and audit trails.

*Source:* [Cisco 300-430 Exam Topics ↗](https://www.cisco.com/c/dam/en_us/training-events/le31/le46/cln/marketing/exam-topics/300-430-ENWLSI.pdf)

## Skills validated

*Concrete technologies and protocols this exam tests.*

- Cisco Catalyst 9800 Wireless Controller (centralized control plane)
- FlexConnect (autonomous mode, local switching, Office Extend)
- 802.11ac/ax (Wi-Fi 5/6) standards and capabilities
- WPA2/WPA3 enterprise security
- 802.1X authentication and EAP frameworks
- Cisco Identity Service Engine (ISE) integration
- QoS classification, marking, and enforcement (DSCP, CoS)
- Multicast routing (PIM, IGMP) on wireless networks
- Cisco DNA Center network automation
- Cisco Prime Infrastructure monitoring and alerting
- RTLS and location services (Cisco Spaces)
- Access point hardening and secure management
- Roaming, load balancing, and failover mechanisms
- SNMP v3 and syslog for device monitoring
- Certificate management and PKI integration
