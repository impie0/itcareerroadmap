## About

The Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud Professional certification validates expertise in deploying, managing, and supporting the Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud platform in multi-tenant managed service provider (MSP) and enterprise environments. Launched in 2023 as part of Acronis's tiered #CyberFit Academy certification track (Fundamentals → Associate → Professional → Expert), it targets backup engineers, MSP architects, and solution specialists. The exam covers cloud platform architecture, multi-tenant administration, backup and disaster recovery operations, anti-malware and endpoint security, compliance and data protection, and advanced troubleshooting. Completion earns a Credly-issued digital badge valid for 12 months.

Backup and cyber protection encompasses enterprise-grade data resilience, disaster recovery (DR), ransomware defense, and integrated endpoint security. ## Topics covered

- **Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud Architecture** — multi-tenant SaaS model, deployment options (cloud-hosted vs. hybrid), API overview
- **Platform Administration & Tenant Management** — organization units, user roles, RBAC, licensing models (Included, Pay-As-You-Go, Advanced Packs)
- **Backup & Recovery Operations** — backup scheduling, retention policies, recovery point objectives (RPO), recovery time objectives (RTO), granular restore options
- **Disaster Recovery** — failover strategies, business continuity planning, RTO/RPO configuration, backup verification
- **Anti-Malware & Threat Protection** — real-time scanning, malware detection and remediation, quarantine management
- **Patch Management & Vulnerability Assessment** — patch deployment policies, vulnerability scanning, remediation workflows
- **Endpoint Management** — agent deployment, remote management, compliance reporting
- **Advanced Security Features** — notarization (blockchain-backed proof of backup integrity), email security, anti-phishing
- **Compliance & Data Protection** — data encryption (at-rest and in-transit), compliance reporting (HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2), audit trails
- **Integration & API** — third-party integrations, API-driven automation, webhooks
- **Troubleshooting & Performance Optimization** — diagnostics, bandwidth throttling, deduplication tuning
- **Hands-on Lab Scenarios** — real-world MSP deployment, multi-customer administration, incident response
