## About

The Acronis Certified Specialist — Backup & Recovery Administrator (formally recognized as Acronis Certified Engineer or ACE) is a professional-level vendor credential validating the ability to plan, implement, configure, and operate Acronis Backup and Acronis Backup Cloud solutions in on-premises, cloud-only, or hybrid environments. Designed for IT professionals managing organizational data protection, disaster recovery, and backup infrastructure, this certification has been offered since 2010 and remains current as Acronis updates training content with each product release. The credential is typically pursued by systems administrators, storage engineers, and disaster recovery specialists seeking formal validation of Acronis platform expertise.

---

## Domain context — Backup/Disaster Recovery

Backup and Disaster Recovery encompasses technologies, strategies, and practices for protecting critical data and IT infrastructure through replication, archiving, and recovery mechanisms. This domain addresses organizational requirements for RPO (Recovery Point Objective) and RTO (Recovery Time Objective) compliance, ransomware mitigation, and business continuity across physical, virtual, and cloud environments.

---

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint covers the following domains and topics:

- **Acronis Product Fundamentals & Architecture** — Product positioning, deployment models, licensing
- **Installation, Configuration & Integration** — Installation prerequisites, configuration of backup agents, integration with storage systems
- **Backup Operations** — Creating backup plans, scheduling, backup types (full, incremental, differential), compression and encryption
- **Recovery Operations** — File, system, and bare-metal recovery procedures; recovery validation
- **Centralized Management** — Management console setup, multi-tenant administration, reporting
- **Storage & Performance Optimization** — Storage node configuration, deduplication, storage pools, tape management
- **Virtualization Support** — VMware, Hyper-V, and cloud platform integration
- **Data Protection & Disaster Recovery Strategies** — RTO/RPO planning, DR plan development
- **Troubleshooting, Performance Optimization & Support** — Log analysis, performance tuning, vendor support procedures
