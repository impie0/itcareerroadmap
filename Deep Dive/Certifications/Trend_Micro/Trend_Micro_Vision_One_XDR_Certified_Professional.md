## About

The Trend Micro Vision One XDR Certified Professional validates hands-on proficiency with Trend Micro's unified extended detection and response (XDR) platform. Candidates complete a three-day instructor-led course featuring practical labs covering Vision One sensor deployment, workbench investigation workflows, threat intelligence integration, data-lake searches, and security playbook automation. Upon course completion, candidates sit for a proctored certification exam. The certification is relevant for SOC analysts, security engineers, MDR/MXDR practitioners, and threat hunters operationalizing cross-domain detection and response across endpoints, email, networks, and cloud infrastructure.

---

## Domain context — Security/XDR

Extended Detection and Response (XDR) is a vendor-consolidation security model that unifies endpoint, email, network, cloud, and identity signals into a single platform for coordinated threat detection and automated response. Trend Micro Vision One is a leading enterprise XDR platform with strong integration of third-party sensors.
