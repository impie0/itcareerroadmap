## About

The Informatica Certified Professional – Master Data Management certification validates advanced expertise in developing, deploying, and administering master data management solutions. This professional-level certification is designed for MDM architects, developers, administrators, and data governance leaders implementing enterprise-wide master data governance programs.

Certified professionals demonstrate mastery in MDM architecture, data governance design, entity resolution, stewardship workflows, performance optimization, and integration with broader data ecosystems—critical capabilities for organizations establishing single sources of truth.

## Domain context

Master Data Management has become central to modern data strategy:

**Master Data Governance**: Establishing organizational policies, roles, and processes for managing master data. Defining data ownership and stewardship responsibilities. Creating accountability frameworks and metrics.

**Data Architecture & Modeling**: Designing efficient MDM systems with appropriate entity hierarchies, relationships, and data structures. Optimizing for query performance and scalability.

**Entity Resolution & Consolidation**: Building sophisticated matching logic to identify and merge duplicate records. Creating unified master records with survivorship rules.

**Stewardship & Workflow**: Implementing collaborative workflows for data ownership, change management, and conflict resolution. Managing quality exceptions and remediation.

**Integration & Synchronization**: Distributing master data to dependent systems. Maintaining consistency across the enterprise. Implementing bi-directional sync patterns.
