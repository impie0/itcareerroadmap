## About

The Proofpoint Certified Engineer credential validates technical expertise in deploying, configuring, and managing Proofpoint's email security, data protection, and threat response platform suite. Positioned at the Professional level, it demonstrates readiness to independently administer Threat Response Auto-Pull (TRAP), Targeted Account Protection (TAP), Email Protection, Data Loss Prevention (DLP), and Insider Threat Management (ITM) modules. Launched in 2021, the certification has evolved continuously to reflect Proofpoint's expanding acquisition portfolio—including Tessian (AI-powered email DLP), Hornetsecurity (MSP-focused M365 security, acquired Dec 2025 for $1.8B), and Normalyze (Data Security Posture Management). The credential is available via Guardian Pass subscription or per-exam purchase; retakes are permitted with a 7-day waiting period.

## Topics covered

Exam content is modularized; candidates typically certify in one or more specialization tracks:

### People Protection (Email + Threat Response)
- **Threat Response Auto-Pull (TRAP)** — incident response automation, malicious email detection & remediation, quarantine workflows
- **Targeted Account Protection (TAP)** — advanced threat detection, URL analysis, malware sandboxing, credential phishing defense
- **Email Protection core** — spam filtering, authentication (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), archiving integration
- **Deployment & administration** — on-premises vs. cloud configuration, Exchange/Office 365/Google Workspace integration, user policies
- **Troubleshooting & incident response** — log analysis, quarantine management, false-positive tuning

### Information Protection (Data Loss Prevention + Insider Threat)
- **Email Data Loss Prevention (DLP)** — policy configuration, sensitive data classification, Tessian Guardian (misdirected email prevention)
- **Insider Threat Management (ITM)** — risky user behavior detection, data exfiltration monitoring, case management workflows
- **Endpoint Data Loss Prevention** — file activity monitoring, cloud app control, compliance reporting
- **Integration & remediation** — CASB integration, automated response playbooks, policy exceptions

### Data Security Posture Management (DSPM)
- **Normalyze platform basics** — data discovery & classification, multi-cloud/SaaS visibility
- **Security posture assessment** — permission audits, compliance gap detection (500+ benchmarks)
- **Remediation workflows** — one-click privilege removal, data exposure prioritization
