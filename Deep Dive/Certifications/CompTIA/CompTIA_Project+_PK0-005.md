## About

CompTIA Project+ (PK0-005) is a vendor-neutral, foundational project management credential designed for IT professionals transitioning from technical roles to project coordination and junior project management. Unlike PMI's CAPM (which requires formal coursework and assumes project management knowledge), Project+ is a low-barrier entry point—no prerequisites, affordable at $369, and covers both Agile and Waterfall approaches. Launched in November 2022, this exam validates competency in small-to-medium project delivery, stakeholder communication, resource management, and IT-specific project lifecycle practices. It is particularly valued as a stepping stone from Help Desk → PMO Coordinator → Junior PM or for career-changers entering IT management. The cert is actively renewed and holds stable market demand among IT operations and systems administration teams.

## Domain context — IT Management

**IT Management** encompasses leadership, governance, and organizational change across project management, service management (ITIL), governance/risk/compliance (GRC), and Agile frameworks. Project+ sits at the **Entry level** of this domain—foundational PM lifecycle, basic stakeholder and risk management, communication planning, and awareness of both predictive (Waterfall) and adaptive (Agile/Scrum) methodologies.

## Topics covered

The PK0-005 exam blueprint covers **five core domains**:

- **1.0 Project Initiation** (22%) — Project charter, scope definition, stakeholder identification, business case, high-level requirements
- **2.0 Project Planning** (26%) — Work breakdown structure (WBS), schedule estimation, resource planning, communication plan, risk register, quality metrics, procurement planning
- **3.0 Project Execution** (20%) — Team management, task coordination, communication execution, quality assurance, document management, status reporting, change control
- **4.0 Project Monitoring and Controlling** (16%) — Performance metrics, variance analysis, corrective/preventive actions, scope/schedule/budget monitoring, stakeholder communication
- **5.0 Project Closure** (16%) — Deliverable acceptance, lessons learned, project documentation archive, resource release, stakeholder sign-off

*Source:* [CompTIA Project+ Exam Objectives ↗](https://www.comptia.org/en-us/certifications/project/)

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests.*

- **Project Charter Creation** – Business case, project objectives, success criteria, authority delegation
- **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)** – Hierarchical decomposition, task sequencing, durations, dependencies
- **Scheduling Techniques** – Critical path method (CPM), Gantt charts, network diagrams, resource leveling, float calculation
- **Budget Estimation & Control** – Cost estimation, earned value management (EVM) basics, budget variance
- **Scope Management** – Scope statement, scope creep prevention, change requests, scope verification
- **Risk Management** – Risk identification, qualitative/quantitative analysis, response planning, contingency reserves
- **Stakeholder Management** – RACI matrix, stakeholder engagement, communication planning, conflict resolution
- **Team Leadership** – Motivation, team formation (forming/storming/norming/performing), delegation, performance management
- **Quality Assurance & Control** – Quality metrics, inspection, corrective/preventive actions, acceptance criteria
- **Document Management** – Project documentation standards, configuration management, lessons learned repository
- **Agile & Waterfall Awareness** – Iterative vs. predictive approaches, Scrum/Kanban roles, hybrid PM
- **Status Reporting & Communication** – Progress reporting, variance analysis, escalation procedures, stakeholder updates
