## About

The Pure Storage FlashArray Implementation Professional certification validates hands-on expertise in designing, deploying, and managing Pure Storage FlashArray systems in production enterprise environments. Candidates demonstrate deep proficiency in FlashArray architecture, Purity//FA operating system, data replication, snapshots, volume management, host connectivity protocols (FC, iSCSI, NVMe), Pure1 monitoring, and Evergreen storage lifecycle management. This certification is designed for storage engineers, systems administrators, and technical specialists transitioning to Pure Storage implementations. Successful candidates typically have 3–5 years of storage or data center operations background plus 6–12 months of hands-on FlashArray experience.

## Topics covered

Based on the official FAIP_002 exam blueprint, candidates are assessed on:

- **FlashArray architecture & Purity//FA OS** — array design, controller pairs, NVRAM, persistent cache, deduplication, compression, thin provisioning
- **Storage management** — volume provisioning, snapshots, protection groups, replication policies (synchronous / asynchronous), ActiveCluster multi-site failover
- **Host connectivity & protocols** — Fibre Channel (FC), iSCSI, NVMe/TCP configuration, zoning, initiator authentication
- **Pure1 monitoring & analytics** — dashboard navigation, performance metrics, health alerts, capacity planning
- **Evergreen & Purity upgrades** — non-disruptive upgrades, subscription management, firmware patching, migration to newer arrays
- **ActiveCluster & replication** — bidirectional replication, asynchronous long-distance replication, failover scenarios, data consistency
- **Disaster recovery & business continuity** — RTO/RPO planning, snapshot schedules, replication policies, failover testing
- **Performance tuning & optimization** — workload characterization, I/O patterns, cache sizing, QoS policies
- **Troubleshooting** — common error codes, diagnostic logs, performance bottleneck identification, vendor support escalation
