## About

The Everpure FlashArray Architect Professional certification validates expertise in designing, integrating, and architecting FlashArray solutions for presales and architecture roles. Targeted at presales engineers and solutions architects with 3–5 years of storage industry experience and 1–2 years hands-on experience with Everpure FlashArray. No formal prerequisites, though completion of the Associate-level certification is recommended. Exam remains current and actively proctored via ExamStudio.

## Topics covered

Based on official exam blueprint (percentage weights reflect vendor documentation):

- **FlashArray architecture deep dive** — system architecture, hardware/software stack, performance characteristics
- **Sizing and capacity planning** — workload analysis, array sizing, provisioning strategies
- **Data protection strategies** — snapshot design, replication architectures (synchronous, asynchronous, ActiveCluster)
- **Multi-site replication and disaster recovery** — ActiveCluster, metro-active architecture, recovery objectives
- **Storage networking** — Fibre Channel, iSCSI, NVMe-oF, fabric design for FlashArray
- **Data migration design** — planning and executing migrations from legacy arrays to FlashArray
- **Pure1 advanced analytics and monitoring** — performance monitoring, predictive analytics, capacity forecasting
- **Integration design patterns** — VMware vSphere, Kubernetes, AWS Outposts, Microsoft Hyper-V, Oracle Database
- **Evergreen lifecycle and economics** — upgrade paths, TCO analysis, subscription models
- **Advanced management and automation** — REST API, Pure1 APIs, automation frameworks, DevOps integration
