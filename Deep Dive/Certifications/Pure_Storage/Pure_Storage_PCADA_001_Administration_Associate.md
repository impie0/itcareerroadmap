## About

The Everpure Certified Administration Associate (PCADA_001) validates hands-on proficiency in configuring, managing, and optimizing Everpure FlashArray storage systems. The certification is designed for storage professionals who have at least 3 months of practical FlashArray administration experience. Candidates are expected to demonstrate competency in capacity planning, snapshot management, disaster recovery configuration, and monitoring via Pure1 cloud management. No formal prerequisites exist, though hands-on experience with FlashArray, Purity operating environment, and Pure1 is essential. The credential is valid for 3 years and demonstrates entry-level mastery of FlashArray administration tasks.

## Topics covered

Based on the official PCADA_001 exam guide:

- **FlashArray Architecture & Deployment** — FlashArray models, physical infrastructure, Purity operating environment versions, cluster configurations
- **Storage Provisioning** — Creating and managing volumes, host groups, hosts, and iSCSI/Fibre Channel connections
- **Snapshot & Replication Management** — Creating snapshots, managing protection groups, configuring replication policies, disaster recovery (ActiveCluster)
- **Pure1 Cloud Management** — Using Pure1 for capacity/workload forecasting, performance monitoring, proactive alerts
- **Data Protection & Business Continuity** — Backup strategies, remote replication, failover procedures, retention policies
- **Performance Monitoring & Optimization** — Identifying bottlenecks, analyzing workload patterns, tuning storage configurations
- **Administration & Troubleshooting** — User and permission management, port configuration, alert handling, diagnostic tools
- **Evergreen & Lifecycle Management** — Hardware refresh cycles, firmware updates, compatibility tracking

*Source:* [Official PCADA_001 Exam Guide ↗](https://www.purestorage.com/content/dam/pdf/en/certifications/cf-administration-associate-exam-guide.pdf)
