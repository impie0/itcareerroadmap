## About

The ServiceNow Certified Implementation Specialist — Human Resources Service Delivery (CIS-HRSD) is a professional-level credential validating expertise in implementing, configuring, and optimizing the ServiceNow HRSD module for enterprise Human Resources transformation. Launched in 2018 and continuously updated with each ServiceNow release cycle (three times yearly), CIS-HRSD bridges foundational system administration (CSA prerequisite) and specialized HR module mastery. The exam covers HR system architecture, employee lifecycle automation (onboarding/offboarding), service portal design, performance management, compensation/benefits administration, manager self-service, reporting, and compliance. Required or strongly preferred for HR technology consultants, ServiceNow HRSD implementation specialists, and IT professionals transitioning into HR tech roles, CIS-HRSD demands 3–6 months hands-on HRSD project experience and completion of official ServiceNow HR Service Delivery Implementation training.

## Domain context — HR / HRSD

ServiceNow HR Service Delivery (HRSD) focuses on employee-centric HR automation: employee lifecycle events (onboarding, offboarding, promotions, parental leave), self-service portals, case and knowledge management for HR inquiries, performance and talent management integration, total rewards (compensation, benefits) administration, manager hubs for delegation, and reporting/dashboarding for HR metrics. HRSD specializes in transforming transactional HR operations into strategic, automated, and data-driven processes.

## Topics covered

The official ServiceNow CIS-HR exam blueprint covers these domain areas:

- **HR System Architecture & Data Model (25%)** — HRSD data structures, employee record setup, organization hierarchy, COE (Centers of Excellence) tables, security models specific to HR.
- **Employee Lifecycle Events (20%)** — Onboarding workflows, offboarding workflows, promotion workflows, parental leave, rehire scenarios, activity sets, task assignments, state transitions.
- **HR Service Delivery Portal & Self-Service (15%)** — Employee Service Center design, catalog item configuration for HR requests, knowledge management for HR FAQs, request fulfillment, mobile support.
- **Performance & Talent Management Integration (12%)** — Goal-setting workflows, performance review cycles, talent review boards, succession planning, learning management integration.
- **Total Rewards Administration (12%)** — Compensation frameworks, benefits enrollment, salary review workflows, incentive management, reporting on total rewards.
- **Manager Hub & Delegation (8%)** — Manager self-service, delegation of HR tasks, team dashboards, direct report workflows, analytics for managers.
- **Reporting & Analytics (5%)** — HR-specific reports (headcount, turnover, time-to-fill, cost-per-hire), dashboards for executives and HR teams, HRSD data compliance.
- **Security, Compliance & Data Governance (3%)** — Role-based access control (RBAC) for sensitive HR data, audit trails, data retention, GDPR/CCPA compliance within HRSD.

## Skills validated

*What the CIS-HRSD exam specifically tests, distinct from shared Professional-level HR skills.*

- **HR system architecture and HRSD data model** — Employee record setup, organization structure (company, department, location), COE (Centers of Excellence) table hierarchies, legal entity configuration, security zones for HR data.
- **Lifecycle event orchestration** — Onboarding workflows (pre-boarding, day-1 tasks, ramp-up periods), offboarding (exit interviews, asset return, knowledge transfer), promotion workflows, parental leave, rehire, role changes.
- **Activity sets and task assignment** — Designing activity sets (groups of related tasks), task routing to departments/managers, deadline management, status tracking, escalation.
- **Employee Service Center (ESC) configuration** — Portal design, user experience personalization, search and navigation, knowledge base integration, request submission and tracking.
- **Service Catalog for HR** — Creating HR service requests (benefits enrollment, address change, equipment request, policy questions), catalog variables, item options, business rules linked to requests.
- **Performance Management** — Goal-setting workflows, performance review cycles, peer feedback, talent review boards, succession planning integration.
- **Total Rewards** — Compensation plan configuration, benefits enrollment workflows, salary review cycles, bonus/incentive tracking, deduction management.
- **Manager Hub** — Manager self-service dashboards, delegation of HR responsibilities, direct-report visibility, team analytics, approval workflows.
- **Reporting and analytics** — HR-specific reports (headcount trends, turnover rate, time-to-fill, cost-per-hire, salary bands), executive dashboards, compliance reporting.
- **Security and compliance** — RBAC for sensitive HR data, audit logging, data retention policies, GDPR/CCPA considerations, secure document handling.
- **Integrations with adjacent systems** — Payroll data exchange, learning management system (LMS) integration, finance (GL coding for compensation), identity provider (Azure AD, Okta) sync.
