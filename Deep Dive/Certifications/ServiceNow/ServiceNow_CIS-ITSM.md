## About

The **CIS-ITSM** is ServiceNow's professional-level certification validating practical implementation expertise across all core ITSM module domains. Candidates demonstrate mastery of incident, problem, change, request, knowledge, and configuration management within the ServiceNow platform. Released in 2020 and updated through 2024, this credential targets architects, senior consultants, and implementation engineers who configure, customize, and deploy ITSM solutions at scale. Requires current **CSA (Certified System Administrator)** prerequisite — evidence of foundational platform literacy before tackling domain-specific expertise.

---

## Domain context — ITSM

IT Service Management (ITSM) spans operational processes from request intake through knowledge capture: incident / problem / change / configuration management, service catalog, knowledge base, and SLA enforcement. ServiceNow dominates the enterprise ITSM market, making this credential especially valuable for organisations standardizing on the platform.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint weights core ITSM domains as follows:

- **Incident Management (15–20%)** — Incident creation, classification, routing, escalation, resolution tracking, and closure
- **Problem Management (10–15%)** — Problem investigation, root-cause analysis, Known Error database, workaround management
- **Change Management (15–20%)** — Change request workflow, approval gates, CAB (Change Advisory Board), implementation, testing, rollback
- **Knowledge Management (10–15%)** — Knowledge article authoring, categorisation, search optimisation, feedback loops
- **Request Management / Service Catalog (15–20%)** — Service catalog design, item configuration, cart functionality, fulfillment workflows
- **Configuration Management (10–15%)** — Configuration Item (CI) definition, relationships, CMDB maintenance, asset reconciliation
- **Service Level Management (5–10%)** — SLA definition, metrics, breaches, reporting
- **CMDB & CI Management (5–10%)** — Auto-discovery, relationship mapping, business service perspective
- **Advanced workflows & integrations** — Process automation, script includes, email integration, REST/SOAP API basics

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests, distinct from the shared "Common skills" above.*

- **ServiceNow ITSM module configuration** — Incident, Problem, Change, Request, Knowledge, CMDB modules
- **Data model relationships** — Linking CIs, incidents, problems, changes, and change tasks
- **Workflow design & automation** — Business rules, script includes, process flows, approval workflows
- **Reporting & dashboards** — Creating reports on incident trends, SLA compliance, request fulfillment
- **CMDB discovery & reconciliation** — Managing auto-discovered assets, CI relationships, business services
- **Knowledge management lifecycle** — Article creation, categorisation, search optimisation, feedback integration
- **Email integration & notifications** — Configuring email-to-case, outbound actions, inbound email parsing
- **REST / SOAP API consumption** — Building integrations with external systems (HR, asset management, ticketing)
- **Multi-instance & production safety** — Change promotion, quality gates, testing in staging environments
