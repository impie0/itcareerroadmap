## About

The ServiceNow Certified Implementation Specialist – Hardware Asset Management (CIS-HAM) validates proficiency in configuring, implementing, and maintaining the ServiceNow HAM application module. This professional-level certification confirms hands-on expertise in asset lifecycle management—from procurement through disposal—within the ServiceNow ecosystem, and is intended for consultants, system administrators, and ITAM professionals who manage hardware asset workflows at scale.

---

## Domain context — IT Asset Management (ITAM)

IT Asset Management (ITAM) is the vendor-neutral discipline of inventorying, tracking, and optimizing the lifecycle of hardware, software, and digital assets across organizations. It spans procurement, discovery, compliance, cost optimization, and refresh/disposal cycles. ServiceNow HAM is a leading enterprise solution within this domain.

## Topics covered

The CIS-HAM exam blueprint covers the following domains and competencies:

- **Hardware Asset Architecture** – Asset model design, CI relationships, plugin configuration
- **Asset Lifecycle Management** – Request, Procure, Receive, Consume, and Dispose workflows
- **Discovery & Integration** – Hardware discovery, normalization rules, reconciliation logic, CMDB alignment
- **Hardware Asset Allocation** – Assignment workflows, user/department tracking, ownership models
- **Stockroom & Shipping** – Inventory control, stock locations, receiving procedures, asset movement
- **Compliance Workflows** – Regulatory adherence, certification tracking, audit trails
- **Cost Tracking & Depreciation** – Cost models, depreciation methods, total cost of ownership (TCO) calculation
- **Refresh & Disposal Management** – Retirement workflows, asset disposal compliance, end-of-life processes
- **Reporting & Dashboards** – HAM-specific reports, KPIs, executive dashboards
- **Hardware Asset Workspace** – Modern UI interaction, mobile-friendly asset interactions
- **Roles & Permissions** – HAM personas (requester, approver, asset custodian, manager)

## Skills validated

*Concrete technologies and tools this exam tests, distinct from broader ITAM domain skills.*

- ServiceNow HAM application configuration and customization
- Asset discovery tools integration (Flexera, Qualys, etc.) and normalization
- ServiceNow CMDB relationship modeling for hardware assets
- Workflow and automation design (business rules, flows, scheduled jobs)
- Reporting and dashboard development in ServiceNow
- HAM Fundamentals module: request, procurement, receipt, allocation, tracking
- Hardware lifecycle state transitions and ownership management
- Cost tracking, depreciation models, and TCO calculation within HAM

- Mobile app and Hardware Asset Workspace interaction
- API integration for third-party asset data feeds
- Role-based access control (RBAC) configuration for HAM personas
