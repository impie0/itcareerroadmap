## About

The Certified Implementation Specialist – Customer Service Management (CIS-CSM) validates your ability to design, configure, and implement the ServiceNow CSM application to optimize customer service processes. This professional-level certification is designed for ServiceNow implementation consultants, system administrators, and functional analysts responsible for deploying CSM across organizations. The CIS-CSM exam assesses practical knowledge of account and contact management, case lifecycle workflows, entitlements and service level agreements (SLAs), customer self-service portals, and performance analytics specific to customer service operations.

## Topics covered

The CIS-CSM exam blueprint covers six primary domains:

- **CSM Foundational Data Model** — Core CSM data structures, business models, product models, install base management, contract and entitlement handling
- **CSM System Setup and Configuration** — Enabling CSM, routing and assignment rules, case type definition, communication channel configuration, CSM workspace customization
- **Case Management** — Case lifecycle states and flows, major incident handling, service definition and digest setup, case actions, tasks, and escalation rules
- **Entitlements and Service Contracts** — Contract management, entitlement policies, service level agreement (SLA) configuration and measurement
- **Customer Self-Service and Knowledge Management** — Self-service portal design, knowledge article authoring and categorization, customer access controls
- **Reporting and Analytics** — Performance dashboards, CSM-specific analytics modules, KPI tracking, operational metrics visualization
