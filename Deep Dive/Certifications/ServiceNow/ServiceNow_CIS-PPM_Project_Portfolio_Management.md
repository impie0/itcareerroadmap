## About

The ServiceNow Certified Implementation Specialist - Project Portfolio Management (CIS-PPM) validates the ability to configure, implement, and deploy PPM capabilities on the ServiceNow platform. This Professional-level cert is designed for consultants, implementation specialists, and project/portfolio managers responsible for translating business strategy into executable portfolios, managing demand intake, allocating resources, and tracking financial outcomes within ServiceNow. The cert is foundational for PPM practitioners and serves as a stepping stone toward Strategic Portfolio Management (SPM) specialist roles.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint areas examined:

- **Demand Management** (weight: ~20%) — Demand intake flows, demand scoring and prioritization, demand-to-project routing, demand prioritization matrix
- **Project & Program Management** (weight: ~20%) — Project fundamentals, milestone tracking, project workspace configuration, program hierarchy and rollup, project vs. program distinctions
- **Resource Management** (weight: ~20%) — Resource pools and allocation models, capacity planning, utilization tracking, resource leveling, conflict resolution, time card integration
- **Portfolio Governance & Controls** (weight: ~15%) — Portfolio health dashboards, strategic alignment, portfolio-level reporting, gate reviews, investment platform integration
- **Financial Management** (weight: ~10%) — Project budgeting and costing, cost tracking, budget vs. actuals, financial forecasting at portfolio level
- **Time Card & Time Management** (weight: ~8%) — Time entry workflows, actuals vs. planned, utilization reporting, time tracking integration with projects
- **Workspace & Dashboard Configuration** (weight: ~7%) — Project workspace layout, custom dashboards, search and visibility, UI customization for PPM roles
