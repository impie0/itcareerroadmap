## About

The CIS-FSM certifies that you can configure, implement, and maintain the ServiceNow Field Service Management (FSM) application for enterprise field operations. The exam validates hands-on expertise in work order management, dispatcher routing, field technician mobile workflows, resource scheduling, and integration with inventory and asset modules. Required prerequisite is the CSA cert plus a 30-hour vendor-delivered implementation course. This is a professional-level credential for implementation specialists and field service architects deploying FSM in production environments.

---

## Domain context — Field Service Management

Vendor-specific implementation of mobile-first field-operations software (work order dispatch, technician routing, inventory sync, customer-facing scheduling). ServiceNow FSM competes with Salesforce FSM and smaller best-of-breed platforms (Skedulo, Subzero, Mphasis).

## Topics covered

- **Work Order Lifecycle** (15%) — creation, prioritization, scheduling, closure, SLA enforcement
- **Dispatch & Routing** (20%) — dispatcher console, field service mobile app navigation, real-time assignment, priority rules
- **Resource Management** (18%) — technician profiles, skills, availability, capacity planning, team assignments
- **Routing & Travel Time Optimization** (17%) — distance/time calculations, geospatial routing, appointment availability windows, map integration
- **Inventory & Parts Management** (12%) — stockroom setup, parts allocation, technician truck inventory, consumption tracking
- **Field Service Mobile App** (10%) — mobile-specific configuration, offline sync, time tracking, photo/signature capture, customer communication
- **Asset & Maintenance Integration** (8%) — asset record linkage, preventive/corrective maintenance workflows, contract integration
