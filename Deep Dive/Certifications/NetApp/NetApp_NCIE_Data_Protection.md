## About

The NetApp Certified Implementation Engineer — Data Protection (NCIE-DP) is an expert-level certification validating advanced hands-on proficiency in designing, implementing, and managing enterprise-scale data protection and disaster recovery solutions using NetApp ONTAP, SnapMirror, MetroCluster, and SnapCenter technologies. Prerequisite is NCDA. This certification is designed for storage architects, senior engineers, and disaster recovery specialists who deploy NetApp in high-availability and multi-site environments. Launched in 2020 as part of NetApp's NCIE track, it remains current with active exam delivery through Pearson VUE.

## Topics covered

Based on NetApp training materials and ONTAP architecture documentation:

- **Replication & Recovery:** SnapMirror (asynchronous, synchronous, unified), SnapVault, SnapLock, vault cascade patterns
- **High Availability:** MetroCluster IP and FC configurations, switchover/switchback, TieBreaker, ALUA
- **Backup & Restore:** SnapCenter plug-ins, ONTAP backup architecture, RPO/RTO design, incremental backups, retention policies
- **Data Protection at Scale:** Consistency groups, application-aware snapshots, multi-site strategies
- **Cyber Protection:** Snapshots with immutable retention (Compliance Lock), ransomware detection hooks, S3 SnapMirror to Object Lock
- **Cloud Integration:** Cloud Volumes Service backups, cloud SnapMirror endpoints, hybrid data protection
- **ONTAP Anti-Ransomware:** Behavioral detection, known-extension blocking, suspicious file activity flagging
- **Compliance & Governance:** Retention management, Legal Hold, audit logging, regulatory alignment

*Source:* NetApp ONTAP cluster administration guides; NCIE-DP exam blueprint (vendor portal).
