## About

The NetApp Certified Storage Installation Engineer, ONTAP (NCSIE) validates professional-level competency in deploying, configuring, installing, testing, and troubleshooting NetApp ONTAP storage systems in production environments. Aimed at field engineers, storage administrators, and IT professionals implementing NetApp solutions, this certification confirms hands-on expertise with ONTAP clustering, networking, system configuration, and customer handoff procedures. The NS0-185 exam is the current iteration of the NCSIE certification, replacing earlier versions (NS0-182, NS0-183, NS0-184) and remains an active credential for NetApp field engineering and enterprise storage roles.
