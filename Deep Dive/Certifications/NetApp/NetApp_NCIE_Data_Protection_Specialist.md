## About

The NetApp Certified Implementation Engineer—Data Protection Specialist (NCIE-DP) validates your ability to design, implement, and troubleshoot NetApp data protection solutions using ONTAP replication, SnapMirror, SnapVault, MetroCluster, and cloud-integrated backup. This professional-level certification is for engineers with 6–12 months of hands-on experience planning and executing backup, disaster recovery, and business continuity deployments. It builds on the NCDA foundation and is a prerequisite for the NCIE Storage Administrator (NCSA) and NCIE Storage Architect (NCSE) certifications.

## Topics covered

- **ONTAP Data Protection Replication** (SnapMirror sync/async, SnapVault, intercluster peering, SVM-DR)
- **MetroCluster and Business Continuity** (zero-RTO sync replication, site failover, configuration)
- **NetApp Backup Integration** (SnapCenter, Veeam, Commvault, Cohesity orchestration)
- **Cloud Backup and Tiering** (BlueXP, Cloud Backup Service, cloud-tiered DR)
- **Encryption and Compliance** (NetApp Volume Encryption / NVE, NetApp Aggregate Encryption / NAE, SnapLock for WORM)
- **Troubleshooting and Testing** (validation, failover testing, recovery procedures, performance tuning)
- **ONTAP Advanced Features** (Snapshots, clones, efficiency, RAID-DP, RAID-TEC, spares management)

## Common skills at Storage / Backup · Professional

- Design enterprise backup and disaster recovery architectures for medium–large environments
- Configure and troubleshoot synchronous and asynchronous replication technologies
- Integrate backup software (SnapCenter, Veeam, Commvault, Rubrik) with storage platforms
- Plan failover/failback procedures and recovery time objectives (RTO/RPO)
- Implement encryption and compliance frameworks (WORM, regulatory holds)
- Monitor and optimize replication bandwidth and snapshot policies

- Document and automate recurring backup and DR drills

## Skills validated

- **SnapMirror Configuration** — synchronous and asynchronous replication policies, cluster peering, SVM replication
- **SnapVault and Backup Policies** — incremental backups, retention policies, archive tiering
- **MetroCluster Design** — zero-RTO active-active synchronous mirroring, site failover, quorum
- **SnapCenter Integration** — application-consistent backup, backup scheduling, policy enforcement
- **Cloud Backup Solutions** — BlueXP Cloud Backup, tiering to cloud tiers, ransomware protection
- **ONTAP Encryption** — NetApp Volume Encryption (NVE), NetApp Aggregate Encryption (NAE), key management
- **SnapLock and Compliance** — write-once-read-many (WORM) storage, legal hold, retention policies
- **Disaster Recovery Testing** — failover drills, RTO/RPO validation, recovery procedures, documentation
- **ONTAP Troubleshooting** — lag monitoring, bandwidth optimization, Snapshot management, RAID recovery
