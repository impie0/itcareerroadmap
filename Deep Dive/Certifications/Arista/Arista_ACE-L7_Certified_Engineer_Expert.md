## About

The ACE:L7 (Arista Certified Engineer — Level 7) is the expert-level certification in the Arista Academy track. It sits at the pinnacle of Arista's certification hierarchy and is designed for senior network engineers and architects who can deliver advanced design and architecture solutions across multiple network domains and use cases.

ACE:L7 validates expertise in:
- Modern networking design, architecture, and implementation
- Advanced Arista EOS (Extensible Operating System) configuration and automation
- Data center leaf-spine fabric design and deployment
- External connectivity and campus networking
- Network automation and DevOps practices
- CloudVision platform management and analytics

Unlike lower levels that require prerequisite courses, ACE:L7 has no mandatory course requirement but does require current or past ACE:L6 certification (or equivalent advanced experience) as a prerequisite. This certification is performance-based and evaluates real-world problem-solving capabilities in a lab environment rather than multiple-choice questions.

## Domain Context

The ACE:L7 sits within Arista Networks' certification ecosystem, which emphasizes practical, hands-on expertise in modern cloud and data center networking. Arista focuses heavily on:

- **Data Center Networking**: Leaf-spine topologies, EVPN (Ethernet VPN), and VXLAN overlay networks
- **Cloud and Virtualization**: Integration with public cloud providers and multi-cloud architectures
- **Automation & Programmability**: EOS CLI, Python scripting, and CloudVision automation
- **Network Operations**: CloudVision Provisioning and Telemetry
- **Security & Compliance**: Network segmentation, firewalls, and DDoS mitigation

The ACE:L7 is targeted at architects and senior engineers who design enterprise-scale networks, manage carrier-grade deployments, and lead network transformation initiatives.

## Topics Covered

### Core Areas

1. **Advanced EOS Configuration**
   - Complex routing protocols (BGP, OSPF, IS-IS)
   - EVPN and VXLAN design and troubleshooting
   - Service routing and traffic engineering
   - QoS and traffic classification

2. **Data Center Fabric Design**
   - Multi-tier and leaf-spine architectures
   - Scale-out network design
   - Redundancy and high availability
   - Load balancing and convergence optimization

3. **External Connectivity**
   - WAN edge design and integration
   - Multi-provider and hybrid cloud connectivity
   - BGP policy and route optimization
   - DCI (Data Center Interconnect) design

4. **Campus Networking**
   - Campus LAN design and deployment
   - User access and mobile client management
   - Wired and wireless integration
   - Campus security policies

5. **CloudVision Platform**
   - CloudVision Provisioning workflows
   - Network analytics and telemetry
   - Intent-based networking
   - End-to-end network visibility

6. **Automation & Programmability**
   - EOS API and eAPI usage
   - Python automation scripts
   - Ansible and infrastructure-as-code patterns
   - Network as a service (NaaS) concepts

7. **L4–L7 Services**
   - Application-aware networking
   - DPI (Deep Packet Inspection)
   - Application firewalling
   - Advanced threat prevention

8. **Security & Compliance**
   - Network segmentation strategies
   - Encryption and data protection
   - Compliance and audit logging
   - Zero-trust network design
