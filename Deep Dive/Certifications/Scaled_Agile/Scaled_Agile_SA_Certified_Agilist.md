## About

The Scaled Agile Certified Agilist (SA) is the core professional certification for enterprise leaders implementing the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe). Launched as part of SAFe's evolution and refreshed in SAFe 6.0 (March 2023), the SA certification requires a mandatory 2-day instructor-led Leading SAFe course delivered by Scaled Agile Practice Consultants (SPCs), followed by a 90-minute online exam. The current version integrates AI fundamentals and decision-support techniques to reflect modern enterprise agile practices. Unlike entry-level SAFe certifications (such as SAFe Practitioner), the SA is positioned as the credential for leaders responsible for driving transformation at program and enterprise scale — program managers, scrum masters, product owners, and coaches. The certification is valid for 1 year and must be renewed annually.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint topics (aligned to SAFe 6.0):

- **Lean-Agile Leadership** — SAFe core values, Lean-Agile mindset, leadership principles, change leadership (30% weight)
- **Organizational Agility** — Lean business operations, strategy agility, Lean-thinking culture, team structure (20% weight)
- **Continuous Learning Culture** — Innovation culture, relentless improvement, learning organization (15% weight)
- **Lean Portfolio Management** — Strategy and investment funding, portfolio operations, Lean governance (20% weight)
- **Agile Release Trains (ARTs)** — ART structure, planning, synchronization, execution, metrics (10% weight)
- **AI Integration (SAFe 6.0+)** — AI fundamentals for agile leaders, SAFe CoPilot AI assistant, AI-augmented decision-making (5% weight)
