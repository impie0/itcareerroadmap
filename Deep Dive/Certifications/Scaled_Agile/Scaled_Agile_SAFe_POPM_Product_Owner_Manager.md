## About

The SAFe Product Owner / Product Manager (POPM) certification is the core credential for product-focused roles implementing the Scaled Agile Framework. Launched with SAFe 6.0 alignment, the POPM certification requires a mandatory 2-day instructor-led course delivered by Scaled Agile Practice Consultants (SPCs), followed by a 90-minute online proctored exam covering 45 questions. The certification validates competency in defining product vision, writing and elaborating user stories using INVEST criteria, managing Agile Team and Agile Release Train (ART) backlogs, prioritizing work with Weighted Shortest Job First (WSJF), facilitating Program Increment (PI) Planning, and applying Customer Centricity and Design Thinking principles. Valid for 1 year; annual renewal required via exam retake ($195) or renewal assessment. Ideal for product owners, product managers, business analysts, and agile team leads operating within large-scale agile enterprises.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint topics (SAFe 6.0 POPM):

- **Product Owner Role in Agile Team** — Story refinement, iteration planning, backlog grooming, team ceremonies (20% weight)
- **User Story Writing (INVEST Criteria)** — Feature decomposition, story writing, acceptance criteria, story elaboration (15% weight)
- **Feature and Story Elaboration** — Splitting features into stories, refining work for iteration delivery (12% weight)
- **Product Vision and Roadmap** — Defining product vision, roadmap management, vision-to-execution alignment (15% weight)
- **Program Increment (PI) Planning** — PI Planning events, ART synchronization, feature ownership, iteration commitments (15% weight)
- **Iteration Planning and Retrospectives** — Sprint planning, daily standups, retrospectives, continuous improvement (10% weight)
- **Customer Centricity and Design Thinking** — Empathy mapping, customer journey mapping, design thinking in product delivery (8% weight)
- **Release on Demand** — DevOps alignment, feature toggles, continuous deployment, release planning (3% weight)
- **ART Backlog Management** — Backlog grooming at ART scale, feature prioritization, dependency management (2% weight)

*Source:* [Official SAFe POPM 6.0 Exam Study Guide ↗](https://support.scaledagile.com/en/articles/9791406-exam-study-guide-popm-6-0-safe-product-owner-product-manager)
