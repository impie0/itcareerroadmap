## About

The Certified SAFe Practice Consultant (SPC) is Scaled Agile's flagship professional-level credential for lean-agile transformation leaders. SPCs complete a mandatory 4-day Implementing SAFe course and pass a proctored exam to become licensed instructors and consultants. Unlike entry-level SAFe certifications (like Agilist or Practitioner), the SPC requires hands-on instructor delivery, making it the recognized credential for organizational change agents, agile coaches, and enterprise-scale transformation consultants. Released in 2013, it evolved with the framework—current SPCs hold SAFe 6.0 credentials. The certification is valid for 2 years and requires annual membership ($895) to maintain teaching rights.
