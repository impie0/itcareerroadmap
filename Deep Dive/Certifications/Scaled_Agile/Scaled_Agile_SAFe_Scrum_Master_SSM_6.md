## About

The SAFe 6.0 Scrum Master (SSM) certification validates expertise in applying Scrum practices within a large-scale Agile framework (SAFe) — the most widely adopted scaled Agile methodology in enterprise environments. The cert is aimed at team-level Scrum Masters, Agile Coaches, and technical leads who work in Agile Release Trains (ARTs) within SAFe-governed organizations. Prerequisite is a mandatory 2-day in-person or virtual course taught by a Scaled Agile Program Consultant (SPC). The exam covers Scrum ceremonies, team coaching, PI planning, iteration execution, and lean-agile principles. Valid for 1 year; renewal requires annual membership in the SAFe Community Platform.

---

## Domain context — Agile/Scrum/SAFe

Enterprise-scale Agile framework (SAFe) used by 650,000+ practitioners globally in financial services, technology, healthcare, manufacturing, and government sectors. SAFe extends Scrum to program and portfolio levels, adding synchronization across teams (PI Planning), release trains (ARTs), and governance layers. The Scrum Master role in SAFe acts as a servant leader, team coach, and impediment remover within a larger scaled structure.

## Topics covered

The SSM 6.0 exam covers six major domains (% weight derived from official blueprint):

- **Introducing Scrum in SAFe** — SAFe principles, Lean-Agile mindset, iterative delivery, feedback loops
- **Characterizing the Role of the Scrum Master** — Servant leadership, coaching, facilitation, team dynamics, conflict resolution
- **Experiencing PI Planning** — Program Increment planning ceremonies, team synchronization, dependency management, business value alignment
- **Facilitating Iteration Execution** — Sprint ceremonies (standup, planning, review, retro), Scrum artifacts (Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog, Increment), velocity tracking
- **Finishing the PI** — PI objectives, iteration goals, completion metrics, inspect-and-adapt retrospectives, continuous improvement
- **Coaching the Agile Team** — Removing impediments, enabling self-organization, fostering team psychological safety, building trust, mentoring

*Source:* [Official Exam Study Guide — SSM (6.0) ↗](https://support.scaledagile.com/en/articles/9791385-exam-study-guide-ssm-6-0-safe-scrum-master)
