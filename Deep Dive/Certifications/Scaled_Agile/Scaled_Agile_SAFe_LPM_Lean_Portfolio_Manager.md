## About

The Certified SAFe Lean Portfolio Manager (LPM) is an advanced professional-level credential for executives, portfolio managers, enterprise architects, and strategic leaders who oversee portfolio-level investment decisions, governance, and lean operations. The 2-day LPM course teaches participants how to implement Lean Portfolio Management principles—Strategy and Investment Funding, Agile Portfolio Operations, and Lean Governance—to align enterprise strategy with funded, executable work. Unlike entry-level SAFe certifications (Agilist, Practitioner, Scrum Master), the LPM targets the senior leadership cadre responsible for managing multiple value streams, epic funding, portfolio Kanban, and continuous portfolio improvement. Released in SAFe 5.0 (2019) and updated for SAFe 6.0 (2023), the LPM is valid for 1 year and requires annual renewal via the SAFe Community Platform. It is a vendor-neutral framework certification delivered by authorized SAFe partners and sits at the intersection of enterprise strategy, agile operations, and financial governance.

## Topics covered

- **Lean Portfolio Management fundamentals** — Core principles, mindset, and the three-pillar model (Strategy and Investment Funding, Agile Portfolio Operations, Lean Governance)
- **Portfolio Vision, Strategy, and Themes** — Creating strategic alignment, defining portfolio vision, establishing strategic themes, and connecting enterprise goals to work
- **Value Stream identification and organization** — Organizing around value streams, identifying dependencies, establishing value stream budgets and governance structures
- **Strategy and Investment Funding** — Budgeting models (participatory budgeting, lean budget guardrails), investment horizons, and funding flow
- **Portfolio Kanban system** — Portfolio flow, epic lifecycle, work-in-progress (WIP) limits, and continuous portfolio improvement
- **Epic prioritization and governance** — Epic identification, business case evaluation, economic prioritization, and lean decision-making
- **Lean governance and compliance** — Governance cadences, decision-making frameworks, risk management, audit trails, and compliance within agile structures
- **OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) in SAFe** — Setting portfolio-level objectives, cascading key results, measurement, and alignment across value streams
- **Portfolio Kanban** — Portfolio canvas, epic lifecycle states (backlog, analysis, implementation, validation, launch, done), work-in-progress management
- **Participatory Budgeting and Budget Guardrails** — Funding distribution, lean guardrails, horizon-based budgeting (Immediate, Near-term, Future), and budget allocation to value streams
- **Portfolio metrics and KPIs** — Portfolio-level KPIs, epic progress tracking, flow metrics, business value realization, and continuous improvement data
- **VersionOne/Rally/Jira Align tooling** — SAFe-aligned tools for portfolio visibility, roadmapping, and cross-team coordination
- **Organizing value streams vs. projects** — Moving from project-based to value stream-based organizational models
- **Portfolio Flow and Continuous Portfolio Improvement** — Inspection and adaptation at portfolio level, metrics-driven decision-making, and systemic optimization
