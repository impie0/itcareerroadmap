# Cloudflare Accredited Services Architect

**`ASA`** · ● **Active** · **Expert** · _Cloudflare_

> Cloudflare's Accredited Services Architect (ASA) is the advanced vendor credential for architects and senior engineers who design, validate, and implement enterprise-scale Cloudflare solutions. Launched May 2025, the ASA comprises two modules—Security and Performance—and requires prior completion of the Accredited Configuration Engineer (ACE). It validates expertise in Zero Trust architecture, WAF/DDoS/bot mitigation design, edge computing, multi-tenant solutions, and business-critical compliance/performance integration.

## About

The Cloudflare Accredited Services Architect (ASA) is the expert-tier credential in Cloudflare's partner certification hierarchy. Launched in May 2025, the ASA targets Solutions Architects, Senior Engineers, and Technical Leaders who design comprehensive Cloudflare deployments for mid-market and enterprise customers. Unlike the Accredited Configuration Engineer (ACE), which emphasizes hands-on implementation and troubleshooting, the ASA validates the strategic and architectural decisions required to deploy Cloudflare at scale—including edge computing, Zero Trust frameworks, security/performance trade-offs, and compliance-aware design. The certification is delivered via Cloudflare University in both self-paced web-based (WBT) and instructor-led training (ILT) formats. It sits at the apex of Cloudflare's publicly available partner certifications and appeals to architects, pre-sales leaders, and customer success teams managing complex migrations and multi-tenant environments.

## Topics covered

Cloudflare's ASA curriculum is delivered in two parallel modules. While the vendor does not publish a formal exam blueprint, the training modules and community references indicate these topics:

**ASA - Security Module**

- **Cloudflare Architecture Fundamentals** — Edge network topology, regional data centers, anycast routing, edge-to-origin failover
- **Zero Trust One / SASE Architecture** — Identity-based access control, Cloudflare One positioning, Secure Web Gateway, Gateway policies, device trust, risk scores
- **Web Application Firewall (WAF) at Scale** — Managed rulesets, custom rule logic, OWASP Top 10 mitigation, threat score tuning, API protection
- **DDoS Mitigation Architecture** — Attack vectors (volumetric, protocol, application), mitigation strategies, advanced mode configuration, BGP Anycast
- **Bot Mitigation and Threat Intelligence** — Bot scores, behavior analysis, API bots vs. malicious bots, threat feed integration
- **Data Privacy and Compliance** — GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, data residency, encryption at rest and in transit, audit logging
- **Multi-Tenant and MSP Architecture** — Tenant isolation, billing integration, permission models, centralized threat management

**ASA - Performance Module**

- **Content Delivery Network (CDN) Design** — Cache hierarchies, origin shield, tiered caching, geographic routing, performance optimization
- **Cache Architecture and Rules** — Page Rules vs. Cache Rules decision trees, TTL strategies, cache keys, origin pulls, ETag handling
- **Cloudflare Workers and Serverless Architecture** — Worker use cases, request/response transformation, API integration, KV storage, durable objects
- **Performance Metrics and Optimization** — Core Web Vitals, LCP/FID/CLS, analytics instrumentation, real-user monitoring (RUM) vs. synthetic
- **Origin Optimization** — Connection pooling, HTTP/2, gzip compression, image optimization, origin caching headers
- **Load Balancing and Failover** — Active-active vs. active-passive, health checks, geographic failover, latency-based routing
- **Cost Optimization** — Bandwidth modeling, origin traffic reduction, cache hit ratio optimization, WAF efficiency
- **Customer Solution Design** — Translating requirements to architecture, cost-benefit trade-offs, migration planning, stakeholder communication
