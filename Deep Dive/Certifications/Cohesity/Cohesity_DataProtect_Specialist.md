## About

The Cohesity Certified Protection Associate (CCPA) – DataProtect is a professional-level vendor certification validating administrative and operational competency in Cohesity's primary data protection platform. Launched in 2023 following the December 2024 completion of Cohesity's $3B acquisition of Veritas Technologies, this cert confirms knowledge of modern hybrid-cloud backup, recovery, and data lifecycle management. The credential is designed for backup engineers, storage administrators, and IT professionals managing Cohesity deployments across enterprise and mid-market organizations. Certification is valid for 2 years from the date of passing; renewal requires retaking the exam.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint covers Cohesity DataProtect administration, monitoring, and operations across hybrid-cloud environments:

- **Cohesity Platform Fundamentals** (15%) — Architecture, components, licensing, cloud integration
- **DataProtect Configuration & Administration** (25%) — Workload policy setup, backup scheduling, retention rules, archival tiers
- **Data Protection Policies** (15%) — RPO/RTO concepts, backup strategies, snapshot management
- **Workload Support** (20%) — VMware, Hyper-V, physical servers, NAS, Microsoft 365, cloud-native workloads
- **Monitoring, Reporting & Troubleshooting** (15%) — Dashboard navigation, audit logs, common failure modes
- **Disaster Recovery & Continuity** (10%) — Recovery workflows, replication, failover procedures, business continuity planning
