## About

The Cohesity Certified Implementation Professional (CCIP) – DataProtect is an advanced professional-level certification validating deep expertise in implementing and administering Cohesity DataProtect in complex, multi-tenant, and hybrid-cloud enterprise environments. Released in 2024 following the December 2024 completion of Cohesity's $3 billion acquisition of Veritas Technologies, this cert confirms mastery of advanced DataProtect cluster administration, multi-workload protection strategies, cloud archival policies, ransomware resilience architecture, and performance optimization.

The credential is designed for senior backup engineers, data protection architects, infrastructure engineers, and consultants responsible for designing and delivering Cohesity solutions at scale across organizations spanning 100+ employees to Fortune 100 enterprises. CCIP-DataProtect represents the professional tier in Cohesity's certification path (Protection Associate is entry-level; Certified Architect Expert is the highest tier). Certification is valid for 2 years from the date of passing; renewal requires retaking the exam or pursuing the higher CCAE certification.

**Merger context:** Cohesity's December 2024 acquisition of Veritas Technologies created the world's largest standalone data protection software provider (12,000+ customers, 85+ Fortune 100 accounts). Cohesity brands certifications under the Cohesity Academy banner; Veritas NetBackup and related products maintain separate (legacy) certification tracks during the integration period. New implementations primarily target Cohesity DataProtect and the unified Cohesity Data Cloud platform.

## Topics covered

The CCIP-DataProtect exam blueprint covers advanced Cohesity DataProtect implementation, architecture, and operations across hybrid-cloud environments:

- **Advanced Cluster Administration** (20%) — Multi-cluster deployments, Helios management plane, cluster networking, performance tuning, capacity planning
- **DataProtect Policy Design & Implementation** (25%) — Advanced policy configuration, SLA definition, RPO/RTO optimization, retention tiers, archival policies, cloud-tier workflows
- **Multi-Workload Protection** (20%) — VMware vSphere administration, Hyper-V, physical servers, NAS (SMB/NFS via SmartFiles), Microsoft 365 (Exchange, SharePoint, Teams), Kubernetes, cloud-native applications
- **Cloud Integration & Archival** (15%) — AWS S3, Azure Blob, Google Cloud Storage archival, cloud-tier retention policies, cost optimization, cross-region replication
- **Ransomware Resilience & Data Security** (12%) — DataLock immutable snapshots, Gaia AI anomaly detection, cyber vault recovery, forensic analysis, recovery SLAs, immutability enforcement
- **APIs, Automation & Integration** (8%) — REST API fundamentals, Python SDK, Terraform automation, third-party integration, custom workflows

*Cert-specific technologies and practices this exam tests, distinct from shared "Common skills" above.*

- **Cohesity DataProtect cluster administration** — Multi-cluster management, Helios configuration, node scaling, firmware upgrades, disaster recovery of clusters
- **Advanced policy design** — SLA-driven policy templates, dynamic tiering, archival cascades, retention inheritance, exception handling
- **Cohesity SmartFiles** — NFS/SMB file services on DataProtect clusters, WORM compliance, unstructured data management at scale

- **Cohesity Helios management plane** — Multi-cluster visibility, compliance dashboards, resource optimization, audit trail analysis
- **Cohesity Marketplace & integrations** — Third-party app ecosystem, REST API, Python SDK, Terraform, custom automation
- **Cloud archival & recovery** — AWS S3 Glacier/Intelligent-Tiering, Azure Blob Lifecycle, GCP Nearline policies, cross-region failover
- **Performance tuning & capacity management** — Deduplication optimization, compression ratios, network bandwidth allocation, I/O profiling
- **Troubleshooting & diagnostics** — Health checks, log analysis, performance profiling, vendor support escalation paths
