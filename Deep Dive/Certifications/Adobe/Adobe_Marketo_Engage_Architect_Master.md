## About

The Adobe Certified Master — Marketo Engage Architect is the vendor's highest-level credential for Marketo professionals, validating mastery in solution design, enterprise architecture, campaign orchestration, governance, and advanced reporting across large-scale deployments. Introduced in 2023, this Expert-level certification is reserved for architects with multiple years of hands-on Marketo experience who lead complex multi-instance, multi-team, and cross-system implementations. It requires an active Business Practitioner Expert prerequisite cert.

## Topics covered

The exam measures mastery across these core architecture domains:

- **System architecture & scalability** — Designing multi-instance strategies, instance planning, performance optimization, instance consolidation trade-offs.
- **Campaign architecture & lead lifecycle** — Smart campaign design at scale, lead scoring logic, person lifecycle states, nurture workflows, transition requirements.
- **Data architecture & governance** — Data governance policies, lead deduplication, field management, data quality frameworks, team role architecture.
- **CRM integration** — Salesforce sync bidirectional requirements, API integrations, third-party tool connectors, lead ownership, sync flow design.
- **Munchkin tracking & visitor behavior** — Web personalization, tracking data collection, form management, landing page architecture.
- **Email deliverability & compliance** — Email sender reputation, authentication (SPF/DKIM), suppression list design, unsubscribe handling, regional compliance (GDPR).
- **Reporting & attribution** — Multi-touch attribution design, custom reporting, analytics, dashboard governance, metrics alignment.
- **Webhooks & API integration** — Custom actions, webhook configuration, API rate limits, third-party orchestration.
- **Audit, security & change management** — Approval workflows, audit logs, user role design, blast radius analysis, rollback strategy.
