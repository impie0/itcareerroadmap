## About

The Adobe Workfront Core Developer Professional certification (AD0-E908) targets practitioners who configure and develop within Workfront, validating real-world ability to manage intake, approvals, planning, execution, and reporting. Launched as part of Adobe's broader Workfront certification track, it sits below the Expert level and serves professionals transitioning from administrator roles or entering the Workfront development career path. No formal prerequisites required; 3–6 months of hands-on Workfront experience is the realistic benchmark. Held for 3 years; renewal is free and automated via two short online modules.

## Topics covered

The AD0-E908 exam blueprint spans five weighted domains:

- **Strategy & Intake (23%)** — Portfolios, Programs, Request Queues, Custom Forms, Basic Approvals
- **Budget & Planning (22%)** — Resource Planner, Project Templates, Capacity Planning, Work Estimation
- **Execution (24%)** — Timeline Management, Task Dependencies, Status Tracking, Kickstart Imports, Timesheet Configuration
- **User Experience & Access (17%)** — User Management, Sharing Rules, Access Levels, Layout Templates, Connector Integration
- **Metrics & Reporting (14%)** — Reports, Dashboards, KPI Visualization, Data Export
