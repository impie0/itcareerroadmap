## About

The Adobe Certified Professional in Visual Design (Photoshop) is Adobe's official entry-to-associate-level certification for digital image editing, photo retouching, and visual composition. Candidates demonstrate hands-on proficiency with core Photoshop tools—layers, masks, selections, color management, smart objects, filters, typography, and compositing—within a 50-minute proctored exam. The exam is designed for creative professionals, photo editors, graphic designers, and career changers entering the visual design field. Launched as part of Adobe's unified certification framework, it is delivered globally through Certiport's network and OnVUE online delivery.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint is organized around core Photoshop competencies:

- **Document Setup & Management** — Create or modify documents with correct resolution, color mode, bit depth, dimensions, and artboards
- **Layers & Document Structure** — Organize, rename, group, and manage layers efficiently for complex workflows
- **Selection & Masking Tools** — Use marquee, lasso, magic wand, and mask features to isolate and modify image regions
- **Color Management & Profiles** — Work with color modes (RGB, CMYK, grayscale), color spaces, and non-destructive adjustment layers
- **Smart Objects & Non-Destructive Editing** — Apply non-destructive transformations, smart filters, and adjustment layers
- **Filters & Effects** — Apply and configure artistic, blur, distortion, and correction filters; use blend modes
- **Type & Typography** — Create, format, and position text; use character and paragraph styles; apply text effects
- **Photo Retouching & Healing** — Clone, heal, and restore image areas; remove blemishes and artifacts
- **Compositing Techniques** — Layer multiple images, control opacity, blending, and alignment for composite work
- **Workflow & Efficiency** — Navigate workspace, customize preferences, use rulers/guides/grids, configure tools
- **Export & Output** — Prepare images for web, print, and video; manage resolution, color space, file formats, and metadata

*Source:* [Official exam guide ↗](https://edex.adobe.com/adobe-certified-professional/exams)

## Skills validated

*Concrete tools and techniques this exam verifies.*

- **Raster image editing** — Non-destructive and destructive workflows in Photoshop
- **Layer management & organization** — Multi-layer document structure, layer groups, adjustment layers
- **Selection & masking** — Marquee, lasso, magic wand, refine edge, and layer masks
- **Color correction & grading** — Adjustment layers, curves, levels, hue/saturation, color balance, camera raw
- **Smart Objects & smart filters** — Non-destructive transformations and re-editable effects
- **Type & typography** — Text formatting, styles, and text-on-path techniques
- **Photo retouching** — Clone tool, healing brush, content-aware fill, blemish removal
- **Compositing** — Layer blending modes, opacity, masking, alignment for multi-element compositions
- **Filter & effect application** — Artistic, distortion, blur, and correction filters
- **Export & optimization** — Save for web/print, compression, color space conversion, file format selection
- **Workspace customization** — Tool palettes, panel organization, keyboard shortcuts, preferences
