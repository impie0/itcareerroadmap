## About

The Adobe Certified Professional — Marketo Engage Business Practitioner (AD0-E556) validates foundational knowledge of Adobe Marketo Engage platform essentials, including lead lifecycle management, smart campaigns, email programs, forms and landing pages, smart lists, lead scoring, database management, and Marketo-to-CRM synchronization (Salesforce/Microsoft Dynamics). Introduced in 2024 as the entry-level credential, it sits below the Professional (AD0-E555) and Expert (AD0-E559) tiers, making it ideal for marketers new to marketing automation, junior marketing operations analysts, and career changers entering MarTech. No prerequisites are required, though 3+ months of hands-on platform use is recommended before attempting.

## Topics covered

- **Lead Lifecycle & Scoring** — lead creation, scoring models, lifecycle stages, lead status definitions
- **Smart Lists & Filtering** — smart list creation, filters vs. triggers, nested logic, list performance
- **Smart Campaigns** — batch campaigns, triggered campaigns, campaign scheduling, flow actions
- **Email Programs** — email template creation, segmentation, A/B testing, compliance (CAN-SPAM)
- **Forms & Landing Pages** — form design, field capture, progressive profiling, form validation
- **Program Structure** — program templates, local vs. shared assets, channel design
- **Database Management** — person records, field management, data deduplication, import best practices
- **CRM Sync** — Salesforce sync, Microsoft Dynamics sync, field mapping, sync logic
- **Analytics Basics** — campaign performance reporting, lead source tracking, simple dashboards
- **Administration Fundamentals** — user roles, workspace setup, audit trails, API basics
