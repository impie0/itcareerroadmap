## About

The Adobe Certified Expert - Adobe Target Business Practitioner (AD0-E406) certifies advanced proficiency in A/B testing, multivariate testing, personalization, and experience optimization using Adobe Target. Designed for digital marketing professionals, optimization specialists, and business practitioners with 2–3 years of Target hands-on experience, this credential validates the ability to plan optimization strategies, configure complex targeting and audience logic, execute advanced testing methodologies, analyze detailed reporting, and troubleshoot implementation challenges. The Expert-level credential is positioned above the Associate-level (AD0-E408) certification and represents mastery of Adobe Target's full feature set in real-world optimization contexts.

---

## Domain context — Personalization & Marketing

Application of data-driven testing and personalization techniques to optimize digital experiences across web, mobile, and email channels. Adobe Target is the central platform for A/B testing, multivariate testing, automated personalization, and recommendations within the Adobe Experience Cloud. Expert practitioners manage sophisticated optimization programs, develop testing roadmaps, and translate analytics into strategic business recommendations.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint topics for AD0-E406, organized by domain:

**Planning and Strategy (~15–20% weight)**
- Translating business KPIs into optimization goals and success metrics
- Defining test hypotheses and prioritizing testing roadmaps
- Understanding lift, confidence, and statistical significance
- Assessing opportunities for experimentation across customer journeys

**Configuration, Execution, and Management (~35–40% weight)**
- A/B testing, Experience Targeting (XT), and Multivariate Testing (MVT) activity setup and best practices
- Automated Personalization (AP) models — configuration, troubleshooting, and interpretation
- Recommendations engine — behavioral triggers, collaborative filtering, and integration
- Audience creation using AND/OR/NOT logic; audience management at scale
- Visual Experience Composer (VEC) and Form-Based Composer — advanced workflows, VEC customization
- mbox implementation, profile parameters, and advanced targeting logic
- Adobe Target mobile SDK for mobile app optimization
- Activity QA procedures and launch validation

**Reporting and Analysis (~25–30% weight)**
- Adobe Target reporting UI — interpreting performance, lift, and confidence metrics
- Adobe Analytics for Target (A4T) integration and analytics workflow
- Segment analysis and audience performance evaluation
- Translating reporting results into actionable insights and optimization recommendations
- Understanding attribution and multi-touch measurement

**Troubleshooting and Optimization (~15–20% weight)**
- Debugging mbox firing, profile synchronization, and implementation issues
- Addressing VEC limitations and form-based workarounds
- Performance impact of Target on page load and UX
- Privacy, GDPR, CCPA, and ITP (Intelligent Tracking Prevention) impact on Target
- Competitive analysis and benchmarking optimization maturity
