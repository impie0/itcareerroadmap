## About

The Adobe Certified Professional - Adobe Customer Journey Analytics Business Practitioner credential validates your ability to analyze, interpret, and derive actionable insights from multi-channel customer data using Adobe Customer Journey Analytics (CJA). This certification is designed for practitioners who use CJA for business analysis and reporting rather than technical architecture or developer-level implementation.

Adobe Customer Journey Analytics is a core analytics solution within Adobe Experience Platform, enabling organizations to gain a unified view of customer behavior across all digital channels (web, mobile, apps, social, etc.) by combining online and offline data in real time.

As a certified CJA Business Practitioner, you demonstrate proficiency in:
- Creating data views and components for analysis
- Building reports and visualizations for business stakeholders
- Translating business questions into analytical components (dimensions, metrics)
- Interpreting customer journey data across channels
- Performing exploratory analysis and trend identification
- Distributing insights through dashboards and reporting
- Optimizing data structures for business analysis

This professional-level certification is ideal for data analysts, marketing analysts, business intelligence professionals, and marketing strategists who need to understand customer behavior at scale across multiple touchpoints.

## Domain context

Adobe Customer Journey Analytics operates within the Adobe Experience Platform (AEP) ecosystem and serves a critical role in the analytics and insights layer:

- **Adobe Experience Platform (AEP)**: The foundation providing unified customer data and identity resolution
- **Adobe Real-Time CDP**: Supplies audience segmentation and activation data to CJA
- **Adobe Journey Optimizer**: Uses CJA post-campaign analytics to optimize journeys
- **Adobe Analytics**: Legacy analytics tool; CJA is the modern replacement for cross-channel analysis
- **Adobe Target**: Works with CJA for testing and personalization analytics
- **Adobe Marketo Engage**: B2B marketing platform; CJA complements B2C analysis

CJA is purpose-built for analyzing customer journeys across channels (web, mobile, email, offline) and integrates all customer touchpoint data into a single analysis environment. Unlike Adobe Analytics (which focuses on web/digital), CJA is designed for unified omnichannel measurement and journey-level insights.

## Topics covered

### 1. Components & Data Configuration (30%)
- **Dimensions**: Creating and configuring dimensions from data elements
- **Metrics**: Building calculated metrics and standard metrics
- **Date Ranges**: Working with time-series analysis
- **Segments**: Creating segments within CJA for analysis
- **Data Views**: Understanding data view structures and filters
- **Schema Understanding**: Grasping XDM schema basics and event data
- **Component Translation**: Converting business questions into appropriate CJA components

### 2. Reporting & Visualizations (32%)
- **Tables**: Building detailed tables with multiple dimensions and metrics
- **Visualizations**: Selecting appropriate visualization types (line, bar, pie, scatter, funnel, flow, etc.)
- **Filters & Breakdowns**: Applying filters and breaking down dimensions for deeper analysis
- **Trend Analysis**: Identifying patterns and seasonality in customer behavior
- **Cohort Analysis**: Building and analyzing customer cohorts over time
- **Segment Reporting**: Analyzing performance across defined segments
- **Attribution**: Understanding multi-touch attribution models (first touch, last touch, linear, time-decay)

### 3. Data Distribution & Sharing (12%)
- **Dashboards**: Building executive dashboards and stakeholder reports
- **Scheduled Reports**: Setting up recurring analysis delivery
- **Project Sharing**: Sharing CJA projects with team members
- **Alerts**: Configuring data anomaly alerts
- **Export Options**: Exporting data for further analysis (CSV, reporting platforms)
- **Annotation**: Adding context to reports and findings

### 4. Data Analysis & Interpretation (26%)
- **Exploratory Analysis**: Discovering patterns in customer data
- **Statistical Thinking**: Understanding variance, confidence, and statistical significance
- **Journey Funnel Analysis**: Analyzing conversion funnels and drop-off points
- **Retention Cohorts**: Measuring customer retention and lifetime value
- **Performance Benchmarking**: Comparing performance across channels, campaigns, audiences
- **Anomaly Detection**: Identifying unusual patterns and root cause analysis
- **Data Quality**: Assessing data completeness and accuracy
- **Insight Synthesis**: Translating analysis into actionable business recommendations
