## About

The Adobe Certified Professional — Marketo Engage Business Practitioner (AD0-E555) validates hands-on mastery of campaign design, lead management, email programs, smart lists, and revenue cycle analytics in Adobe Marketo Engage. It is the foundational professional-level certification for marketers and marketing operations professionals building and optimizing marketing automation flows. Launched in 2018 and regularly updated, it remains the primary credential for demonstrating mid-career competency in marketing automation on the Adobe platform.

---

## Domain context — Marketing

Marketing automation and MarTech strategy, spanning email campaigns, lead nurturing, demand generation, and revenue operations. Marketo is a core platform in the Adobe Experience Cloud ecosystem and sits alongside other MarTech infrastructure (Salesforce, HubSpot, Klaviyo, etc.).

[Read full deep dive — Adobe Experience Cloud →](../../Ecosystems/Adobe_Experience_Cloud.md)

## Topics covered

- **Lead Management** — list creation, lead scoring, lead lifecycle, smart lists and static lists
- **Email Programs** — email creation, segmentation, A/B testing, deliverability and compliance
- **Engagement Programs** — nurture flows, transitional campaigns, drip programs
- **Operational Programs** — batch campaigns, triggered workflows, system design
- **Smart Lists & Smart Campaigns** — advanced filtering, filters vs. triggers, nested logic, performance
- **Reporting & Revenue Cycle Analytics** — pipeline reporting, attribution, revenue impact, dashboard creation
- **Forms & Landing Pages** — form design, capture, progressive profiling, CRM sync
- **Salesforce Sync** — lead sync, account sync, field mappings, sync logic and troubleshooting
- **General administration** — workspace setup, API basics, audit trails, instance hygiene
