## About

The Adobe Certified Professional – Adobe Animate certification validates expertise in vector animation, interactive media design, and publishing for web and digital platforms. Adobe Animate is the industry-standard tool for creating interactive vector animations and bitmap graphics for games, web applications, and interactive media. This Associate-level cert targets animators, motion graphics designers, and interactive media professionals with foundational-to-intermediate Animate skills. The exam combines practical in-app performance tasks (drawing, animating, exporting) with conceptual knowledge of animation principles, publishing workflows, and interactive design. Adobe's Certified Professional program has been active since the early 2020s, with Animate as a dedicated option for creative professionals.

## Domain context — Animation/Web

Web-based animation and interactive media design — creating vector graphics, frame-by-frame and tween animations, ActionScript interactivity, and publishing optimized assets for HTML5 Canvas, WebGL, and video embed workflows. Related to, but distinct from, video motion graphics (After Effects domain) and 3D animation (Cinema 4D, Blender ecosystem).

## Topics covered

- **Animation Principles** (12 core principles: squash & stretch, anticipation, staging, pose-to-pose, follow-through, overlapping action, slow-in/slow-out, arc, secondary action, timing, exaggeration, appeal)
- **Interactive Design & User Experience** (UX/UI fundamentals, interactivity, user affordances, accessibility, discoverability in interactive media)
- **Document Setup & Authoring** (stage dimensions, frame rate, presets, document type selection)
- **Timeline & Symbol Management** (keyframe animation, tween animations, symbols, graphic vs. movie clip symbols, nested animation)
- **Drawing & Shape Tools** (vector drawing, brush and pencil tools, shape creation, color application)
- **Interaction & ActionScript Basics** (basic event listeners, button interactivity, movie clip control, simple scripting for web)
- **Stage Management & Layers** (layer organization, visibility control, depth stacking, layer effects)
- **Publishing & Export Workflows** (publish settings for SWF, HTML5 Canvas, WebGL, video formats, optimization, file size management)
- **Testing & Platform Considerations** (testing across devices, accessibility validation, performance optimization, proofreading and QA)
