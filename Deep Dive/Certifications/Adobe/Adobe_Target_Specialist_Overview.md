## About

Adobe Target is a real-time personalization engine that powers A/B testing, multivariate testing, and AI-driven automation across digital properties. The Adobe Target Business Practitioner certification validates hands-on expertise in configuring activities, managing audiences, analyzing results, and optimizing experiences using Target's Visual Experience Composer (VEC) and advanced features like Automated Personalization (AP) and Recommendations. Released to support the Adobe Experience Cloud's expansion into personalization-first marketing (2024–2025), this certification is suited for digital marketers, optimization specialists, and analysts working with enterprise-grade testing and personalization platforms. The Professional (AD0-E408) level targets practitioners with 6 months of experience; Expert (AD0-E406) is for 1+ year of hands-on deployment and strategic optimization work. [Source: Adobe Experience League](https://learning.adobe.com/learning-path/target-optimization-specialist.html)

## Topics covered

- **Activity Setup & Configuration** – Creating A/B tests, multivariate (MVT), auto-allocate, auto-target, and automated personalization activities using the Visual Experience Composer (VEC) and form-based composer.
- **Audience Segmentation & Management** – Building, refining, and applying audience rules; integrating Experience Cloud Audiences; lookalike audiences; behavioral targeting.
- **Testing & Optimization Fundamentals** – Designing valid experiments; understanding statistical significance; sample size and traffic allocation; avoiding common testing pitfalls.
- **Reporting & Analytics** – Interpreting lift, confidence, and conversion metrics; correlation with Adobe Analytics; goal tracking; performance analysis over time.
- **Recommendations Engine** – Configuring product recommendations, collection filtering, exclusions, and backup recommendations within Target or as an offer in other activities.
- **Advanced Features** – Automated Personalization (AP) algorithms; predictive analytics; server-side targeting; real-time APIs; mobile SDK implementation basics.
- **Mobile & API Targeting** – Implementing Target on mobile apps; at.js and Target SDK; server-side delivery; context parameters and entity attributes.
- **Permissions, Governance & QA** – User roles and workspace management; approval workflows; QA and preview URLs; activity lifecycle.
