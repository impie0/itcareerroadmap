## About

The Adobe Certified Professional - After Effects is an industry-recognized credential for motion graphics designers, visual effects artists, and video producers. Introduced in the early 2020s and continuously updated by Adobe, this certification validates practical competency in compositing, animation, effects, and rendering workflows. The exam combines selected-response questions with live, in-application tasks performed within After Effects itself, ensuring candidates demonstrate real-world proficiency rather than theoretical knowledge. Widely recognized across digital creative studios, broadcast facilities, and streaming production teams.

## Topics covered

Based on Adobe's official exam objectives, the After Effects certification validates the following domain competencies:

- **Composition & Project Setup** — Creating and configuring compositions with correct frame rate, resolution, duration, aspect ratio, and workspace optimization
- **Layers & Layer Properties** — Managing layer types, attributes, transformations, anchor points, parenting, and expressions
- **Keyframes & Animation** — Creating keyframe-based animations with timing, easing, and velocity adjustments
- **Masks & Masking** — Using mask tools, feathering, expansion, and mask animation for selective visibility and effects
- **Effects & Presets** — Applying built-in effects, adjustment layers, effect controls, and utilizing effect presets
- **Text Animation** — Text layer creation, formatting, text animation properties, and animator groups
- **3D Layers & Camera** — 3D coordinate space, camera positioning, light interaction, and 3D layer depth compositing
- **Track Mattes & Stencils** — Using track mattes (alpha and luminosity) for advanced masking and compositing
- **Blending Modes & Adjustment Layers** — Layer blending modes, opacity, and adjustment layer application across compositions
- **Rendering & Export** — Output module settings, render queue management, and exporting formats (ProRes, H.264, DNxHR, etc.)
- **Adobe Media Encoder** — Queue management, format conversion, and batch export workflows

*Source:* [Adobe Certified Professional exam objectives ↗](https://edex.adobe.com/adobe-certified-professional/exams)
