## About

This certification path validates expert-level proficiency in designing, building, and operating Real-Time Customer Data Platform (RTCDP) solutions within Adobe Experience Platform (AEP). The combination of AD0-E605 (Real-Time CDP Developer Expert) and AD0-E600 (Platform Foundations) establishes a master-equivalent credential for CDP and RTCDP architects. It demonstrates deep knowledge of XDM schema design, identity management, audience segmentation, real-time activation, and data governance in production environments.

**Note:** Adobe does not currently offer a single "Experience Platform Architect Master" certification. This path combines the highest-level RTCDP technical credential (Expert) with platform foundations. Organizations seeking AEP architect credentials typically combine AD0-E605 with practical implementation experience and may pursue vendor-specific Adobe Professional Services certifications.

## Domain context — Customer Data Platform (CDP) & Marketing Technology

Real-Time Customer Data Platform (RTCDP) is a unified marketing-data infrastructure tool that ingests customer data from multiple sources, creates a unified customer profile, and executes real-time personalization across channels. It sits at the intersection of data engineering, marketing operations, and solutions architecture.

## Topics covered

### AD0-E605: Adobe Real-Time CDP Developer Expert

- **XDM Schema Architecture & Modeling** (15–20%)
  - Creating and validating XDM schemas for profiles and events
  - Schema inheritance and composition patterns
  - Custom field groups and extensions
  - Schema versioning and deprecation strategies

- **Identity Service & Identity Graphs** (15–20%)
  - Identity namespace definition and management
  - Primary vs. secondary identity selection
  - Merge policies and identity graph resolution
  - Cross-device identity stitching

- **Real-Time Customer Profile** (15–20%)
  - Profile merge logic and duplicate handling
  - Profile enrichment from external sources
  - Profile attribute management and retention
  - Query and segmentation from unified profiles

- **Audience Segmentation & Management** (15–20%)
  - Building segment definitions via UI and API
  - Segment validation and preview
  - Segment scheduling and recurrence
  - Edge segmentation and real-time activation

- **Destinations & Activation** (15–20%)
  - Destination connectors (first-party, third-party, social)
  - Audience mapping and attribute export
  - Scheduled and streaming activation patterns
  - Data activation pipelines to marketing tools

- **Data Governance & Compliance** (10–15%)
  - Data usage labels and policy enforcement
  - DULE (Data Usage Labeling & Enforcement)
  - Consent management and privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
  - Data retention and deletion strategies

- **Sources & Data Connectors** (10%)
  - Adobe-native sources (Analytics, Audience Manager)
  - Third-party connectors (Salesforce, Marketo, databases)
  - Batch and streaming ingestion patterns

- **Sandbox Management** (5%)
  - Production vs. development sandbox isolation
  - Data and configuration promotion

### AD0-E600: Adobe Experience Platform Foundations

- **Platform Architecture Overview**
  - AEP service ecosystem and microservices
  - Data lake, real-time profile service, query service
  - Relationships between AEP services

- **Core Concepts**
  - Datasets, schemas, attributes
  - Unified identity and profiles
  - Audiences and segments
  - Destinations and activation

- **Basic Data Workflows**
  - Ingesting data (batch, streaming)
  - Basic segment creation
  - Simple activation to a destination

## Common skills at CDP / Marketing Technology · Expert level

*Shared competencies expected of expert-level CDP architects across all platforms (Segment, mParticle, Tealium, Salesforce CDP, etc.), not specific to Adobe.*

- **Customer data strategy** — unified data collection, identity resolution, privacy-first architecture
- **Data modeling** — designing normalized data schemas, handling hierarchical and time-series data
- **Audience segmentation logic** — threshold definition, recency/frequency/monetary rules, lookalike modeling
- **Activation orchestration** — multi-channel campaign sequencing, frequency capping, suppression
- **Data governance & compliance** — GDPR, CCPA, consent management, data lineage documentation
- **Performance optimization** — query tuning, incremental sync, real-time vs. batch trade-offs
- **Enterprise data integration** — connector architecture, API design, data quality monitoring
