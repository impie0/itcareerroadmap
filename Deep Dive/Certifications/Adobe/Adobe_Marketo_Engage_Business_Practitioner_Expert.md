## About

Adobe's Expert-level Marketo certification targets advanced practitioners responsible for designing, executing, and optimizing marketing automation campaigns at enterprise scale. Launched in 2024 as part of Adobe's broader Digital Experience Certification rebranding (consolidating Marketo under Adobe Experience Cloud), the AD0-E559 exam builds on foundational Professional knowledge (AD0-E555) to test deep expertise in campaign architecture, lead management, data governance, reporting, and platform administration. Roughly 18 months of hands-on Marketo usage is recommended before sitting.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint spans six weighted domains:

- **Administration & Maintenance (12%)** — User roles, workspace configuration, data integrity, compliance, platform security
- **Marketing Activities / Campaign Management (46%)** — Multi-step campaign design, program orchestration, email and content delivery, scheduling, testing
- **Lead Management (12%)** — Lead scoring models, lifecycle stage progression, audience segmentation, enrichment
- **Data Management (10%)** — Data import/validation, custom field management, record deduplication, Salesforce sync
- **Reporting (4%)** — Custom reports, dashboards, ROI tracking, performance analytics
- **Best Practices (16%)** — Optimization strategies, governance, troubleshooting, scalability, compliance

*Source:* [Adobe Marketo Business Practitioner Expert Prep Guide ↗](https://certification.adobe.com/courses/1194)
