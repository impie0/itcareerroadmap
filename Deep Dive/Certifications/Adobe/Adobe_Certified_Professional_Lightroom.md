## About

Adobe Lightroom, while one of the most widely used photo-editing and asset-management tools in creative workflows, has never been included in Adobe's official Certified Professional credential program. As of May 2026, Adobe offers ACP certifications only for Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Dreamweaver, Adobe Express, and InDesign. Lightroom remains absent despite significant market adoption in photography, content creation, and hybrid photo-video workflows.

This absence reflects Adobe's positioning: Lightroom is marketed as a self-contained, user-friendly application requiring less formalized professional certification than Photoshop, which remains the flagship image-editing credential. Organizations seeking to validate Lightroom expertise typically rely on vendor-neutral third-party certificates or on a combination of Photoshop ACP (for advanced image editing) and portfolio demonstrations.

## Why no ACP for Lightroom?

**Product positioning:** Lightroom is designed as a simplified, task-focused tool for photographers and content creators who may lack deep design training. Adobe's official certification program targets roles requiring broader creative software mastery (designers, video editors, production specialists). Lightroom, by contrast, is marketed as learnable through its integrated tutorials and Academy resources.

**Overlap with Photoshop:** Any advanced image-editing work (layering, compositing, retouching at pixel level) requires Photoshop. Lightroom's strength is in non-destructive adjustment and library management. Adobe's strategy consolidates credential value into the Photoshop ACP.

**Market reality:** Industry surveys (Heidrick & Struggles 2025, talent.adobe.com ecosystem reports) show that Lightroom is typically listed as a "preferred" or "nice-to-have" skill alongside Photoshop, rather than a standalone job requirement. There is minimal business demand for a standalone Lightroom certification.

## Closest alternative: Adobe Certified Professional — Photoshop

For professionals seeking an **official Adobe certification** in the image-editing domain, the **[Adobe Certified Professional — Photoshop](./Adobe_Certified_Professional_Photoshop.md)** credential is the industry-recognized path. Photoshop covers advanced image manipulation, compositing, retouching, and design workflows that extend beyond Lightroom's non-destructive adjustment scope.

**Why Photoshop instead of Lightroom:**
- Official Adobe ACP credential (exam code: **ACP-PS**)
- ~90-minute performance-based exam with live in-app tasks
- Covers image editing, layer management, masking, and composition
- Recognized globally for creative professional roles
- Lightroom skills are prerequisite knowledge for the Photoshop ACP
