## About

The Adobe Certified Professional in Digital Video Using Adobe Premiere Pro is an industry-recognized validation of expertise in professional video editing. Launched in 2024 and expanded in 2026 for Premiere 2026, this certification demonstrates mastery of Premiere Pro's interface, video editing workflows, and foundational knowledge required for digital media careers. The exam tests practical skills through a 50-minute format combining multiple-choice questions and live in-app editing tasks, requiring approximately 150 hours of hands-on experience. The certification is valid for three years and earns holders a digital badge, certificate, and the opportunity to unlock Adobe's Video Design specialty credential when paired with Photoshop or After Effects certification.

- Transitions and effects (audio/video effects, layering, opacity)

- Color grading and correction (Lumetri color panel, adjustment layers)
- Exporting and delivery (media encoder, output formats, codecs)
- Workflows with Adobe Media Encoder (batch export, presets)
- Multi-camera editing and synchronization
- Essential editing techniques (ripple, roll, slip, slide edits)
