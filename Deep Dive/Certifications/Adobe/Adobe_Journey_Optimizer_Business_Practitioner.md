## About

The Adobe Certified Professional - Adobe Journey Optimizer Business Practitioner credential validates your ability to design, implement, and optimize customer journeys at scale using Adobe Journey Optimizer (AJO). This certification is specifically designed for professionals who focus on business implementation and marketing operations rather than technical development.

Adobe Journey Optimizer is a core component of the Adobe Experience Platform, enabling real-time, personalized customer experiences across all channels through intelligent journey orchestration, offer decisioning, and dynamic content management.

As a certified AJO Business Practitioner, you demonstrate proficiency in:
- Creating and managing customer journeys using AJO's drag-and-drop interface
- Configuring campaigns and messages across email, SMS, and push channels
- Leveraging customer data and segmentation for personalization
- Implementing basic conditions, exclusions, and journey optimization techniques
- Working with message templates and predesigned components
- Understanding AEP foundations and how data flows into AJO

This professional-level certification is ideal for marketing professionals, campaign managers, journey orchestration specialists, and marketing operations professionals who work in mid-market to enterprise organizations using Adobe Experience Cloud.

## Domain context

Adobe Journey Optimizer operates within the broader Adobe Experience Cloud ecosystem, specifically as part of Adobe Experience Platform (AEP). It sits alongside complementary solutions:

- **Adobe Experience Platform (AEP)**: The foundational data and identity platform that feeds customer profiles and behavioral data into Journey Optimizer
- **Adobe Real-Time CDP**: Provides unified customer profiles and audience segmentation capabilities
- **Adobe Analytics / Customer Journey Analytics**: Delivers post-campaign analytics and performance insights
- **Adobe Target**: Enables in-app and web personalization alongside journey-driven experiences
- **Adobe Marketo Engage**: Focused on B2B marketing automation; AJO targets B2C and cross-channel scenarios
- **Adobe Workfront**: Project management and campaign operations coordination

The Business Practitioner level is positioned for practitioners who execute and optimize journeys, as opposed to the Expert and Developer levels which focus on advanced architecture and technical implementation.

## Topics covered

### 1. Journey Design & Management (25-30%)
- Creating journeys from templates and from scratch
- Adding journey activities: start events, conditions, exclusions, actions
- Journey canvas navigation and component configuration
- Testing journeys and managing journey versions
- Schedule-based vs. event-based journey triggering
- Journey performance monitoring and optimization

### 2. Campaign & Message Creation (20-25%)
- Creating campaigns in AJO
- Designing email, SMS, in-app, and push notifications
- Using predesigned templates and drag-and-drop editors
- Message validation and proof sending
- Seed lists for message testing
- Handling message failures and retries
- Personalization basics using profile attributes and event data

### 3. Segmentation & Audience Management (15-20%)
- Creating and applying segments in AJO
- Using Adobe Audience Portal
- Exclusion rules and suppression lists
- Profile merge and identity matching
- Frequency capping and preference center basics
- GDPR and privacy compliance in audience targeting

### 4. Data Sources & Profile Data (10-15%)
- Understanding AEP schema and data structures
- Configuring data sources for journeys
- Real-time event ingestion and activation
- Profile attributes and calculated fields
- Data lookups and enrichment within journeys
- Custom parameters and event payloads

### 5. Offer Management & Decisioning (5-10%)
- Creating and configuring offers
- Offer decision rules and constraints
- Personalized offer selection in journeys
- Offer performance and reporting basics

### 6. Reporting & Analytics (5-10%)
- Journey performance metrics (delivery, engagement, conversion)
- Message-level reporting
- Segment performance analysis
- Troubleshooting common delivery and engagement issues

### 7. AEP Foundations (5%)
- Data governance and consent management
- Experience Event schema basics
- Customer Identity Service (ECID, CRM ID)
- Sandbox and data isolation concepts
