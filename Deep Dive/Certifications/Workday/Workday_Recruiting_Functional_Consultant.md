## About

The Workday Pro Recruiting Functional Consultant certification validates mid-level expertise in Workday's talent acquisition and recruiting module. Designed for professionals responsible for configuring and maintaining recruiting functionality, it confirms understanding of top talent acquisition features and HR business processes. Updated in September 2024, it is restricted to Workday employees and licensed partner consultants (e.g., Deloitte, Accenture); independent candidates cannot pursue it directly.

## Domain context — Recruiting / Talent Management

Workday Recruiting is a core component of Workday's Human Capital Management (HCM) platform, focused on candidate management, job posting, interview workflow, offer letters, and onboarding orchestration. The Recruiting module integrates with core HCM, compensation, and learning systems.
