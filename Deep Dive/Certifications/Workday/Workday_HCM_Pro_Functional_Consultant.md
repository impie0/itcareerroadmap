## About

The Workday Professional Certified Functional Consultant — HCM is Workday's **flagship hands-on professional credential** for enterprise HR systems implementation and configuration. Launched in 2015 and continuously updated, this certification validates the ability to design, configure, and optimize Workday HCM across core HR, compensation, talent management, time tracking, and benefits domains. **This is a gated certification — available only to Workday employees, customers, and certified partners.** Candidates pursue this certification as part of their employer's Workday implementation or career development program, with all costs absorbed by the employer.

## Domain context — HCM / HR Systems

Enterprise human capital management (HCM) systems manage recruitment, onboarding, payroll, benefits, time tracking, performance management, and organizational analytics for mid-market to Fortune 500 organizations. Workday dominates this segment as the dominant modern cloud HR platform, competing primarily with SAP SuccessFactors and Oracle HCM Cloud.

## Gating & access model

**Why this certification is gated:**

1. **Hands-on environment requirement** — Functional Consultant exams require live Workday tenant access (production or sandbox), which is provisioned only to employees/customers/partners.

2. **Proprietary system** — Workday does not license its platform to general learners for study; all training happens within employer-sponsored implementations.

3. **Market protection** — Gating limits supply of certified professionals, maintaining high market rates and job security for qualified consultants.

4. **Vendor control** — Workday manages the quality of "certified consultant" brand through direct partner relationships.

**How to access:**

- **As a Workday employee:** Apply through Workday's internal career development program.
- **As a customer employee:** Work with your Workday implementation partner; they will nominate you for Workday Education access.
- **As a consultant:** Join a Certified Workday Implementation Partner (firms like Deloitte, PwC, Accenture, Infosys, Cognizant, IBM, Slalom, etc.). You will be enrolled in Workday Education once onboarded.

**No alternative pathway:** There is no "self-study" or "independent test" route. You must have an active employer relationship.
