## About

The Workday Pro - Integrations / Studio Developer certification validates hands-on expertise in designing, building, and deploying integrations using Workday Studio, the vendor's low-code / no-code integration platform. This is a **professional-level gated credential**, restricted to employees of Workday Partners, Workday Customers, or Workday itself. The exam tests real-world integration scenarios: data mappings, Cloud Connect connectors, EIB (Enterprise Interface Builder) design, XSLT/JSON/REST transforms, and Workday-to-Workday interoperability. Holders are expected to architect integration solutions across HCM, Financials, and Supply Chain domains. Annual recertification is mandatory.

## Gated certification notice

**This certification is GATED.** It is not available for public enrollment or examination. To pursue this credential, you must:

1. **Be employed** by a Workday Partner, Workday Customer, or Workday itself
2. **Have access** to an active Workday tenant (development or production)
3. **Enroll** through your employer's Workday Education portal (managed by your organization's Workday account team)
4. **Complete** employer-provided training and hands-on labs in the gated Workday Education platform
5. **Pass** the hands-on integration development assessment (delivered via your tenant)

**No third-party course, book, or public practice exam can substitute for this pathway.** The certification validates direct, hands-on experience building integrations in a live Workday environment—something only available to organizations with Workday licenses and partner relationships.

If you do not have Workday access, this credential is not an option. Consider vendor-neutral integration certifications (e.g., MuleSoft Certified Integration Architect, Informatica Cloud Data Integration) or other platform-specific credentials tied to systems your employer uses.
