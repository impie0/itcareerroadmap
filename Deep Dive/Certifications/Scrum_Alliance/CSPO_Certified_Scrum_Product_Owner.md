## About

The Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) is a professional-level certification from Scrum Alliance recognizing practitioners who can effectively lead product delivery in Scrum environments. Unlike most industry certifications, CSPO does not require passing an exam; certification is awarded upon successful completion of a 16-hour Scrum Alliance-approved course taught by a Certified Scrum Trainer (CST). The credential was established by Scrum Alliance as a foundational certification for product owners seeking formalized recognition of their knowledge in product ownership, backlog management, and stakeholder collaboration. Currently, CSPO is active and widely recognized across organizations adopting Agile methodologies.
