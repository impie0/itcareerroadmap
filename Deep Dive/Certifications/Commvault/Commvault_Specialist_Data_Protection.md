## About

The **Commvault Certified Specialist — Data Protection** credential validates foundational and intermediate expertise in enterprise data protection, backup, and recovery using Commvault software. This professional-level certification is designed for IT professionals transitioning into data protection roles, backup administrators managing day-to-day operations, and systems engineers responsible for CommCell environment administration. Holders demonstrate competency in configuring backup and recovery policies, managing storage resources, implementing deduplication strategies, performing disaster recovery operations, and troubleshooting common deployment issues. As a "specialist" track within Commvault's Professional tier, this credential bridges entry-level knowledge and advanced engineer-level expertise, making it ideal for candidates seeking to establish credibility in enterprise backup and data resilience roles. Commvault has maintained a Leader position in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Backup and Data Protection for 14 consecutive years (2011–2025), validating the relevance and industry demand for Commvault skill certification.

## Topics covered

Exam blueprint topics (based on Commvault Professional Foundations & Professional Advanced curricula):

- **CommCell Architecture & Administration** (15%)
  - CommCell components and hierarchy
  - Command Center and legacy console interfaces
  - CommServe, MediaAgent, client deployment

- **Backup & Recovery Operations** (20%)
  - Backup job scheduling and monitoring
  - Recovery from single files to full systems
  - Incremental, differential, and full backup strategies
  - Recovery objectives and recovery point objectives (RTO/RPO)

- **Storage Policy & Deduplication** (18%)
  - Storage policy design and assignment
  - Primary and secondary copy management
  - Deduplication architecture and implementation
  - Data tiering and retention policies

- **Data Protection for Applications & Databases** (15%)
  - SQL Server, Oracle, PostgreSQL backup and recovery
  - Microsoft 365 and Exchange Online protection
  - SAP and ERP data protection strategies
  - Application-aware backups

- **Virtual Machine & Cloud Protection** (15%)
  - VMware vSphere backup and recovery
  - Microsoft Hyper-V protection
  - AWS EC2, RDS, S3 backup
  - Azure VM, Blob Storage, SQL Database protection
  - Kubernetes persistent volume protection

- **Disaster Recovery & Continuity** (10%)
  - Snapshots and replication
  - Failover and failback procedures
  - Business continuity planning with Commvault
  - Ransomware recovery and isolation testing

- **Monitoring, Troubleshooting & Compliance** (7%)
  - CommCell monitoring and alerting
  - Log analysis and event tracking
  - Common failure scenarios and resolution
  - GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 compliance features

*Source:* [Commvault Professional Foundations Curriculum ↗](https://readiverse.commvault.com/learn) and [Commvault Professional Advanced Topics ↗](https://readiverse.commvault.com/learn)

## Certification note

**Clarification on "Specialist" vs. "Professional" terminology:** Commvault's official certification ladder consists of **Certified Professional**, **Certified Engineer**, and **Certified Expert**. The "Specialist — Data Protection" designation in this file reflects a thematic grouping of data-protection-focused exam topics within the Professional tier, rather than a formally named credential code. Candidates pursuing data protection specialization follow the **Commvault Professional Foundations** and **Professional Advanced** learning paths, which emphasize backup, recovery, deduplication, and compliance topics. Upon passing the Professional-level exam, earners receive the **Commvault Certified Professional** digital badge via Credly, which can be annotated as data-protection specialized in resumes and LinkedIn profiles. This file uses "Specialist — Data Protection" to help candidates identify the career-relevant path; all exam details, prerequisites, and skills align with Commvault's official **Professional** level.
