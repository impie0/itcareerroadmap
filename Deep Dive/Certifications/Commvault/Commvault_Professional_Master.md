## About

The Commvault Certified Master Professional validates expertise in designing, deploying, and managing enterprise backup and disaster recovery (DR) solutions across hybrid and cloud environments. Launched in 2020, this certification targets infrastructure engineers, backup architects, and storage professionals responsible for data protection at scale. Holders demonstrate mastery of Commvault CommServe, MediaAgent infrastructure, storage policies, job scheduling, recovery operations, and SaaS workload protection (Microsoft 365, Salesforce, Endpoint). The credential is active and widely recognized within the data protection industry, with Commvault consistently ranked as a Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader in Backup and Recovery for 14+ consecutive years.

## Topics covered

- **CommServe Architecture & Administration** (15%)
  - CommServe role, database management, user and role administration, security settings
  
- **MediaAgent Deployment & Configuration** (15%)
  - MediaAgent roles, indexing server configuration, throttling, firewall rules, storage pool management
  
- **Storage Policies & Copy Management** (18%)
  - Primary copy, auxiliary copy, retention rules, deduplication storage, snapshots, storage lifecycle
  
- **Job Scheduling & Execution** (12%)
  - Backup job creation, scheduling policies, priority, resource allocation, job monitoring
  
- **Data Recovery & Restore Operations** (18%)
  - File/folder recovery, granular restore (GRS), out-of-place restore, recovery workflows, recovery metrics
  
- **SaaS Workload Protection** (12%)
  - Microsoft 365 (Exchange, Teams, OneDrive, SharePoint), Salesforce, Endpoint backup, SaaS-specific recovery
  
- **Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity** (8%)
  - DR orchestration, failover workflows, Cleanroom Recovery, RTO/RPO validation, site recovery
  
- **Reporting, Monitoring & Compliance** (2%)
  - Audit logs, compliance reporting, GDPR/HIPAA considerations, data residency

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests, distinct from the shared "Common skills" above.*

- **CommServe database and infrastructure** — configuration, security, user provisioning, backup, high availability
- **MediaAgent and storage indexing** — deployment, scaling, indexing server tuning, deduplication storage pools
- **Storage policy design** — copy strategies (primary, auxiliary, snapshots), retention, compression, encryption
- **Job templates and scheduling** — incremental backup design, scheduling windows, priority management, resource allocation
- **Granular restore and recovery** — file-level, application-aware restore, out-of-place recovery, Commvault recovery workflows
- **Microsoft 365 protection** — Exchange Online, Teams, OneDrive, SharePoint Online backup and granular recovery
- **Salesforce and SaaS backup** — Salesforce data protection, metadata backup, recovery within SaaS platform constraints
- **Cleanroom Recovery and DR** — disaster recovery planning, failover orchestration, Commvault Metallic integration (cloud-native DR)
- **Compliance and auditing** — GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 considerations, audit logs, data residency enforcement
- **Reporting and monitoring** — CommCell statistics, job reporting, capacity planning metrics, performance troubleshooting
