## About

The **Commvault Certified Engineer** (CCE) credential validates advanced technical skills in designing and implementing Commvault data protection solutions at the enterprise level. Holders demonstrate proficiency in planning and designing multi-site backup architectures, installing and configuring CommCell environments (both legacy CommCell Console and modern Command Center), optimizing storage policies and deduplication, managing replication and snapshots, and troubleshooting complex deployments. The credential is the standard entry point into Commvault-specialist career tracks and is often a prerequisite for solutions architect and master-level certifications. Commvault has held 14 consecutive positions as a Leader in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Backup and Data Protection (2011–2025), underscoring the market relevance of the platform.

## Topics covered

The Commvault Certified Engineer exam assesses competency across these domains:

- **CommCell Architecture & Design** — CommServe, MediaAgent, Storage policies, client-library configuration, scalability planning
- **Installation & Configuration** — Software component deployment, best practices for CommCell environment setup, prerequisites and compatibility
- **Storage Design & Optimization** — Deduplication, compression, snapshot integration, storage-policy tiering, capacity planning
- **Backup & Recovery Operations** — Full, incremental, and differential backup strategies; restore mechanisms; recoverability testing
- **Virtual Environment Protection** — VMware vSphere, Hyper-V, and cloud-VM backup; snapshot and hypervisor-native integration
- **Replication & Disaster Recovery** — Synchronous and asynchronous replication, RTO/RPO tuning, failover testing
- **Monitoring, Reporting & Troubleshooting** — Performance metrics, health checks, log analysis, issue diagnosis, and resolution tools
- **Cloud Integration** — Commvault Cloud (Metallic), AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud native connectors
- **Cybersecurity & Ransomware Defense** — Immutable backups, offline vaulting, ThreatWise deception, recovery confidence validation
