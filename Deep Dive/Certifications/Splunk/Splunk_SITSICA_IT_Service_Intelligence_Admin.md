## About

The Splunk IT Service Intelligence Certified Admin (SITSICA) validates expertise in designing, deploying, and managing Splunk IT Service Intelligence (ITSI) environments. This professional-level certification is intended for experienced Splunk administrators seeking to deepen their skills in AIOps and service monitoring. ITSI enables organizations to correlate events, predict incidents, and automate root cause analysis across IT infrastructure.

---

## Domain context — AIOps / IT Service Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence for IT Operations (AIOps) combines machine learning, event correlation, and automation to streamline IT monitoring and reduce mean time to resolution (MTTR). Splunk ITSI is a platform for modeling IT services, tracking KPIs, managing service dependencies, and triggering automated responses to incidents.
