## About

AB-210 is a new Microsoft Associate-level certification designed for functional consultants and sales professionals who design, configure, and operationalize AI-enhanced sales solutions using Dynamics 365 Sales, Microsoft Copilot, and AI agents. Launched in beta in May 2026 with general availability expected June 2026, AB-210 replaces the retiring MB-210 (Dynamics 365 Sales Functional Consultant) and reflects Microsoft's shift toward AI-first sales automation. The certification focuses on modern seller workflows driven by Copilot intelligence, AI-powered lead qualification, agent configuration, and governance aligned with responsible AI principles across the lead-to-cash process.

## Topics covered

**Official exam blueprint not yet published.** Based on official Microsoft training course (AB-210T00-A: _Accelerate Sales Pipelines with AI in Dynamics 365_) and public Microsoft announcements, AB-210 likely covers:

- **Design AI-enhanced sales solutions** — translating business requirements into AI-first seller workflows aligned with organizational revenue goals
- **Configure Dynamics 365 Sales for AI** — core sales table setup, product pricing, forecasting, and collaboration tools
- **Implement Copilot and AI agents** — deploying and managing AI sales agents, configuring Copilot-driven productivity features and insights
- **AI-powered opportunity intelligence** — intelligent research, lead qualification with AI, opportunity analysis and data-driven decision-making
- **Security, governance, and responsible AI** — configuring secure, scalable automation aligned with governance standards and responsible AI practices
- **Customize and extend sales solutions** — tailoring AI intelligence to business needs, extending Dynamics 365 with Power Platform and integrations
