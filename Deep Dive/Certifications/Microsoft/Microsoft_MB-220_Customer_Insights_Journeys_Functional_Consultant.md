## About

MB-220 validated expertise in implementing, configuring, and managing Microsoft Dynamics 365 Customer Insights – Journeys (formerly branded as D365 Marketing). The certification was intended for functional consultants who design and configure customer journeys, manage marketing lists and segments, create email campaigns, and analyze marketing results using the platform. The exam was last available November 30, 2024, and has been superseded by evolving Microsoft credentials focused on customer experience and AI-driven marketing capabilities. Candidates seeking current marketing certifications should review **MB-280** (Customer Experience Analyst, retiring 2026) or upcoming AI-focused alternatives.

## Domain context — Marketing & Dynamics Ecosystem

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is Microsoft's cloud enterprise resource planning (ERP) and customer relationship management (CRM) suite. ## Topics covered

The exam blueprint (before retirement) covered:

- **Configure marketing applications** (7–10% weight)
  - Organization and management settings
  - Marketing app settings, lead scoring
  - Event registration and configuration

- **Manage segments and lists** (10–12% weight)
  - Create and manage segments using query-based logic
  - Import and manage contact lists
  - Dynamic and static segment strategies

- **Create and manage marketing forms and pages** (12–15% weight)
  - Form design and field mapping
  - Landing page configuration
  - Form submission and captcha handling

- **Manage customer journeys** (20–22% weight)
  - Design interactive and real-time customer journeys
  - Journey orchestration and triggers
  - Journey execution and analytics
  - Conditional branching and audience-based logic

- **Create and manage marketing email messages** (11–13% weight)
  - Email template design and personalization
  - Dynamic content and liquid tokens
  - A/B testing and send optimization

- **Manage leads, accounts, and contacts** (10–12% weight)
  - Lead lifecycle management
  - Lead scoring and qualification
  - Account and contact synchronization

- **Manage events and webinars** (5–7% weight)
  - Event registration setup
  - Webinar configuration
  - Attendee tracking and engagement

- **Analyze marketing results** (8–10% weight)
  - Dashboard and analytics interpretation
  - KPI tracking (opens, clicks, conversions)
  - Journey performance analysis

## Skills validated

*Concrete competencies this exam tested, distinct from shared domain skills.*

- Dynamics 365 Customer Insights application configuration and customization
- Journey orchestration and workflow design using Customer Journeys module
- Email marketing template design with dynamic content and A/B testing
- Lead scoring model configuration and lead lifecycle automation
- Segment creation using query-based logic and audience targeting
- Event registration and webinar integration (deprecated in newer versions)
- Form and landing page builder usage
- Marketing analytics, KPI dashboards, and campaign performance measurement
- List import, deduplication, and consent management
- Privacy and compliance controls (GDPR, CAN-SPAM, CASL)
- Integration with Dynamics 365 Sales for lead handoff
