## CRITICAL FLAG: Certification Does Not Exist

**As of May 1, 2026, Microsoft does not offer a certification with the code `AB-101` or a named "Cybersecurity AI Architect Expert" certification.**

Research conducted via Microsoft Learn, official certification pages, and vendor announcements confirms:

- **AB-101 does not exist** as a cert code or credential.
- **AB-100** (Agentic AI Business Solutions Architect) exists and was released January 2026.
- **SC-100** (Cybersecurity Architect Expert) is the current cybersecurity architecture expert cert (updated April 27, 2026).
- **No hybrid "AI + Cybersecurity" expert cert** has been announced for 2026.

## Why AB-101 Does Not Exist

Microsoft's current certification strategy (as of 2026) uses:

1. **AB-series** (100-level) for agentic and multi-agent AI architecture
2. **SC-series** (100-level) for cybersecurity architecture
3. **AZ-series** for Azure infrastructure and services
4. **DP-series** for data and analytics

No single certification bridges both "Cybersecurity" and "Agentic AI" at the Expert level. A candidate pursuing both skill sets would either:

- Take **AB-100** (for agentic AI design) + **SC-100** (for security architecture)
- Take **SC-100** alone and supplement with **AI-series** associate certs (e.g., AI-102: AI Engineer Associate)
