## About

MB-330 validates the competencies of a functional consultant who implements and configures Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management to meet business requirements. Candidates demonstrate mastery of product information management, inventory operations, procurement processes, warehouse management, and transportation. Passing MB-330 alone qualifies you for the Associate-level D365 SCM Functional Consultant certification; MB-300 was retired in February 2024, eliminating the two-exam prerequisite path. The exam emphasizes hands-on configuration and real-world scenario problem-solving across end-to-end supply chain workflows.

## Domain context — ERP / Supply Chain Management

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems like Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations unify finance, manufacturing, inventory, procurement, and logistics workflows in large organizations. Supply Chain Management (SCM) within ERP focuses on sourcing, procurement, inventory planning, warehouse operations, and distribution logistics.

## Topics covered

**Based on the official Microsoft exam blueprint:**

- **Product Information Management (15–20%)**
  - Configure product masters and variants
  - Set up product hierarchies and attributes
  - Implement product naming and numbering schemes

- **Inventory Management (20–25%)**
  - Configure inventory parameters and journal types
  - Set up ABC classifications and inventory valuation
  - Manage quality control and quarantine processes
  - Process inventory adjustments, closings, and cycle counts
  - Implement inventory blocking and hold functionality

- **Supply Chain Procurement & Sales (25–30%)**
  - Configure purchase orders and purchase agreements
  - Set up vendor management and approval workflows
  - Manage request for quotation (RFQ) and purchase requisition processes
  - Configure sales orders and customer pricing
  - Implement intercompany order processing

- **Warehouse & Transportation Management (15–20%)**
  - Configure warehouse management system (WMS) parameters
  - Set up warehouse zones, locations, and picking strategies
  - Manage inbound receiving and quality inspection
  - Implement outbound packing and shipping workflows
  - Configure transportation management and carrier integration
  - Handle wave and batch processing

- **Master Planning (10–15%)**
  - Configure master planning parameters
  - Set up Planning Optimization (note: legacy built-in master planning deprecated)
  - Manage supply forecasts and demand planning
