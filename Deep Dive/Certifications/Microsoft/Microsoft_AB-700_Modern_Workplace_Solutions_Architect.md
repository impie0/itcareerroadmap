# Modern Workplace Solutions Architect (AB-700)

**Certification Status: Does Not Exist**

---

## Verification Result

The **AB-700 Modern Workplace Solutions Architect** certification does not exist in Microsoft's 2026 certification lineup.

As of May 2026, Microsoft does not offer a standalone architect-level certification specifically titled "AB-700 Modern Workplace Solutions Architect" or any equivalent under the AB-series (which currently includes AB-900 and AB-100, focused on AI and Agents).

---

## Recommended Equivalent Stack

For IT professionals seeking **architect-level credentials in Microsoft 365 and modern workplace solutions**, Microsoft currently offers **no single architect certification**. Instead, professionals typically combine:

### Option 1: M365 Administration + Security Architecture (Recommended)

**Stack:**
1. **MS-102: Microsoft 365 Administrator Expert** — tenant-level deployment, identity management (Entra), security operations (Defender XDR), compliance (Purview).
2. **SC-100: Microsoft Cybersecurity Architect Expert** — security strategy, Zero Trust, GRC, SecOps, data/application security.

This combination covers the modern workplace platform architecture and security design required for enterprise M365 deployments.

## Why No Standalone M365 Architect Cert?

Microsoft's certification strategy separates **administrator** (hands-on operations) from **architect** (design, strategy, infrastructure) roles across different ecosystems:

- **Azure infrastructure** → AZ-305 (Azure Solutions Architect Expert)
- **Power Platform** → PL-600 (Power Platform Solution Architect Expert)
- **Dynamics 365** → Retiring MB-700; no direct replacement for D365 architect track
- **Security architecture** → SC-100 (Cybersecurity Architect Expert)
- **Microsoft 365 / modern workplace** → No dedicated architect cert; role typically combines MS-102 + domain-specific certs

For modern workplace / M365 architecture, practitioners rely on:
- MS-102 for platform administration and tenant architecture
- SC-100 for security and identity architecture
- MS-700 (Teams Administrator Associate) or MD-102 (Endpoint Administrator Associate) for specific workloads

The recommended path combines **MS-102 (Microsoft 365 Administrator Expert)** and **SC-100 (Cybersecurity Architect Expert)**, which together validate the design and governance capabilities expected at the architect level in modern workplace scenarios.

No singular replacement for "modern workplace architect" exists in Microsoft's portfolio. Instead, the role is constructed from domain-specific expert certifications and hands-on deployment experience.
