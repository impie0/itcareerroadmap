## About

The MB-700 is an expert-level certification for Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations professionals who architect, design, and oversee implementation of enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions. Candidates advise stakeholders, translate business requirements into scalable solutions, and manage complex implementations across finance, supply chain, and commerce modules. This exam validates deep knowledge of the D365 F&O ecosystem, Copilot/AI agents, and industry-specific domain expertise. **Note:** This exam retires June 30, 2026; Microsoft is guiding candidates toward AB-100 for future expert certifications.

## Domain context — Architecture/ERP

Enterprise resource planning architecture—designing, deploying, and scaling integrated business applications (D365 F&O, SAP, Oracle) across finance, supply chain, manufacturing, and commerce. D365 sits within the broader Microsoft Dynamics 365 ecosystem.

## Topics covered

Based on the official exam blueprint, the MB-700 evaluates four core domains:

- **Architect Solutions (20–25%)** — Design end-to-end system architectures aligned with business requirements; evaluate trade-offs; plan integration with Power Platform and legacy systems.
- **Define Solution Strategies (40–45%)** — Create implementation roadmaps, set governance frameworks, prioritize features, manage scope and stakeholder expectations.
- **Manage Implementations (15–20%)** — Oversee project execution, coordinate teams, allocate resources, mitigate risks, manage change across finance and supply chain modules.
- **Manage Testing (10–15%)** — Validate functionality, ensure data integrity, confirm solutions meet requirements before cutover.

## Retirement Transition Notes

Given MB-700's June 30, 2026 retirement, candidates should:

1. **Sit the exam before June 30, 2026** if pursuing the credential for immediate career lift.
2. **Plan AB-100 (Agentic AI Business Solutions Architect)** as the successor flagship expert cert.
3. **Consider SAP S/4HANA or Oracle Cloud certifications** if seeking vendor-portable ERP architecture credentials beyond Microsoft.
4. **Leverage existing D365 hands-on experience** for AB-100 prep (significant overlap in architecture thinking and stakeholder management).
