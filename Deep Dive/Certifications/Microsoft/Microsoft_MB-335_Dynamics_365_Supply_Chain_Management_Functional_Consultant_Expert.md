## About

The MB-335 certification validates expertise in advanced supply chain management within Microsoft Dynamics 365. Candidates demonstrate ability to design, configure, and optimize complex supply chain solutions including master planning, manufacturing, warehouse management, quality control, cost management, and production control. Released October 2022, this expert-level credential requires the MB-330 Associate certification as prerequisite foundation. As of May 2026, this certification is in its final 2 months before retirement.

---

## Domain context — ERP / Supply Chain

Specialized knowledge of enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems and supply chain optimization across procurement, inventory, production, logistics, and distribution. Covers demand planning, supply chain visibility, manufacturing execution, and financial integration with supply operations.

## Topics covered

Per Microsoft's official exam blueprint:

- **Configure products and production** (15–20%) — Product definitions, bill of materials (BOM), routing, production prerequisites, formula-based products
- **Configure production control** (15–20%) — Production orders, production scheduling, advanced planning and scheduling, intercompany production
- **Implement supply chain management features** (15–20%) — Master scheduling, production control, costing, cost rollup, version management
- **Configure warehouse management** (15–20%) — Warehouse setup, advanced warehouse operations, mobile warehouse app, shipping integrations
- **Configure quality and cost management** (10–15%) — Quality processes, non-conformance, testing plans, cost accounting, absorption costing
- **Implement supply chain insights** (10–15%) — Demand-driven planning, production scheduling, supply chain visibility, analytics and reporting
