## About

MB-820 certifies developers who design, develop, test, and maintain solutions extending Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. The exam validates competency in the AL (Application Language) programming model, Business Central architecture, app development, system integration with Power Platform, and application lifecycle management. Targeted at experienced software developers new to Business Central or those deepening expertise in the platform; 8–12 weeks of focused study is typical for developers transitioning from other languages.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint (domain weights):

- **Describe Business Central** (10–15%)
  - Platform architecture, licensing, deployment models, role-based security.

- **Install, develop, and deploy for Business Central** (10–15%)
  - Environment provisioning, app packaging, publishing, extension deployment.

- **Develop by using AL objects** (35–40%)
  - Tables, pages, codeunits, reports, queries; CRUD operations; events; error handling.

- **Develop by using AL** (15–20%)
  - Language syntax, variables, type system, control flow, string manipulation, date/time handling.

- **Work with development tools** (10–15%)
  - Visual Studio Code, AL Language Extension, the AL debugger, test automation.

- **Integrate Business Central with other applications** (10–15%)
  - APIs (REST and SOAP), webhooks, Power Automate integration, data import/export.
