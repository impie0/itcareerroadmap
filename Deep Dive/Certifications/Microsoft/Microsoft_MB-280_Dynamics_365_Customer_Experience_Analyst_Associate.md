## About

MB-280 is a role-based certification validating intermediate competency in implementing and configuring Dynamics 365 Sales and Customer Insights solutions. Launched in 2025, it consolidated three legacy certifications (MB-210 Sales, MB-220 Customer Insights – Journeys, MB-260 Customer Insights – Data) into a single, broader credential. Candidates demonstrate hands-on ability to configure customer experience model-driven apps, manage Dataverse configuration, implement security, and leverage Dynamics 365 Customer Insights capabilities for data-driven customer engagement. This is a critical certification for CRM functional consultants and implementations specialists targeting mid-market and enterprise Microsoft stacks. **Critical:** This certification retires July 31, 2026. Microsoft recommends transitioning to the new AB-210 (Accelerate Sales Pipelines with AI) certification for future upskilling in Dynamics 365 Sales with AI-first capabilities.

## Topics covered

- **Configure Dynamics 365 customer experience applications (35–40%)**
  - Configure Dynamics 365 Sales and Customer Service apps
  - Customize forms, views, and business logic
  - Set up sales pipelines, product catalogs, and quote management
  - Implement service management and entitlements
  - Configure Dynamics 365 app for Outlook integration

- **Implement security and customize Dynamics 365 (20–25%)**
  - Configure role-based access control (RBAC) and hierarchical security
  - Implement field-level security and row-level security
  - Apply security roles and business units
  - Manage team ownership and shared records
  - Configure API security and OAuth flows

- **Configure and customize Dataverse and model-driven apps (25–30%)**
  - Create and manage tables, columns, relationships
  - Implement calculated and rollup fields
  - Apply business rules and workflow automations
  - Configure plug-ins and real-time workflows
  - Manage solution layering and managed solutions

- **Implement Dynamics 365 Customer Insights capabilities (10–15%)**
  - Configure Customer Insights – Data (unified customer profiles)
  - Implement Customer Insights – Journeys (customer journey orchestration)
  - Create segments and journeys for targeted engagement
  - Integrate predictive models and AI-driven recommendations
  - Monitor and optimize customer data platforms (CDP)

## Important notice: Certification retirement

**This certification retires on July 31, 2026.** Microsoft is transitioning from broad CRM coverage to AI-first sales acceleration. Candidates should plan accordingly:

- If you plan to pursue MB-280, complete the exam by July 31, 2026.
- If you already hold MB-280, you have until July 31, 2026 to renew; after this date, the cert is no longer valid.
- **Microsoft's recommended transition path:** AB-210 Accelerate Sales Pipelines with AI in Dynamics 365 (released late 2025) emphasizes AI-driven sales workflows, copilot capabilities, and agent-assisted customer engagement — a strategic shift reflecting Microsoft's AI-first product roadmap.
