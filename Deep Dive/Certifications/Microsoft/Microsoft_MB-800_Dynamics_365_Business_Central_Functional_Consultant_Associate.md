## About

The MB-800 certification validates your ability to implement and configure Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). As a functional consultant, you work across core application setup, financial configuration, sales and purchasing workflows, and day-to-day operations. The exam, renewed in 2026, assesses hands-on configuration skills in a real Business Central environment and is a role-based credential for implementation teams and enterprise customers deploying BC in their organizations.

---

## Domain context — ERP

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems integrate core business operations—finance, supply chain, inventory, projects, and human resources—into a single platform. Business Central is Microsoft's affordable, cloud-native ERP for SMEs, competing with Sage Intacct, Oracle NetSuite, and SAP S/4HANA. Functional consultants bridge business requirements and system configuration.

## Topics covered

- **Set up Business Central** (15–20%) — company setup, fiscal calendars, user permissions, chart of accounts initialization
- **Configure Financials** (20–25%) — general ledger, posting groups, bank reconciliation, tax setup, dimensions for cost tracking
- **Configure Sales and Purchasing** (15–20%) — customer/vendor master data, pricing, payment terms, order fulfillment, approval workflows
- **Perform Business Central Operations** (20–25%) — daily transaction processing, month-end/year-end close procedures, reconciliation, reporting
- **Set up reporting and analytics** (10–15%) — built-in reports, Power BI integration, KPIs, dashboards
- **Configure inventory and assembly** (10–15%) — item master, warehouse locations, stock transfers, item tracking, assembly workflows
