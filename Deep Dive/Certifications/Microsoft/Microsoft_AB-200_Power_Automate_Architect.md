## Correct alternatives for Power Automate architect-level skills

If you are seeking Power Automate architect-level or advanced developer-level certification, use one of these verified paths:

### 1. **PL-500: Power Automate RPA Developer Associate** (Active → **Retiring June 30, 2026**)

The current associate-level Power Automate credential covering cloud flows, desktop automation, AI Builder integration, and RPA solutions. **This will be retired June 30, 2026 with no direct replacement announced.**

- **Exam code:** PL-500
- **Level:** Associate
- **Status:** Retiring June 30, 2026
- **Details:** [Microsoft Certified: Power Automate RPA Developer Associate ↗](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/power-automate-rpa-developer-associate/)

**Note:** If pursuing architect-level Power Automate work, schedule PL-500 immediately before retirement. Microsoft has not published a PL-600 or higher-tier Power Automate cert; the architect track for automation has shifted toward Dynamics 365 Business Solutions Architect (MB-700) or AI-driven solutions (AB-100 Agentic AI).

---

### 2. **PL-600: Power Platform Solution Architect Expert** (Active → **Retiring June 30, 2026**)

The Expert-level Power Platform architect credential — covers Power Apps, Power BI, AND Power Automate at the solution architecture level (not deep RPA development).

- **Exam code:** PL-600
- **Level:** Expert
- **Status:** Retiring June 30, 2026
- **Details:** [Microsoft Certified: Power Platform Solution Architect Expert ↗](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/power-platform-solution-architect-expert/)

**Key difference from PL-500:** PL-600 focuses on solution design, governance, and cross-platform architecture; PL-500 focuses on automation flows and RPA development. **Both retire June 2026.** Microsoft has not announced successors.

---

### 3. **MB-700: Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations Apps Solution Architect Expert**

If Power Automate work is tied to Dynamics 365 or enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems, the solution architect path for enterprise Dynamics solutions.

- **Exam code:** MB-700
- **Level:** Expert
- **Status:** Active
- **Details:** [Microsoft Certified: Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations Apps Solution Architect Expert ↗](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/dynamics-365-finance-operations-apps-solution-architect-expert/)

---

### 4. **AB-100: Agentic AI Business Solutions Architect Expert** (Emerging, Active as of 2026)

Microsoft's new Expert-level credential for AI-driven business solutions, including agentic automation with Copilot Studio and enterprise AI orchestration. This is the emerging path for architect-level automation work in the AI-era Power Platform.

- **Exam code:** AB-100
- **Level:** Expert
- **Status:** Active (launched early 2026)
- **Details:** [Microsoft Certified: Agentic AI Business Solutions Architect ↗](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/agentic-ai-business-solutions-architect/)

**Why this matters:** PL-500 and PL-600 retirement (June 30, 2026) coincides with Microsoft's shift from traditional RPA to AI-powered automation. AB-100 represents the next-generation path for enterprise automation architects.

## Why AB-200 may have been confused with other certs

1. **Naming confusion with PL series:** The PL (Power Platform) series includes PL-500 and PL-600, both retiring. AB-200 may have been hypothesized as an "AB-series Power Automate" cert, but Microsoft uses AB codes exclusively for AI / Copilot / Agentic solutions.

2. **Dynamics 365 MB series:** Dynamics 365 enterprise solutions use MB codes (e.g., MB-700, MB-800, MB-820). There is no MB-200 specifically for Power Automate.

3. **"Architect" aspiration:** The term "architect" in automation typically maps to PL-600 (solution architect) or MB-700 (enterprise architect) in the Microsoft portfolio, not a standalone AB-200.

## Recommended pathway if AB-200 was your target

**If your role or learning goal was "become a Power Automate architect or senior automation specialist,"** pursue this sequence:

1. **Foundation (Fundamentals):** PL-900 (Power Platform Fundamentals) or PL-100 (App Maker Associate)
2. **Developer track:** PL-400 (Power Platform Developer Associate) → PL-500 (Power Automate RPA Developer Associate) — *both active until June 30, 2026*
3. **Architect track:** 
   - **Option A (traditional RPA/Power Platform):** PL-600 (Power Platform Solution Architect Expert) — *retires June 30, 2026*
   - **Option B (AI-era automation):** AB-100 (Agentic AI Business Solutions Architect Expert) — *current/emerging*

**Timeline alert:** PL-500 and PL-600 expire June 30, 2026. If targeting either, register for exams immediately.
