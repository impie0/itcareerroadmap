## About

The Veeam Certified Security Expert (VMCSE) is a professional-level certification introduced in Q2 2026 as part of Veeam's expanded certification portfolio. It validates expertise in designing and implementing secure, compliant, and resilient enterprise-scale data protection solutions using Veeam Data Platform. Unlike VMCE+ (which focuses on engineering and operational depth), VMCSE emphasizes ransomware defense, cybersecurity alignment, Zero Trust principles, and regulatory compliance frameworks. Holders demonstrate the ability to architect immutable backup strategies, implement hardened repositories, enforce S3 Object Lock compliance, defend against advanced ransomware, and integrate post-breach incident response with Veeam Coveware tools.
