## About

The Veeam Certified Security Expert (VMCSE) is a new expert-level certification launching in Q2 2026 that validates enterprise-ready skills in secure, compliant, and resilient data protection. This credential replaces the retiring VMCA (Veeam Certified Architect) as Veeam shifts its certification strategy away from traditional operations and architecture toward a primary focus on cybersecurity and ransomware resilience. VMCSE holders demonstrate advanced proficiency in implementing robust data protection strategies, defending against ransomware attacks, enforcing Zero Trust principles, and aligning backup security with enterprise cybersecurity frameworks.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint details not yet published. Based on training announcement, VMCSE covers:

- Secure data protection architecture and Zero Trust principles for backups
- Ransomware detection, response, and recovery strategies
- Immutable Backup and WORM (Write-Once-Read-Many) configurations

- Access control, role-based security, and principle of least privilege
- Audit logging, compliance reporting, and forensic capabilities
- Secure configuration of Veeam Data Platform components
