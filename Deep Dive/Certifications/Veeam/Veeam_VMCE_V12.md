# Veeam Certified Engineer (VMCE) v12

**`VMCE v12`** · ● **Retiring (Mar 31, 2026)** · **Professional** · _Veeam_

> **Status alert:** Veeam is retiring VMCE v12 effective March 31, 2026 (extended from December 31, 2025). Last exam booking date is March 23, 2026. The certification itself remains valid; however, new candidates cannot take this exam. Veeam has transitioned to **VMCE+** (launching Q2 2026) with expanded requirements: completion of three modular courses (Veeam Backup & Replication, Veeam ONE, Veeam Orchestrator). Existing VMCE v12 holders can use this credential as proof of the Backup & Replication training requirement for VMCE+ eligibility.

## About

The VMCE v12 is Veeam's principal professional-level certification for backup and disaster recovery engineers. It confirms hands-on proficiency in the full Veeam Backup & Replication v12 platform—from designing backup infrastructure and configuring jobs, to managing replication and executing recovery operations in production environments. The cert is widely recognized in enterprise organizations using Veeam (banking, healthcare, manufacturing, service providers). **Retirement notice (May 2026):** Veeam is sunsetting VMCE in favor of a modular ecosystem—VMCE+ (entry-level professional), VMCSE (security/compliance specialist), and VMCEA (architect). Candidates should plan to either complete VMCE v12 by March 31, 2026, or transition to the new track.

## Topics covered

Based on the official Veeam VMCE v12 exam blueprint, the exam assesses knowledge across these domains:

- **Backup Infrastructure & Architecture** — Proxy placement, repository sizing, deduplication, immutability policies
- **Backup Job Configuration** — Primary jobs, retention policies, application-aware processing (VSS, transaction logs)
- **Backup Copy Jobs** — Secondary/tertiary copies, geographic distribution, compliance/archive strategies
- **Replication** — Continuous replication, replica seeding, failover testing, recovery time/point objectives
- **NAS Backups** — SMB/NFS protocol configuration, file-level and image-level backup to network storage
- **Recovery Operations** — File/VM/application-level restore, granular item recovery, instant VM recovery
- **Cloud & Object Storage** — Veeam Cloud Connect, S3-compatible repositories, cloud tiering strategies
- **Veeam ONE** — Backup monitoring, capacity planning, health checks, reporting
- **SureBackup & SureReplica** — Automated verification, sandbox testing, failover testing
- **Security & Compliance** — Encryption at rest/in transit, role-based access control, audit logging, WORM/immutability
- **Tape Integration** — Tape backup targets, off-site archival, long-term retention
- **Configuration Management & Permissions** — Backup server administration, credential management, multi-tenant/RBAC scenarios
