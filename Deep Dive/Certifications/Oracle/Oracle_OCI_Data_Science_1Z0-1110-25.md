## About

The Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2025 Data Science and AI Professional certification (1Z0-1110-25) validates expertise in building and deploying machine learning and AI solutions on Oracle Cloud. This professional-level credential is designed for data scientists, ML engineers, and AI practitioners who architect, implement, and optimize end-to-end data science workflows using OCI's managed services. The exam tests both foundational OCI knowledge and advanced ML lifecycle practices, including AutoML, model deployment, MLOps, and emerging generative AI capabilities. The 2025 version incorporates LangChain, prompt engineering, and OCI's AI Quick Actions as new domains.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint reflects weighted domain coverage as follows:

- **OCI Data Science fundamentals** (10%) — OCI Data Science overview, tenancy configuration, ADS SDK setup, credential management
- **Data Science workspace setup & management** (15%) — Workspace provisioning, project organization, notebook sessions, Conda environments, source control integration
- **End-to-end ML lifecycle implementation** (45%) — Data sourcing and exploration; preparation and feature engineering; visualization; supervised and unsupervised modeling; AutoML; model evaluation and explanation; model deployment; batch and real-time inference
- **MLOps practices & automation** (20%) — OCI MLOps architecture, job orchestration, model versioning, pipeline automation, autoscaling, monitoring, logging
- **Emerging technologies** (10%) — Generative AI integration, LangChain deployment, prompt engineering, AI Quick Actions, responsible AI practices
