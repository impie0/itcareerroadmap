## About

Oracle Fusion Cloud HCM Global Human Resources Implementation Professional (1Z0-1046) validates competency in implementing Oracle Fusion Cloud's core human resources modules for mid-to-large enterprise deployments. The certification covers workforce structures, employee records, benefits administration, compensation design, talent management workflows, learning management, time & labor tracking, absence management, and payroll integration. Active since Oracle's cloud transformation (2014+), the exam is versioned annually to reflect feature releases. Candidates typically have 6+ months of hands-on implementation experience configuring Oracle Fusion HCM in real-world projects.

---

## Domain context — ERP / HCM

Enterprise Resource Planning and Human Capital Management represent a specialized IT domain focused on configuring, customizing, and supporting business-critical cloud applications that manage workforce data, compensation, benefits, talent pipelines, and financial reporting across global organizations. Oracle Fusion HCM is a SaaS cloud platform and one of the 'big three' HCM ecosystems alongside Workday and SAP SuccessFactors.

## Topics covered

Oracle Fusion HCM Implementation Professional exam blueprint (per official guide):

- Workforce Structures (setup, organizational hierarchies, legal entities, balancing organizations)
- Person Management (worker creation, contingent workers, termination workflows, record maintenance)
- Benefits Administration (plan creation, eligibility rules, enrollment workflows, reconciliation)
- Compensation Management (compensation plans, salary reviews, merit increases, equity management)
- Talent Management (performance management, goals, succession planning, talent acquisition)
- Learning Management (course catalogs, enrollment, transcripts, learning paths)
- Time & Labor (time entry, timesheet approvals, leave balances, accrual rules)
- Absence Management (absence types, accrual policies, carryover rules, compliance)
- Payroll Integration (integration with Global Payroll, validation, reconciliation)
- Security & User Access (role-based access control, data security, audit trails)
- Workflow & Approvals (business process automation, approval routing, notifications)
- Reporting & Analytics (OTBI — Oracle Transactional Business Intelligence, BI Publisher)
