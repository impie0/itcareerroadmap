## About

The Oracle Inventory Cloud 2025 Implementation Professional credential (1Z0-1073-25) validates hands-on expertise in designing, configuring, and managing Oracle's cloud-native inventory and material management system. Candidates demonstrate mastery of inventory organizations, item hierarchies, replenishment strategies, physical inventory management, and integration with supply chain orchestration workflows. This certification is essential for inventory specialists, warehouse managers, and supply chain implementation professionals working within Oracle's modular SCM ecosystem. Released in 2025, it represents Oracle's latest approach to certifying mid-market and enterprise inventory operations.

## Domain context — ERP/Supply Chain Management

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) cloud platforms automate core supply chain and operational workflows for organizations of all sizes. Oracle Fusion Cloud Supply Chain Management spans inventory, procurement, order management, manufacturing, and transportation. Implementation professionals design data structures, configure business processes, perform testing, and provide hypercare (go-live support). Inventory Cloud is the foundational inventory and material visibility module within this ecosystem.

## Topics covered

The Inventory Cloud implementation exam focuses on inventory structure, transactions, and orchestration:

- **Enterprise Structure & Inventory Organizations** — Legal entities, organizations, inventory orgs, business structures
- **Item & Product Management** — Item definition, item templates, categories, attributes, product catalog
- **Inventory Transactions** — Receipt, issue, transfer, adjustment, consignment flows
- **Stock Management & Replenishment** — Physical inventory, cycle counting, ABC analysis, replenishment strategies
- **Subinventories & Locators** — Subinventory setup, locator hierarchies, storage organization
- **Supply Chain Orchestration** — Material request flows, demand planning, inventory visibility
- **Receiving & Integration** — Receipt processing, supplier integration, FBDI (File-Based Data Import), ADFDi integration
- **Consignment Inventory** — Third-party consignment management, ownership transfers
- **Inventory Visibility & Reporting** — Inventory inquiry, balance analysis, shortage/surplus visibility
- **Integration Patterns** — Integration with Procurement, Order Management, Manufacturing, Finance

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests.*

- Inventory organization setup: legal entities, organizations, inventory orgs, business structures
- Item definition and hierarchies: item templates, categories, attributes, extended attributes
- Subinventories and locator hierarchies: storage organization, locator control
- Inventory transactions: receipt, issue, transfer, adjustment workflows
- Physical inventory and cycle counting: count schedules, count adjustments, variance investigation
- Consignment inventory: consignment setup, ownership transfers, ROI (Returns on Investment)
- Inventory replenishment: ABC analysis, safety stock, reorder points, replenishment strategies
- Material request flow within Supply Chain Orchestration
- Receiving and integration: supplier integration, FBDI/ADFDi data import, receipt matching
- Inventory visibility and reporting: inquiry tools, balance analysis, shortage reporting
- Integration patterns: GL account mapping, subledger integration, multi-org transactions
- Hands-on configuration of inventory structures without code
