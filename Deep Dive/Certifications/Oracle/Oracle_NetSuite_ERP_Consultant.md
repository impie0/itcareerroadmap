## About

The NetSuite Certified ERP Consultant credential validates professional-level competency in NetSuite ERP platform configuration, deployment, and optimization. Holders demonstrate expertise in financials, inventory, order management, multi-subsidiary operations, analytics, and security—enabling them to design and implement complete ERP solutions for mid-market and enterprise organizations. This certification is recognized across the NetSuite partner and consulting ecosystem and typically signals 2+ years of hands-on NetSuite implementation experience.

---

## Domain context — ERP / Cloud ERP

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems unify finance, supply chain, inventory, order management, and reporting across organizations. Cloud ERP platforms (NetSuite, SAP S/4HANA Cloud, Microsoft Dynamics 365) shift deployment from on-premise to Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), reducing infrastructure overhead while improving scalability and access.

## Topics covered

- **Financial Management** — General ledger, journals, accounts payable/receivable, multi-currency, budgeting, revenue recognition
- **Order-to-Cash** — Sales orders, fulfillment, shipping, invoicing, revenue management
- **Procurement & Inventory** — Purchase orders, vendor management, inventory tracking, multi-location warehousing, item configuration
- **Multi-Subsidiary Operations** — Intercompany transactions, consolidation, subsidiary management, elimination entries
- **Account Preferences & Setup** — Chart of accounts, accounting periods, subsidiary structures, transaction recording policies
- **Dashboard & Analytics** — Reporting, SuiteAnalytics, KPIs, role-based dashboards, custom reports
- **Platform Administration** — User roles, permissions, customization, workflows, SuiteFlow, SuiteScript fundamentals
- **Data Management & Security** — Data integration, backup/restore, role-based access control, audit trails
