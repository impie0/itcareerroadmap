## About

The MikroTik Certified Network Associate (MTCNA) is an entry-level certification validating practical knowledge of RouterOS (MikroTik's operating system) and IP networking fundamentals. Delivered exclusively through authorized MikroTik training centers (ATCs), the MTCNA requires completion of a 3-day hands-on course followed by a proctored 25-question exam. The certification is widely recognized in WISP (Wireless ISP) and service-provider environments, particularly in regions where MikroTik hardware dominates cost-sensitive infrastructure (Eastern Europe, Africa, Asia, South America). MTCNA is the entry point to MikroTik's professional certification ladder, leading to MTCRE (Routing/Switching), MTCSE (Security), MTCWE (Wireless), and MTCINE (Enterprise) at the Expert level.

## Topics covered

- **RouterOS Fundamentals** — System interfaces, basic configuration, console/WinBox access
- **IP Addressing & Subnetting** — IPv4 basics, CIDR notation, address allocation, DHCP configuration
- **Static & Dynamic Routing** — RIP, OSPF configuration, default routes, route priorities
- **Bridging & Switching** — Bridge configuration, VLAN setup, port isolation, spanning tree
- **Wireless (802.11 a/b/g/n/ac)** — SSID setup, encryption modes (WPA2/WPA3), channel configuration, power levels
- **Firewall & NAT** — Filter rules, connection tracking, Network Address Translation, port forwarding
- **Quality of Service (QoS)** — Traffic shaping, queue trees, priority queues, bandwidth limiting
- **Tunneling Protocols** — IPSec VPN, PPTP, L2TP, GRE tunnel setup and troubleshooting
- **Hotspot & PPPoE** — User authentication, bandwidth management, user isolation, accounting
- **Basic Troubleshooting** — Packet sniffing, ping/traceroute, bandwidth monitoring, log review
