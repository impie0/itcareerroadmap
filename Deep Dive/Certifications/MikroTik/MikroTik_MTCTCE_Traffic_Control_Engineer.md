## About

The MikroTik Certified Traffic Control Engineer (MTCTCE) is a professional-level certification for network engineers and infrastructure specialists who design, implement, and manage traffic control, quality of service (QoS), and advanced firewall configurations using MikroTik RouterOS. The certification is mandatory to complete a 3-day official training course delivered by an approved MikroTik Trainer before sitting the exam. Valid for 3 years; renewal requires passing the exam again.

---

## Domain context — Networking

Advanced traffic management and quality-of-service engineering in ISP, WISP, and enterprise network environments. Focuses on packet flow manipulation, firewall policies, NAT traversal, and queue-based traffic shaping using RouterOS.

## Topics covered

Official curriculum includes the following domains and technical areas:

- **Packet Flow Diagram & Routing** — understanding RouterOS packet processing order and flow
- **Firewall Fundamentals** — filter rules, connection tracking, and stateful inspection
- **Network Address Translation (NAT)** — source NAT (SNAT), destination NAT (DNAT), NAT traversal, and PAT
- **Mangle & Connection Marking** — packet marking, connection tracking, flow classification
- **Quality of Service (QoS)** — hierarchical token bucket (HTB), per-connection queuing (PCQ), queue trees, bursts
- **Queue Types** — bFIFO, pFIFO, RED (Random Early Drop), SFQ (Stochastic Fairness Queuing)
- **DHCP & DNS** — DHCP server/client configuration, DNS caching, DNS client setup
- **Web Proxy & Caching** — web proxy configuration, transparent proxy, cache management
- **ICMP & Diagnostics** — ICMP handling, ping, traceroute, bandwidth testing tools
- **RouterOS Tools** — command-line utilities, scripting, API basics for traffic management
