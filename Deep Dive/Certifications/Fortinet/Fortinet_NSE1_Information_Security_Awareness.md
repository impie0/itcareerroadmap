## About

Fortinet NSE 1 (Information Security Awareness) is the entry-level foundation course in the Fortinet Network Security Expert certification ecosystem. Delivered free and entirely online via the Fortinet Training Institute, NSE 1 outlines today's cyberthreats and provides practical security awareness guidance for individuals at all technical levels. The course covers the motivations and tactics of threat actors, explains how attacks impact organizations at the C-suite level, and teaches basic personal security hygiene. Originally released in 2016, NSE 1 was significantly expanded in October 2025 as part of Fortinet's certification program restructuring that reintroduced the familiar NSE 1–8 progression. No formal proctored exam is required; candidates simply complete 5 mandatory lessons and pass the embedded quizzes to earn a 2-year certificate.

## Topics covered

- **The Bad Actors** – Who launches attacks, attacker motivations, and common threat actor profiles
- **CIO Perspectives** – How security threats impact Chief Information Officer strategy and organizational risk
- **CISO Perspectives** – Cybersecurity incident response, defense-in-depth, and security governance principles
- **CFO Perspectives** – Financial impact of security breaches and cost-benefit analysis of security investments
- **Personal Security Awareness** – Best practices for individual users: password hygiene, phishing awareness, safe browsing, data protection
- **Attack Vectors & Threat Landscape** – Common entry points for attacks, emerging threat categories, and real-world incident case studies
- **Key Cybersecurity Terminology** – Essential vocabulary for threat modeling, risk assessment, and compliance discussions
