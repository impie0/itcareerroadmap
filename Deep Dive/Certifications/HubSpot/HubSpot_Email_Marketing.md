## About

The HubSpot Email Marketing Certification validates hands-on expertise in email marketing strategy, tactics, and execution using email tools. This specialized certification focuses on the fundamentals of email marketing, campaign strategy, deliverability, segmentation, personalization, and best practices for achieving strong email performance.

This credential is ideal for email marketers, marketing coordinators, content marketers, and marketing automation specialists. Email marketing remains one of the highest ROI marketing channels, making this a valuable and practical certification.

## Domain Context — Email Marketing / Marketing Automation / CRM

Email marketing is a cornerstone of modern digital marketing because it:

- Delivers highest ROI of any marketing channel (often 40:1 or higher)
- Enables direct, personalized communication with prospects and customers
- Integrates with CRM for data-driven targeting and automation
- Supports customer journey from awareness through advocacy
- Complements content, social, and paid marketing strategies
- Provides measurable results and continuous optimization opportunities

Email marketers who understand both strategy and execution are in high demand across industries and company sizes.

## Topics Covered

- **Email Marketing Fundamentals**: Channels, use cases, strategy
- **List Building & Segmentation**: Building clean lists, segmentation tactics
- **Email Design & HTML**: Template design, responsive design, best practices
- **Copy & Messaging**: Subject lines, body copy, CTAs, persuasion
- **Personalization & Dynamics**: Variable content, behavioral personalization
- **A/B Testing**: Testing methodology, what to test, analyzing results
- **Automation & Workflows**: Triggered campaigns, nurture sequences, workflows
- **Compliance & Deliverability**: GDPR, CAN-SPAM, authentication, spam filters
- **Metrics & Analytics**: Open rates, click rates, conversions, ROI
- **Mobile Email**: Mobile-optimized design and strategy
- **Re-engagement**: Winning back inactive subscribers
- **Email Design Tools**: Using email builder platforms
- **Integration with CRM**: Lead scoring, lead nurturing with email
