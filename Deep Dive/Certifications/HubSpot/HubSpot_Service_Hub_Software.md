## About

The HubSpot Service Hub Software Certification validates hands-on proficiency with HubSpot's customer service and support tools. This entry-level certification is designed for customer service representatives, support managers, customer success managers, and support team leaders implementing HubSpot's Service Hub platform.

The certification covers ticketing systems, knowledge base creation, customer communication management, feedback collection, and service automation. As customer success and retention become increasingly important for business growth, this certification demonstrates expertise in modern customer service platforms.

## Domain Context — Customer Service / Support / CRM

Modern customer service has evolved from reactive issue resolution to proactive retention and advocacy. Service Hub enables:

- Unified customer service across multiple channels (email, chat, phone)
- Ticket management and triage
- Self-service resources and knowledge bases
- Customer satisfaction measurement and feedback
- Service automation and workflow
- Integration with sales and marketing for seamless handoff
- Customer health tracking and at-risk alert detection

Customer service professionals who understand modern CRM-integrated service platforms are increasingly valuable as companies recognize the impact of service on customer lifetime value and retention.

## Topics Covered

- **Service Hub Fundamentals**: Value, channels, customer journey
- **Ticketing System**: Creating, organizing, prioritizing, closing tickets
- **Multi-channel Support**: Email, chat, phone integration
- **Knowledge Base**: Creating, organizing, and optimizing self-service content
- **Customer Communication**: Managing interactions, email templates, communication workflows
- **Automation & Workflows**: Automating ticket routing, assignment, escalation
- **Customer Feedback**: Surveys, NPS, feedback collection and analysis
- **Service Metrics & Reporting**: KPIs, dashboards, performance tracking
- **Chatbots & AI**: Using bots for first-response and qualification
- **Customer Portal**: Self-service customer resources
- **Integration with CRM**: Unified customer view, case history
- **Team Management**: Assigning, escalating, collaboration
