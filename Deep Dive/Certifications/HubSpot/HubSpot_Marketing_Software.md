## About

The HubSpot Marketing Software Certification validates hands-on proficiency with HubSpot's Marketing Hub platform. This entry-level certification is designed for marketing professionals, content creators, campaign managers, and anyone implementing HubSpot's marketing tools. The certification covers core marketing automation, email campaigns, landing pages, social media management, analytics, and lead nurturing workflows within HubSpot.

This is one of HubSpot's most popular certifications and is a prerequisite for those seeking HubSpot Agency Partner certification status.

## Domain Context — Marketing / CRM / RevOps

Marketing professionals are increasingly expected to own the full customer journey from awareness through advocacy. HubSpot's Marketing Hub integrates with CRM data to enable this, and certified professionals understand how to:

- Align marketing and sales through shared CRM data
- Automate marketing processes (nurture, scoring, routing)
- Measure campaign ROI and attribute revenue
- Implement inbound marketing methodology
- Build customer journeys across multiple touchpoints

Marketing certified professionals often transition into RevOps (Revenue Operations) roles that sit between marketing, sales, and customer success—making this a valuable foundational credential.
