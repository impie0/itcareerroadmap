## About

The HubSpot CRM Certification validates foundational knowledge of customer relationship management principles and HubSpot's CRM platform. This entry-level credential is ideal for anyone implementing, managing, or working with a CRM system—from salespeople to administrators to business owners.

The certification covers core CRM concepts, data management, contact organization, company management, and how CRM enables better customer relationships. It's an excellent prerequisite or complement to more specialized certifications in marketing or sales.

## Domain Context — CRM / Operations / Business Systems

A CRM system is the central repository for all customer interactions and data. Modern businesses rely on CRM to:

- Unify customer data across departments (sales, marketing, support)
- Track interactions and touchpoints across the customer lifecycle
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Reduce manual data entry and improve accuracy
- Provide visibility into sales pipeline and business metrics
- Support customer success and retention
- Enable personalization and targeted engagement

CRM professionals (administrators, analysts, specialists) are increasingly in demand as companies recognize the business value of clean data and organized customer information.

## Topics Covered

- **CRM Fundamentals**: Definition, value, business impact
- **Contact Management**: Creating, organizing, enriching contact records
- **Company Management**: Account structure, firmographic data, relationships
- **Activity Tracking**: Logging calls, meetings, notes, tasks
- **Custom Properties**: Extending standard fields for business needs
- **Deal & Pipeline Management**: Organizing sales processes
- **Workflows & Automation**: Automating repetitive tasks
- **Integration**: Connecting CRM with other business systems
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Creating dashboards, measuring KPIs
- **User Management & Permissions**: Team setup and access control
- **Data Quality & Hygiene**: Maintaining accurate, clean data
- **List Segmentation**: Organizing contacts for targeting and analysis
- **Mobile CRM**: Accessing CRM on the go
