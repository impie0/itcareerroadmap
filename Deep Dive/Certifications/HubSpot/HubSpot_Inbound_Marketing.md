## About

The HubSpot Inbound Marketing Certification validates understanding of inbound marketing methodology, philosophy, and best practices. Unlike the software-specific certifications, this credential focuses on marketing strategy and the inbound framework that emphasizes attracting customers through valuable content rather than outbound interruption marketing.

This is a foundational certification ideal for marketers, business owners, entrepreneurs, and anyone seeking to understand modern marketing principles and the customer flywheel model. It's often taken alongside HubSpot's software certifications for a complete picture of HubSpot's philosophy and tools.

## Domain Context — Marketing / Sales / Business Strategy

The inbound methodology represents a fundamental shift from traditional "push" marketing (outbound, interruptive) to "pull" marketing (inbound, helpful). This philosophy has become industry standard and is core to digital marketing success:

- **Attract** customers through relevant, helpful content
- **Engage** with prospects through personalized experiences
- **Delight** customers to drive loyalty and advocacy
- Build a sustainable, relationship-focused business model

Companies practicing inbound marketing report higher ROI, shorter sales cycles, and stronger customer lifetime value. This certification demonstrates you understand the strategic framework that drives modern marketing.
