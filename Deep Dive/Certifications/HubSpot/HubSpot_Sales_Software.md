## About

The HubSpot Sales Software Certification validates hands-on proficiency with HubSpot's Sales Hub platform. This entry-level certification is designed for sales professionals, account executives, business development representatives, and sales managers implementing HubSpot's sales automation and CRM tools. The certification covers deal management, contact and company management, email tracking, call logging, sales automation, pipeline visibility, and sales forecasting.

One of HubSpot's most widely recognized certifications, especially popular with sales teams seeking to demonstrate software competency and best practices in modern sales operations.

## Domain Context — Sales / CRM / RevOps

Modern sales organizations require deep visibility into the customer relationship lifecycle and the ability to automate repetitive tasks to focus on selling. Sales Hub provides:

- Central repository for all customer data and interactions
- Deal pipeline visibility and forecasting
- Activity tracking and sales automation
- Lead routing and task automation
- Sales metrics and performance dashboards
- Integration with communication tools (email, phone, calendar)

Sales professionals with this certification often transition into Sales Operations, Revenue Operations (RevOps), and Sales Enablement roles.

## Topics Covered

- **Contact & Company Management**: Data entry, enrichment, organization, segmentation

- **Sales Automation**: Sequences, workflows, task assignment, follow-up automation
- **Sales Tools Integration**: Calendar sync, email sync, mobile sales tools
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Sales dashboards, pipeline reports, performance metrics
- **Sales Playbooks & Methodology**: Selling frameworks, best practices
- **Mobile Sales**: Accessing HubSpot on the go, sales enablement
- **Compliance & Data Management**: GDPR, data hygiene, security
