## About

The IBM Certified Specialist — Storage Technical v2 (C0005007) certification validates professional-level expertise across IBM's entire storage portfolio. Holders demonstrate comprehensive knowledge of IBM Flash Storage, all-flash architectures, hybrid storage solutions, software-defined storage, tape technologies, and storage management software. This is a vendor-specific enterprise storage certification designed for storage professionals, systems engineers, and consultants who architect, implement, and support IBM storage infrastructure in enterprise and cloud environments.

---

## Domain context — Enterprise Storage

Enterprise-grade storage systems spanning flash, hybrid, software-defined, and tape platforms for Fortune 500 organizations, cloud providers, and mission-critical workloads.

---

## Topics covered

The exam validates knowledge across the following IBM storage technologies and solutions:

- IBM Flash Storage systems (FlashSystem, all-flash architectures)
- Hybrid storage solutions (IBM System Storage DS8800, DS8900F)
- Software-defined storage (IBM Spectrum Suite, Spectrum Accelerate, Spectrum Virtualize, Spectrum Scale)
- Storage management and monitoring (IBM Spectrum Control, IBM Storage Insights)
- Tape solutions and technologies (IBM Spectrum Protect, Spectrum Protect Plus, Spectrum Archive)
- Cloud Object Storage integration
- Storage networking and connectivity
- Data protection and disaster recovery strategies
- Performance optimization and capacity planning
- Competitive offerings and market positioning
