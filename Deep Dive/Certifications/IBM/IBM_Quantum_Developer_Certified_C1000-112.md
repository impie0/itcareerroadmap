## About

The IBM Certified Associate Developer – Quantum Computation using Qiskit v0.2X validates foundational competency in quantum computing using IBM's open-source Qiskit framework. Launched in 2021, this certification was designed for developers beginning their quantum computing journey, demonstrating proficiency in defining, executing, and visualizing quantum circuits on IBM Quantum hardware and simulators. The certification covers Qiskit SDK v0.2X and was the entry-level quantum developer credential until its retirement on September 30, 2025. IBM released the successor certification (C1000-179) aligned with Qiskit v2.X in 2025; new candidates should pursue that credential. This certification remains valuable for historical reference and represents the foundational quantum developer skillset established during the early Qiskit ecosystem maturation.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint (from IBM Training):

- **Quantum fundamentals and Bloch sphere** (15–20%): Qubits, superposition, entanglement, quantum state representation, Bloch sphere visualization, Pauli matrices
- **Single-qubit gates and rotations** (15–20%): Hadamard (H), Pauli (X, Y, Z), Phase (S, T), RX, RY, RZ rotations, gate effects on Bloch sphere
- **Multi-qubit gates and circuits** (15–20%): CNOT, Toffoli, controlled gates, quantum circuit construction, circuit depth and gate counts
- **Quantum measurement and classical registers** (10–15%): Measurement basis, collapse postulate, classical bit association, measurement in standard and custom bases
- **Qiskit SDK fundamentals** (20–25%): QuantumCircuit API, circuit creation, gate application, parameter binding, circuit composition, Qiskit Terra and Aer
- **Running circuits on quantum hardware and simulators** (10–15%): IBM Quantum Composer, IBM Quantum Lab, local simulators (Aer), running on real IBM Quantum hardware, transpilation basics
- **Visualization and analysis** (5–10%): State vector plots, measurement histograms, circuit diagrams, visualization tools in Jupyter notebooks

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests.*

- Qiskit SDK fundamentals: QuantumCircuit API, circuit creation, gate application
- Single-qubit gate operations: H, X, Y, Z, S, T, RX, RY, RZ with angle parameters
- Multi-qubit gate operations: CNOT, Toffoli, controlled-phase, entanglement creation
- Quantum measurement: measurement basis selection, classical bit mapping, collapse postulate
- Bloch sphere visualization and interpretation of quantum states
- State vector simulation and statevector backend usage
- Unitary matrix representation and gate composition
- Quantum circuit transpilation and optimization levels (basic understanding)
- Visualization tools: circuit diagrams, state vector plots, measurement histograms
- Running circuits on IBM Quantum hardware vs. local simulators (Aer)
- Pauli matrix operations and phase kickback
- Basic quantum algorithms: Deutsch-Jozsa, Bernstein-Vazirani (awareness)
