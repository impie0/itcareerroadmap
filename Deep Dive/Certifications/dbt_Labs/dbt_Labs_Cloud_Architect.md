# dbt Cloud Architect Certification

## Exam facts

- **Cost**: Examination fee (varies, typically $150-$200)
- **Duration**: 60-75 minutes
- **Format**: Online proctored exam
- **Passing Score**: 70% or higher
- **Proctoring**: Examity partner platform
- **Prerequisites**: dbt Analytics Engineering Certification or equivalent experience; minimum 12+ months dbt production experience recommended
- **Result Delivery**: Digital badge immediately upon passing
- **Retake Policy**: Available (standard exam retake fees apply)

## About

The dbt Cloud Architect Certification validates your ability to design, implement, and operate robust dbt Cloud deployments at enterprise scale. This is an advanced certification that recognizes professionals who can architect secure, scalable, and maintainable analytics platforms using dbt Cloud as the central orchestration tool.

Introduced in 2026, this certification establishes a standard set of best practices for enterprise dbt Cloud deployments and is designed for senior technical professionals, team leads, and architects responsible for analytics infrastructure strategy and governance.

## Domain context — Cloud Data Platform Architecture

dbt Cloud Architect validates expertise in modern cloud data platform design:

- Enterprise dbt Cloud governance and security
- Multi-tenant and multi-project environments
- Integration with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks)
- CI/CD and deployment strategies at scale
- Data mesh and self-service analytics patterns
- Cost optimization and performance tuning
- Disaster recovery and business continuity
- Collaboration frameworks for distributed teams
- Advanced automation and orchestration
