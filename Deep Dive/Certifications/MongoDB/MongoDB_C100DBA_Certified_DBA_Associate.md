## About

The MongoDB Certified DBA Associate certification validates the skills of database administrators, DevOps engineers, and technical leads who design, deploy, and manage MongoDB databases in production environments at scale. This certification confirms that DBAs understand MongoDB administration fundamentals and have practical experience supporting, securing, and optimizing MongoDB infrastructure.

The certification is designed for professionals who have:
- Foundational MongoDB and database administration concepts
- Production MongoDB experience (6+ months minimum)
- Understanding of database deployment and management
- Knowledge of operational best practices and monitoring
- Experience with replica sets and basic sharding concepts

This certification is particularly valuable for DBAs transitioning from relational databases to NoSQL, DevOps engineers managing MongoDB infrastructure, and operations teams supporting MongoDB in production.

## Topics Covered

The C100DBA exam tests knowledge across these primary domains:

### 1. **Philosophy & Features**
- MongoDB architecture and design philosophy
- Key features and capabilities
- Use cases and limitations
- NoSQL database concepts

### 2. **CRUD Operations for Administrators**
- Understanding CRUD operations from admin perspective
- Write operations and durability
- Read preferences and consistency
- Bulk operations and performance

### 3. **Indexing & Performance**
- Index types and creation
- Query optimization and explain plans
- Performance monitoring and analysis
- Index maintenance and troubleshooting
- Performance tuning strategies

### 4. **Server Administration**
- Server startup and shutdown
- Configuration options and tuning
- Storage engine management
- Resource utilization and optimization

### 5. **Application Administration**
- User authentication and authorization
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Connection pooling and management
- Database and collection management

### 6. **Replication**
- Replica set architecture
- Election and failover processes
- Initial sync and re-sync
- Replica set monitoring and maintenance
- Read replicas and secondary nodes

### 7. **Sharding**
- Sharding architecture and concepts
- Shard key selection and strategy
- Chunk management and balancing
- Monitoring sharded clusters
- Sharding operations and troubleshooting

### 8. **Backup & Recovery**
- Backup strategies and tools
- Point-in-time recovery concepts
- Restore procedures
- High availability planning

### 9. **Security**
- Authentication mechanisms
- Authorization and RBAC
- Network security and firewalls
- Encryption at rest and in transit
