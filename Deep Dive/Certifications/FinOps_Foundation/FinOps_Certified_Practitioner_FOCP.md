## About

The FinOps Certified Practitioner (FOCP) is a foundational certification that validates knowledge of the FinOps Framework and practical cloud cost management. Launched in 2021, it covers the three phases of the FinOps lifecycle (Inform, Optimize, Operate), the FinOps Principles (collaboration, business value, ownership, timely reporting, centralized team, variable cost model), and hands-on cloud cost management tools and practices. The FOCP is often a prerequisite or stepping stone toward the FinOps Certified Professional (advanced level). It serves both career changers (finance/ops professionals moving into cloud cost roles) and cloud engineers broadening into cost optimization.

## Topics covered

- FinOps Principles and culture (collaboration, business value, ownership, timely reports)
- FinOps Lifecycle: Inform (cost visibility, allocation, forecasting)
- FinOps Lifecycle: Optimize (rightsizing, reserved instances, commitment discounts, waste reduction)
- FinOps Lifecycle: Operate (governance, cost allocation models, chargeback/showback)
- Cloud cost management tools and platforms (AWS Cost Explorer, Azure Cost Management, GCP Cost Management)
- Cloud billing, pricing models, and FOCUS billing standard
- FinOps team structure, roles, and personas (practitioner, engineer, analyst, architect, director)
- Cost allocation, shared services, and chargeback strategies
- Reserved instances, savings plans, and commitment-based discounts
- Tagging strategies, reporting, and cost visibility
- Cost forecasting and budgeting
- Fundamentals of cloud cost optimization across multiple cloud providers
