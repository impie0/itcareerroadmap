## About

JNCIP-ENT is the professional-level certification in the Juniper enterprise routing and switching track, designed for experienced networking professionals with advanced knowledge of Junos routing and switching implementations. Candidates must hold current JNCIS-ENT before attempting this exam. The certification validates mastery of advanced BGP, OSPF, MPLS, multicast, Class of Service, EVPN, and enterprise high-availability architecture on Juniper platforms. Typical preparation timeline is 3–6 weeks for candidates with strong Juniper platform experience.

## Topics covered

The JN0-650 exam blueprint covers advanced enterprise networking domains:

- Interior Gateway Protocols (IGPs): OSPF (multi-area design, convergence tuning, authentication) and IS-IS (configuration, optimization, multi-area deployment)
- Border Gateway Protocol (BGP): Route reflectors, communities, policy control, eBGP and iBGP configuration, path selection, route filtering, performance tuning
- MPLS and VPN Technologies: Label-switched paths, traffic engineering, L3 VPN, MPLS forwarding plane optimization
- IP Multicast: PIM (Protocol Independent Multicast), multicast trees, source-specific multicast, troubleshooting
- Layer 2 and Switching: Advanced VLAN design, VSTP (VLAN Spanning Tree), MVRP (Multiple VLAN Registration Protocol)
- Layer 2 Authentication and Access Control: Port security, MAC-based access, 802.1X integration
- Ethernet VPN (EVPN): Route types (Type 1, 2, 3, 5), VXLAN multi-homing, active-active redundancy
- Class of Service (CoS): Packet loss priority (PLP), policers, schedulers, drop profiles, traffic shaping, rewrite rules
- High Availability: Graceful restart, nonstop active routing (NSR), link aggregation, redundancy mechanisms
- IP Telephony Features: Voice VLAN, QoS signaling, SIP integration
- Junos Automation: PyEZ, Jinja2 templates, basic scripting workflows for configuration automation

*Source:* [Official exam guide ↗](https://www.nwexam.com/juniper/juniper-jn0-650-certification-exam-syllabus)
