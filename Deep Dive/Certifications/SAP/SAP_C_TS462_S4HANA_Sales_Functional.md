## About

The **C_TS462** certification validates that a functional consultant possesses expert knowledge in designing, configuring, and optimizing SAP S/4HANA Sales & Distribution (SD) solutions. Launched in 2024 as an evolution of sales functional consulting credentials, this exam tests deep understanding of sales order management, pricing & condition configuration, output determination, billing strategies, availability-to-promise logic, and cross-functional integration with Finance, Materials Management, and Production Planning. It is designed for mid-career functional consultants who lead implementation projects or manage SD configuration in enterprise environments.

---

## Domain context — ERP/Sales

Enterprise resource planning systems orchestrate sales order processing, fulfillment, and revenue recognition across the organization. The Sales & Distribution (SD) module in SAP handles customer master data, quotations, sales orders, delivery, shipping, and billing — it is mission-critical in any distribution, manufacturing, or service delivery business. S/4HANA represents the current standard for new projects; predecessor ECC systems are in long-term support but declining in adoption.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint emphasizes the following domains (equal or near-equal weights, ~8–12% each):

- **Sales Master Data Configuration** — Customer master record setup (billing/shipping/sales areas, payment terms, credit limits), material master (sales attributes, pricing indicators, delivery categories), pricing condition records and scales (volume, customer-material, seasonal), sales document type customization
- **Sales Order Management & Fulfillment** — Standard, blanket, and rush order processing; quotation creation and conversion; order-to-cash workflow; contract and outline agreement setup; order blocking, release, and cancellation procedures; availability checking (ATP) logic and backorder handling
- **Pricing & Discount Strategy** — Condition tables and determination logic, scale calculation (quantity, value-based), rebate agreements and processing, customer-specific pricing hierarchies, promotion and discount logic, tax determination and posting
- **Billing & Revenue Recognition** — Invoice document type configuration, billing plan setup and maintenance, milestone billing for projects, partial invoicing, consolidated billing, credit memo and cancellation reversal, revenue recognition integration with Finance
- **Output Determination & Communication** — Form output design and printing (invoices, delivery notes, order confirmations), EDI and e-mail output, condition records for output determination, SAPscript and SmartForms customization basics
- **Delivery & Shipping Logistics** — Delivery document creation from sales orders, picking list generation, goods-issue posting and warehouse management integration, shipping point determination, transportation mode selection, shipment consolidation
- **Outline Agreements & Long-Term Contracts** — Blanket purchase order (framework agreement) setup, contract release workflow, milestone and call-off processing, collective invoices, contract amendment and termination handling
- **Available-to-Promise (ATP) & Inventory Integration** — ATP checking strategies (stock-based, make-to-order, make-to-stock), backorder processing, stock reservation, Materials Management integration (GR, material issue), demand and supply matching
- **Cross-Functional Integration** — Finance (FI) integration: GL account determination, tax codes, revenue posting; Materials Management (MM) integration: stock reservations, material movements, GR/GI; Production Planning (PP) integration: make-to-order scenarios, production order linking
- **Sales Analytics & Period-End Processes** — Open orders analysis, revenue reconciliation, aging analysis, sales forecast accuracy, month-end closing procedures specific to SD module

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests.*

- Sales master data design: customer master (sales areas, billing/shipping addresses, payment terms, credit logic), material master (sales attributes, pricing data, delivery constraints)
- Sales document customization: document type configuration, text determination, print output setup, EDI/email output design
- Pricing and condition architecture: condition tables, scales, determination strategies, discount hierarchies, rebate agreement processing, tax integration
- Quotation and contract management: quotation-to-order workflow, outline agreement (blanket order) setup, contract release procedures, milestone and call-off processing
- Sales order processing workflows: standard and rush order creation, order blocking/release, cancellation and reversal, document flow and cross-reference logic
- Availability checking: ATP checking strategies (stock-based, make-to-order, make-to-stock), backorder procedures, inventory integration
- Delivery and shipping: delivery document creation, picking and packing, goods-issue posting, shipping point determination, transportation mode, shipment consolidation
- Billing strategy: invoice document type, billing plan setup, partial and milestone billing, collective invoicing, credit memo and cancellation handling
- Output determination configuration: form printing (SAPscript / SmartForms basics), EDI/email output condition records
- Finance integration: GL account determination, revenue posting, tax code setup, open-item management
- Materials Management integration: stock reservations, goods receipt/issue, material movement postings
- Production Planning integration: make-to-order link, production order creation, demand/supply matching
- Period-end processes: open orders analysis, revenue reconciliation, aging reports, month-end closing in SD
