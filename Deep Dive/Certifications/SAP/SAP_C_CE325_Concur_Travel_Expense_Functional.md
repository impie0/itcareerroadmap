## About

The **C_CE325** certification validates that a consultant possesses intermediate to advanced knowledge of SAP Concur Expense Professional Edition configuration, implementation, and administration. Launched in 2023 as a replacement for the Advanced Configuration Training (ACT) program, this exam emphasizes hands-on configuration, policy setup, approval workflows, reporting customization, and integration with SAP S/4HANA Finance modules. It is positioned as a core credential for Concur Functional Consultants implementing travel and expense solutions in mid-market and enterprise environments, particularly where organizations require complex approval hierarchies, cost-allocation rules, and compliance reporting.

---

## Domain context — Travel/Expense Management

Travel and Expense (T&E) management is a critical operational function in organizations with distributed workforces, multi-location operations, or frequent customer/supplier engagement. SAP Concur dominates the enterprise T&E market globally, serving as the primary spend-management platform for policy enforcement, approval workflows, audit trails, and reimbursement. Integration with SAP S/4HANA Finance (GL posting, cost center allocation, tax recovery) and external booking engines (hotel, airfare, rental) is standard in large deployments.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint weights the following domains equally (~12–15% each):

- **Concur Expense Module Configuration** — policy setup, expense types, approval workflows, reimbursement rules, advance/cash advance handling
- **Concur Travel Module Setup** — booking engine integration, traveler profiles, hotel/airfare rate contracts, travel policy enforcement, approval routing
- **User & Role Management** — Concur user lifecycle, role assignment, delegation rules, manager hierarchy setup, access control
- **Approval Workflows & Expense Report Routing** — multi-level approval chains, exception handling, report status lifecycle, delegation of authority
- **Cost Allocation & Coding** — cost center assignment, profit center allocation, project billing, customer codes, expense-to-GL mapping
- **Reporting & Analytics** — dashboard configuration, audit reports, spend analysis, custom report builder, data export to external systems
- **Integration with Finance Systems** — SAP S/4HANA GL posting, AP integration, tax recovery automation, journal entry creation
- **Invoice Module & Supplier Integration** — supplier invoice capture, three-way matching, approval workflows (if applicable to organization)
- **Regulatory & Compliance** — audit trail requirements, GDPR/local privacy, receipt retention, corporate policy enforcement
- **System Administration & Troubleshooting** — data governance, master data maintenance, log analysis, performance optimization
