## About

The SAP Certified Application Associate — Business Process Integration with SAP S/4HANA (C_TS410) validates foundational knowledge of end-to-end business process design, integration, and configuration across SAP S/4HANA core modules. This certification was the entry-level credential for aspiring SAP functional consultants and business analysts from 2018 until January 1, 2025, when SAP retired the exam in favor of the cloud-native successor C_IEE2E. The C_TS410 remains valuable for organizations still running on-premise S/4HANA 1909 and 2020 releases.

---

## Retirement notice

**This certification has been retired effective January 1, 2025.** SAP no longer administers C_TS410 exams or publishes new course materials. The successor is [C_IEE2E (SAP Certified Associate — Implementation Consultant)](C_IEE2E_End_to_End_Business_Processes.md) — aligned to S/4HANA Cloud.

Professionals already holding C_TS410 credentials remain certified; renewal is by retaking the higher-level module-specific certifications (C_TS450, C_TS460, etc.) or transitioning to C_IEE2E.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint spans integrated business processes across SAP S/4HANA and is weighted as follows:

- **Financial Accounting (FI)** – 8–12% : General ledger, balance sheet, P&L, currency conversion, bank reconciliation
- **Management Accounting / Cost Control (CO)** – 8–12% : Cost centers, profit centers, cost object accounting, variance analysis
- **Human Capital Management (HCM)** – 8–12% : Employee master data, payroll integration, organizational management, talent development
- **Procurement (MM/Sourcing)** – 12–16% : Purchase-to-pay (P2P) cycles, vendor master, requisition, goods receipt, invoice matching
- **Inventory Management (MM)** – 8–12% : Material master, stock levels, goods movement, warehouse integration
- **Manufacturing (PP)** – 8–12% : Production planning, demand forecasting, work orders, bill of materials (BOM), routing
- **Order Fulfillment / Sales (SD)** – 12–16% : Lead-to-cash (L2C) cycle, customer master, quotation, sales order, delivery, billing
- **Customer Service (CS)** – 8–12% : Service orders, technician assignment, parts consumption, billing
- **Project Management (PS)** – 4–8% : Project master data, costing, resource planning, actuals tracking
- **Plant Maintenance (PM)** – 8–12% : Preventive and corrective maintenance, work orders, spare parts, technician scheduling
- **Warehouse Management (WM)** – 4–8% : Inbound / outbound processes, bin assignment, wave picking
- **Processes & Integration** – 4–8% : Inter-module workflows, master data synchronization, document flow, business partner roles

*Source:* [SAP Learning Center — Exam Objectives](https://learning.sap.com/certifications/c-ts410-2504-sap-certified-application-associate-business-process-integration-with-sap-s-4hana)

## Skills validated

*Specific technologies and workflows this exam tests, distinct from shared ERP competencies above.*

- SAP S/4HANA core transaction codes (VF01, FB01, ME51, etc.)
- Fiori UX and design principles (new UI paradigm for S/4HANA)
- Business process configuration within SAP Customizing (IMG)
- Master data creation and maintenance (MM02, BP, etc.)
- Document flow and inter-module linkages (e.g., PO → GR → IV)
- SAP Analytics Cloud (AC) integration for KPI dashboards
- SAP Intelligent Robotic Process Automation (iRPA) process integration concepts
- System testing methodology (unit, integration, user acceptance)
- Authorization and security role design (PFCG)
- Basic ABAP reports and smart forms (report reading, not coding)
