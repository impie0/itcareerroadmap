## About

SAP Ariba Sourcing is a cloud-based procurement solution that enables organizations to manage strategic sourcing events, supplier collaboration, and competitive procurement processes. This certification validates your ability to implement and configure the SAP Ariba Sourcing platform for conducting auctions, negotiations, and complex sourcing events.

The Ariba Sourcing certification demonstrates expertise in:
- Sourcing event setup and management
- Auction and RFQ configuration
- Supplier management and collaboration
- Sourcing project administration
- Ariba marketplace integration
- Sourcing analytics and performance tracking
- System integration with procurement systems

This credential is essential for organizations implementing SAP Ariba Sourcing to optimize procurement processes and achieve cost savings through competitive sourcing.

## Domain context

SAP Ariba is SAP's Business Network platform for spend management, connecting buyers and suppliers globally. Ariba Sourcing is the module focused on enabling organizations to run efficient, transparent sourcing processes.

Companies using Ariba Sourcing typically:
- Have significant procurement volumes
- Need strategic sourcing capabilities
- Conduct frequent RFQs, RFPs, and auctions
- Require supplier collaboration features
- Integrate sourcing with financial systems
- Need multi-language and multi-currency support
- Track spending and supplier performance
