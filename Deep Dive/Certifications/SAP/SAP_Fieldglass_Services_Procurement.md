## About

The C_TFG61_2405 certification validates foundational and core knowledge in configuring, administering, reporting, and integrating SAP Fieldglass for services procurement workflows. This Associate-level credential is intended for functional consultants, system administrators, and business analysts implementing or supporting services procurement operations within SAP Fieldglass. The exam was updated to version 2405 in 2024; the earlier C_TFG61_2211 is no longer current. As of May 2026, this is an active SAP certification with strong market demand driven by enterprise adoption of SAP Fieldglass as a strategic vendor and contingent workforce management hub.

---

## Domain context — Services Procurement

SAP Fieldglass is a cloud-based vendor management system and contingent workforce platform, now fully integrated with SAP Spend Management (formerly Ariba Network). Services Procurement refers to the SOW (Statement of Work) and external services ordering, invoicing, and governance workflows within Fieldglass.

## Topics covered

Exam objectives are organized into six major domains:

- **Users & Delegation** – User creation, roles, access control, supervisor and delegate configurations, user troubleshooting
- **Company Structure & Organization** – Legal entities, cost centers, organizational units, business calendars, financial data setup
- **Supplier & Buyer–Supplier Relationship Management** – Supplier creation, onboarding workflows, buyer–supplier interactions, communication templates
- **Services Procurement Transactions** – Statement of Work (SOW) characteristics, statuses, invoice matching, SOW bid management, service-delivery tracking
- **Configuration & System Tools** – Contextual help, reason codes, message templates, email notification configuration, system parameter setup
- **Analytics & Integrations** – Report configuration, report audit trails, advanced analytics navigation, API integrations, data exports

## Skills validated

*Concrete technologies and workflows this exam tests.*

- SAP Fieldglass Services Procurement user interface and navigation
- Statement of Work (SOW) lifecycle and status management
- Supplier master data creation and validation
- Buyer–supplier transaction workflows (RFx, ordering, invoicing)
- Services module configuration (rate structures, invoice rules, SOW templates)
- User roles, access control, and delegation setup
- Reporting framework and custom report creation in Fieldglass
- Integration with SAP S/4HANA and Ariba Network (procurement orchestration)
- Email templates, notification workflows, and system messaging
- Compliance and audit trail maintenance in procurement transactions
