## About

The **C_TS460** certification validates that a consultant possesses foundational knowledge and proven practical skills in SAP S/4HANA Sales & Distribution (Sales Order Management), covering both on-premise and Cloud Private Edition deployments. Launched in 2023 to align with the S/4HANA roadmap, this exam emphasizes scenario-based decision-making, end-to-end sales transaction flows (from quotation through billing), master data governance, and integration with finance and supply-chain modules. It is positioned as an entry-level qualification for sales/supply-chain consultants implementing projects under experienced mentorship.

---

## Domain context — ERP/Sales

Enterprise resource planning systems orchestrate sales order processing, fulfillment, and revenue recognition across the organization. The Sales & Distribution (SD) module in SAP handles customer master data, quotations, sales orders, delivery, shipping, and billing — it is mission-critical in any distribution, manufacturing, or service delivery business. S/4HANA represents the current standard for new projects; predecessor ECC systems are in long-term support but declining in adoption.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint emphasizes the following domains (equal weights, ~10–15% each):

- **Sales Master Data** — Customer master (billing/shipping/sales areas), material master (sales attributes, pricing indicators), pricing & condition records, sales documents master data setup
- **Sales Transactions & Document Flow** — Quotation creation and management, sales order entry and processing, contract/outline agreement setup, returns and cancellations, document flow logic and cross-document references
- **Sales Pricing & Conditions** — Condition tables and scales (volume, customer, material), discount and surcharge logic, rebate agreements, pricing procedure determination, tax calculation
- **Output Determination & Communication** — Form printing (invoices, delivery notes, order confirmations), EDI/email output, output determination logic and condition records
- **Outline Agreements & Long-Term Contracts** — Blanket purchase order processing, framework agreements, contract release workflow, milestone billing, collective invoices
- **Quotations & Sales Order Management** — Quotation creation from opportunities, sales order creation (standard, rush, blanket-order releases), order blocking and release procedures, availability checking (ATP)
- **Delivery & Shipping** — Delivery document creation, picking and packing, shipping point determination, shipment consolidation, goods-issue posting
- **Billing & Invoicing** — Invoice document type setup, billing plan maintenance, milestone billing, partial invoicing, cancellation and reversal of invoices
- **Returns Processing** — Return order entry, credit memo creation, return goods receipt, refund and replacement logic
- **Cross-Functional Integration** — FI posting (revenue recognition, GL accounts, tax codes), MM integration (stock reservations, issue-to-stock), PP integration (make-to-order scenarios), material traceability

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests.*

- Sales master data setup: customer master (sales areas, billing/shipping addresses), material master (sales data), pricing records
- Sales order creation, processing, and fulfillment workflows (quotation → order → delivery → invoice)
- Pricing logic: condition tables, scales, discounts, surcharges, rebates, tax determination
- Outline agreements (blanket orders, framework contracts) and release procedures
- Delivery and shipping processes: delivery document creation, picking, goods-issue
- Billing and invoicing: invoice document types, billing plans, partial invoicing, cancellations
- Returns and credit memo processing
- Output determination: form printing, EDI, email notifications
- Integration with Finance (GL posting, revenue recognition), Materials Management (reservations, GR), Production Planning (make-to-order)
- Availability-to-promise (ATP) checking and backorder handling
- Period-end sales analysis and month-end reconciliation (revenue, open orders, aging)
