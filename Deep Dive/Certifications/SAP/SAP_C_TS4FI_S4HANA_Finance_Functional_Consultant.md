## About

The **C_TS4FI** certification verifies that a consultant possesses fundamental knowledge and proven skills in SAP S/4HANA Financial Accounting, covering both on-premise and Cloud Private Edition deployments. Launched in 2023, this exam replaced earlier Financial Accounting certifications and emphasizes scenario-based questions, system behavior, configuration logic, and practical decision-making over isolated theory. It is positioned as an entry-level qualification for finance consultants to implement knowledge in projects under experienced mentorship.

---

## Domain context — ERP/Finance

Enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems orchestrate financial, supply-chain, and operational data across an organization. Finance modules within ERP (GL, AP/AR, asset accounting, closing) are mission-critical; SAP dominates mid-market and enterprise deployments globally. The S/4HANA generation (cloud and on-premise) represents the current standard; predecessor ECC systems are in long-term support but declining in new projects.

## Topics covered

The exam blueprint weights the following domains equally (~11–20% each):

- **General Ledger Accounting** — Universal Journal, posting logic, period-end reconciliation, currency revaluation, consolidated reporting
- **Accounts Payable & Accounts Receivable** — vendor/customer master data, invoice processing, payment terms, dunning, aging analysis
- **Asset Accounting** — fixed asset master data, depreciation methods, retirement, transfers, impairment
- **Financial Closing** — accrual reversal, intercompany reconciliation, variance analysis, legal consolidation, audit trail
- **Bank Accounting** — bank master data, payment processing, bank reconciliation, cash management integration
- **Organizational & Master Data Setup** — company code, plant, cost center, profit center structures; GL/AP/AR chart of accounts design

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what this exam actually tests.*

- General Ledger posting logic and month-end reconciliation in S/4HANA
- Accounts Payable / Accounts Receivable invoice-to-payment and collections workflows
- Fixed Asset Management (FA module) — capitalization, depreciation, retirement
- Financial Closing processes — accruals, reversals, intercompany reconciliation
- Bank Accounting — bank master data, payment matching, reconciliation
- Organizational structure design — company code, plant, cost center, profit center hierarchies
- Master data governance — GL chart of accounts, vendor/customer master, business partner setup
- Universal Journal query and reporting in S/4HANA (consolidated view of all financial postings)
- Period-end control procedures and audit trail verification
- Scenario analysis and practical decision-making in finance configuration contexts
