## About

The **C_C4H47_2503** certifies that a candidate possesses fundamental and core knowledge required of an SAP Sales Cloud Implementation Consultant and can participate as a contributing team member in implementation and optimization projects. Released December 2023, it replaced the earlier C_C4H41_2405 version. The certification validates hands-on expertise in configuring Sales Cloud core modules, designing lead-to-cash flows, managing master data (accounts, contacts, products, pricing), integrating with S/4HANA, and deploying mobile capabilities for field sales. Ideal for ERP consultants transitioning into cloud CRM or CX roles.

## Topics covered

- **Introduction to SAP Sales Cloud Version 2** (10%): Core capabilities, release history, deployment options, integration scenarios, and positioning within SAP ecosystem.
- **General, Company, Users, and Control Settings** (11–20%): System setup, company structure, user/employee creation, role-based authorization, control parameters, organizational hierarchy.
- **Master Data** (10%): Account and contact management, individual customer profiles, product master, registered products, pricing and product hierarchies.
- **Lead Management** (10%): Lead creation, qualification, conversion to opportunity, automated workflows, lead routing, lead scoring via AI.
- **Opportunity Management** (15%): Opportunity creation and tracking, sales cycle stages, deal probability, engagement, activity integration, opportunity metrics and forecasting.
- **Activity Management** (10%): Activity types (calls, emails, tasks, meetings), activity planning, visit planning, sales rep calendar, mobile activity capture.
- **Quoting and Order Management** (12%): Quote creation, quote approval workflows, order generation, order tracking, fulfillment integration, invoice and payment sync.
- **Sales Forecasting and Analytics** (8%): Forecast hierarchies, pipeline analytics, sales metrics dashboards, territory management, quota setting, performance reporting.
- **Integration with S/4HANA and Other Systems** (8%): Standard integrations (accounts, contacts, orders, pricing sync), middleware configuration, data replication, master data governance.
- **Mobile Sales** (4%): Mobile app deployment, offline capabilities, field data capture, push notifications, geolocation features.
- **Pricing and Discounting** (4%): Pricing rules, discount matrices, promotion management, advanced pricing scenarios, price override workflows.
- **Machine Learning and Generative AI** (2%): AI-driven lead scoring, next-best-action recommendations, sales predictions, chat-assisted selling via CoPilot.
