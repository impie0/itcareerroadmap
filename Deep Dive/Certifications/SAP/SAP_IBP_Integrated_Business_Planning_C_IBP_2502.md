## About

The SAP Certified Associate – SAP Integrated Business Planning (C_IBP_2502) validates foundational knowledge in SAP's integrated business planning platform. This certification is designed for supply chain professionals, SAP consultants, and IT analysts who implement and support SAP IBP for demand planning, inventory optimization, supply chain control, and sales & operations planning (S&OP). SAP IBP is a cloud-native SaaS solution within the SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) and represents a strategic shift from on-premise APO (Advanced Planning and Optimization) toward integrated, real-time planning across the entire supply chain.

---

## Domain context — Supply Chain Management & Planning

SAP Integrated Business Planning is a core module within the **Supply Chain Management (SCM)** domain, addressing end-to-end demand-to-supply orchestration. SCM encompasses network planning, procurement, manufacturing operations, logistics, and inventory optimization; SAP IBP specifically focuses on collaborative, data-driven planning across these functions.

## Topics covered

The C_IBP_2502 exam assesses competency across six primary functional areas:

- **SAP IBP for Demand Planning** — demand forecasting, statistical methods, machine learning integration, demand sensing from market signals, and scenario planning
- **SAP IBP Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP)** — cross-functional planning alignment, monthly consensus-building, demand-supply balancing, financial constraint integration
- **SAP IBP for Supply Planning** — supply-source assignment, manufacturing capacity allocation, procurement timeline integration, fulfillment strategy design
- **SAP IBP for Inventory Optimization** — inventory policy configuration, buffer setting, stock level balancing, safety stock and reorder point logic
- **SAP IBP for Demand-Driven Replenishment (DDR)** — DDMRP methodology, decoupling points, material flow buffers, replenishment signaling
- **SAP IBP Analytics & Reporting** — built-in planning analytics, dashboard design, KPI monitoring, exception management, drill-down analysis
- **Integration with SAP Supply Chain Control Tower** — end-to-end supply chain visibility, event management, real-time monitoring
- **System Configuration & Administration** — user roles, data quality governance, system setup, approval workflows, audit trails

## Skills validated

- SAP IBP platform architecture (cloud SaaS, Fiori UI, data model)
- Demand forecasting with statistical models and ML-driven pattern recognition
- Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP) process design and execution
- Inventory optimization algorithms (safety stock, reorder points, service level)
- Supply chain network design and fulfillment strategy
- Demand-Driven MRP (DDMRP) methodology and buffer management
- SAP IBP Analytics and reporting suite

- Multi-scenario planning and what-if analysis
