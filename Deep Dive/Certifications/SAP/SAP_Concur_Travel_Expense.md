## About

The SAP Concur Certified Associate – Expense Administrator credential validates entry-to-intermediate knowledge of SAP Concur Expense Professional Edition configuration and day-to-day administration. Released in January 2024 as part of SAP's modernized digital certification program, this credential replaces the legacy Advanced Configuration Training (ACT) and offers a more flexible, self-paced path to demonstrating administrative competency. Candidates who pass the exam gain digital badges and can request unrestricted administrative access to their organization's Concur Expense deployment. This cert is suitable for finance ops professionals, T&E coordinators, and aspiring Concur Expense admins entering the role.

---

## Domain context — Enterprise Finance & Travel Management

Concur Expense is SAP's flagship spend-management solution for processing, auditing, and controlling employee travel and out-of-pocket expenses at scale. The T&E (Travel & Expense) function spans financial controls, compliance, policy enforcement, and integration with ERP systems (SAP S/4HANA Finance, Oracle, etc.). Admin-level knowledge requires hands-on facility with system configuration, user provisioning, reporting, and integration workflows.

## Topics covered

Official exam blueprint is not publicly released by SAP; however, community sources and exam prep materials indicate coverage of:

- **Configuration & setup** — User provisioning, expense types, policy rules, approval workflows, custom fields
- **Expense processing** — Report creation, submission, approval routing, audit trails, expense classifications
- **Travel integration** — Concur Travel to Concur Expense handoff, booking policies, pre-expense vs. post-expense flows
- **Reporting & analytics** — Standard and custom reports, data export, compliance dashboards
- **Compliance & controls** — Policy enforcement, duplicate detection, audit rules, regulatory requirements
- **Integration** — S/4HANA Finance, Oracle Financials, general ledger mapping, bank feeds
- **Mobile & user experience** — Mobile app capabilities, self-service features, end-user support
- **Invoice & intercompany** — Concur Invoice basics (if bundled in associate curriculum)
