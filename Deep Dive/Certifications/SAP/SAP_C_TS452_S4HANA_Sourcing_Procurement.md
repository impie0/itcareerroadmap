## About

The **C_TS452** certification validates competency in SAP S/4HANA Materials Management (Procurement/Sourcing) covering the complete Procure-to-Pay cycle: vendor and material master data, purchase requisitions, purchase orders, outline agreements, goods receipt, invoice verification, and integration with finance and inventory. Launched in 2021 and actively maintained through 2024, it is positioned as the entry-level SAP procurement credential for functional consultants and power users implementing S/4HANA projects. Open to all experience levels; prized in manufacturing, distribution, pharmaceuticals, and utilities sectors.

---

## Domain context — ERP/Procurement

Enterprise procurement orchestrates sourcing strategy, vendor relationships, purchase requisitions, purchase orders, invoice verification, and spend analytics across the organization. The Materials Management (MM) module in SAP handles procurement master data, purchase requisitions, purchase orders, outline agreements, goods receipt, inventory movement, invoice verification, and reporting — it is mission-critical in manufacturing, distribution, retail, and service delivery organizations. S/4HANA represents the current standard for new projects; predecessor ECC systems are in long-term support but declining in adoption.

## Topics covered

Equal weighting across 10 domains (~10% each):

- **Enterprise Structure & Clean Core** — Org hierarchy, purchasing org configuration, master data governance
- **Procurement Master Data** — Vendor/material master setup, source lists, contract structures
- **Purchase Requisitions** — Manual and MRP-driven creation, approval workflows, prioritization
- **Purchase Orders** — PO types/item categories, creation from requisition/quotation, release procedures, monitoring
- **Outline Agreements** — Scheduling agreements, blanket orders, contract releases, target/actual analysis
- **Vendor Management & Sourcing** — Vendor selection, RfQ management, vendor rating, performance metrics
- **Goods Receipt & Inventory** — GR posting, movement types, stock management, returns
- **Invoice Verification** — Three-way matching (PO–GR–Invoice), variance analysis, blocked invoices
- **Finance Integration** — GL posting, GR/IR accounts, AP accrual, integration with FI/SD
- **Procurement Reporting** — PO aging, vendor dashboards, spend analysis, month-end accruals

## Skills validated

Cert-specific capabilities tested:

- Vendor/material master setup (classification, purchasing org, payment terms, incoterms, ordering quantities)
- Purchase requisition creation and workflows (manual, MRP-driven, approval chains)
- Purchase order types and item categories; creation from requisition/quotation/outline agreement
- Outline agreements: scheduling agreements, blanket orders, release procedures
- Purchasing conditions: pricing, discounts, surcharges, tax determination
- Goods receipt posting, movement types, stock management (unrestricted/inspection/blocked)
- Three-way matching (PO–GR–Invoice), variance analysis, blocked invoice resolution
- Finance integration: GL posting, GR/IR accounts, AP accrual
- Materials Management integration: inventory, reservations, returns
- Vendor performance analysis, PO aging, spend analysis, month-end reconciliation
- S/4HANA-specific: simplified data models, embedded analytics, clean-core governance
