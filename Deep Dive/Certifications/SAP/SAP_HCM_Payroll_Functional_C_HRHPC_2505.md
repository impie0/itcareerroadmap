## About

The SAP Certified Associate – SAP SuccessFactors for Employee Central Payroll (C_HRHPC) certification validates core competency in implementing and configuring SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll, the cloud-native payroll solution integrated with SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central. Candidates demonstrate understanding of payroll run architecture, wage-type configuration, tax and garnishment rules, and integration with HR and financial systems. This associate-level credential is recommended for consultants, functional analysts, and payroll administrators joining SAP implementation projects in a mentored role. The C_HRHPC_2505 version released in 2025 reflects the current feature set and configuration approach for Employee Central Payroll in the SAP SuccessFactors cloud ecosystem.

---

## Domain context — HR/Payroll

Configuration and operational management of enterprise payroll systems, including wage processing, tax compliance, garnishment handling, period-end closing, and country-specific regulatory requirements. Closely aligned with Human Capital Management (HCM) platforms, particularly cloud-native solutions that integrate employee data, time-off, benefits, and compensation into unified payroll workflows.

---

## Topics covered

- **Payroll Processing** — Payroll run execution, data validation, result distribution, error handling, and process monitoring using Payroll Control Center
- **Payroll Control Center** — Configuration and use of the centralized control interface for payroll administration, including process definition, scheduling, and result review
- **Wage Types & Schemas** — Definition and configuration of wage types, earnings, deductions, and payroll schema structures
- **Period Schemas & Run Calendars** — Period-based payroll calendar setup, multiple-payroll-period handling, and schema assignment
- **Tax & Garnishment Configuration** — Income tax, payroll tax rules, court-ordered garnishments, and tax-country-specific compliance settings
- **Off-Cycle Payroll** — Ad-hoc payroll runs, merit increases, bonus payments, and retroactive adjustments outside regular cycles
- **Year-End Processing** — Final-pay processing, tax reconciliation, and annual compliance closeout (W-2, 1099, etc.)
- **Country-Specific Configurations** — Region-based payroll rules, statutory deductions, and local regulatory compliance (US, UK, EU, APAC, etc.)
- **Integration & Data Replication** — Employee Central data sync with Employee Central Payroll, time-off provisioning, and downstream integration with SAP Financials (CO/FI modules)
- **Authorizations & Security** — Role-based access control (RBAC), user permissions, and data protection in Payroll Control Center

## Skills validated

- **Payroll Control Center** — End-to-end setup, configuration, and operational use for payroll batch processing
- **Wage Type Architecture** — Design and implementation of custom wage types, rule logic, and payroll schema assignment
- **Tax & Statutory Compliance** — Country-specific tax tables, garnishment workflows, and audit-trail management
- **Employee Central Integration** — Data replication mapping, infotype synchronization, and bidirectional sync validation
- **Off-Cycle Payroll Execution** — Ad-hoc runs, bonus/merit processing, retroactive adjustments, and reversal procedures
- **Period Schema Management** — Multi-frequency payroll setup, period-calendar mapping, and run-date scheduling
- **Payroll Error Handling** — Diagnosis of calculation discrepancies, log analysis, and correction workflows
- **Year-End & Regulatory Closeout** — Final-pay processing, tax reconciliation, and statutory reporting (W-2/1098 generation, EU reporting, etc.)
- **Role-Based Security** — Payroll Control Center authorization configuration and compliance documentation
- **Reporting & Analytics** — Payroll result extraction, ad-hoc reporting, and integration with SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC)
