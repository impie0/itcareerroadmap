## About

The SAP Certified Application Associate — SAP S/4HANA Manufacturing/Production Planning (C_TS422) certifies professionals who design, configure, and operate SAP production planning and manufacturing modules in S/4HANA environments. Launched in 2019 as S/4HANA adoption accelerated across Global 2000 manufacturers, it validates expertise in demand management, MRP execution, production order workflows, capacity planning, shop floor control, and quality integration. The exam reflects S/4HANA-specific process simplifications (e.g., embedded analytics, single-instance architecture, streamlined transaction sets). Active status as of May 2026; no announced retirement.

## Topics covered

*Derived from official SAP C_TS422 exam blueprint and training curriculum.*

- **Master Data & Data Structures (15%)** — Products, BOMs, routings, planning parameters, work centers, production versions, forecast profiles
- **Demand Management & Forecasting (12%)** — Sales forecasts, customer demand, statistical forecasting, forecast consumption, demand sensing
- **Material Requirements Planning (MRP) (20%)** — MRP run execution, lot sizing, planned orders, safety stock, lead time management, exception messages
- **Production Order Management (20%)** — Order creation, scheduling, component picking, confirmations, backflushing, co-products and by-products, order settlement
- **Capacity Planning & Scheduling (15%)** — Capacity requirements planning (CRP), load leveling, bottleneck analysis, finite scheduling, resource constraints
- **Shop Floor Control (SFC) & Execution (12%)** — Time tracking, labor/machine confirmation, goods receipt, quality inspections, rework, scrap handling
- **S/4HANA Production Simplifications & Integration (6%)** — Embedded analytics, simplified transactions, integration with GR/IR, quality management, procurement integration

## Skills validated

*Cert-specific — what the C_TS422 exam actually tests.*

- **MRP execution & optimization** — running MRP transactions (MD01, MD02), interpreting exception messages, managing planned order parameters
- **Production order lifecycle** — creation (CO01), release, component allocation, backflushing, confirmations (COFM), goods receipt, order settlement
- **Demand-driven planning** — forecasts, ATP (Available-to-Promise), demand consumption rules, independent vs. dependent demand
- **Bill of Materials (BOM)** — structure creation, alternative BOMs, phantom assemblies, co-products, by-products, engineering change orders
- **Routing & Work Center management** — operation definition, lead-time calculation, capacity constraints, resource planning
- **Capacity planning** — CRP (Capacity Requirements Planning) execution, load analysis, bottleneck resolution, finite scheduling
- **Shop floor control transactions** — time tracking (CRTX), labor/machine confirmations (COFM), quality inspections (QM01/QM02), goods movements
- **Quality integration in production** — in-process quality checks, inspection lots, rework vs. scrap decisions, quality records
- **S/4HANA production simplifications** — embedded planning analytics, new simplified transaction set, tight GL/CO integration
- **Integration with procurement & supply chain** — MRP → PO linkage, vendor evaluation, GR/IR (Goods Receipt/Invoice Receipt), planning in a multi-site environment
