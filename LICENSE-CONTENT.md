## Why this license?

The `Deep Dive/` corpus represents months of research effort — verifying cert codes against vendor pages, sourcing salary figures from Robert Half / Glassdoor / BLS / Heidrick & Struggles / IAPP, citing every fact, cross-referencing across vendors. Open content licenses with **no commercial restriction** would let third parties scrape it and resell it as a paid certification guide, which would defeat the public-good intent of the project.

CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 keeps it open for:

- Personal study and career planning
- Educational and non-profit use
- Blog posts and articles that link back
- Forks that build new free tools on top of the data

While preventing:

- Commercial republication / paid courses based on this data
- Closed-source forks that strip the citations
- Third-party packaging into a paid product

If you want to use the content commercially, please open an issue on the repo to discuss licensing.
