## How to Use This Guide

### Discovery Strategy
1. **Subscribe to 3-5 key blogs** in your primary domain (e.g., backend, frontend, security)
2. **Follow aggregators** like SRE Weekly, The New Stack, or Pointer for discovery
3. **Explore citations** from papers—most research blogs link to foundational work
4. **Join communities** around blogs (many have Discord, Slack, or Twitter followings)

### Quality Signals to Look For
- **Regular, substantive posts** (not just promotional content)
- **Technical depth** with code examples or case studies
- **Learning resources** (interactive guides, visualizations, explainers)
- **Original research or insights** not rehashed elsewhere
- **Actionable advice** you can apply to your own work
