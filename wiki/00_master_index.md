# IT Career Roadmap — Deep Resources Index

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This folder contains the deep-research companion to the platform pages. Read **[PLATFORM_README.md](PLATFORM_README.md)** first for the full overview of the 4 HTML pages and how they fit together. Read **[BUILD_LOG.md](BUILD_LOG.md)** for the chronological build history.

Thirteen thematic `.md` files cover the things the matrix can't fit on a grid: missing domains, niche careers, free university courses, government standards, hundreds of books, blogs, podcasts, conferences, YouTube channels, and more. They informed the design of the roadmap; they don't appear on the page.

---

## How to use these documents

Read the **[01 Gap Audit](01_gap_audit_and_missing_domains.md)** first if you want the high-level story of what's missing from the standard 10-domain roadmap. Then dive into whichever specific file matches your career stage or pivot.

Every link in every file points to a real publisher, vendor, or author page that was verified during research. If a link breaks, it's worth checking whether the resource was archived rather than removed — most of these resources are stable.
